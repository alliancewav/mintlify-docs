BeatPass tracks various metrics to help you understand performance and optimize earnings. This guide defines each metric and explains how it's calculated.

## Engagement Metrics

### Plays

**Definition:** The total number of times your tracks have been streamed.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Total Plays" icon="play">
    All streaming events, regardless of user or duration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unique Plays" icon="user">
    Distinct users who played your tracks. One per user per track per day.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Repeat Plays" icon="rotate">
    Same user playing again. Indicates sticky, replayable content.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Completions

**Definition:** Plays that reach at least 80% of the track duration.

- Completions indicate genuine interest
- Weighted higher in contribution calculations
- High completion rate signals quality

### Completion Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of plays that become completions.

```
Completion Rate = (Completions / Total Plays) × 100
```

<Steps>
  <Step title="Excellent (70%+)">
    Outstanding engagement. Your tracks hold attention exceptionally well.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Good (50-70%)">
    Above average. Most tracks fall into this healthy range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Average (30-50%)">
    Room for improvement. Consider track length, intro hooks, or genre fit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Low (Below 30%)">
    Listeners are skipping early. Review audio quality, misleading tags, or overly long intros.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Saves

**Definition:** Number of users who added your track to their library.

- Indicates intent to return
- Stronger engagement signal than plays
- Contributes to discovery algorithms

### Save Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of listeners who saved the track.

```
Save Rate = (Saves / Unique Plays) × 100
```

## Performance Metrics

### Contribution Value

**Definition:** Your calculated share of the platform revenue pool.

- Based on engagement metrics
- Relative to total platform engagement
- Updates hourly

<Card title="Contribution Formula" icon="calculator" href="/contribution/formula">
  Learn how contribution value is calculated.
</Card>

### Estimated Earnings

**Definition:** Projected payout based on current contribution value.

- Estimate only, not guaranteed
- Final amount determined at month end
- Depends on total pool and platform engagement

### Quality Score

**Definition:** Composite score based on metadata completeness and audio quality.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Positive Factors" icon="circle-check">
    - Complete metadata (title, BPM, key, genre)
    - High audio bitrate (320kbps+ or lossless)
    - Cover art uploaded
    - Accurate tags and description
  </Card>
  <Card title="Negative Factors" icon="circle-xmark">
    - Missing BPM or key information
    - Low audio bitrate (under 256kbps)
    - No cover art
    - Sparse or missing tags
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  A high Quality Score improves your tracks' visibility in search results and recommendations. Always fill out complete metadata.
</Tip>

## Audience Metrics

### Listener Demographics

Understand your audience composition:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Geographic" icon="globe">
    See which countries and regions your listeners come from. Useful for targeting marketing efforts and understanding your fanbase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Platform" icon="laptop-mobile">
    Breakdown of web vs mobile listeners. Optimize your presence where your audience is.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Source" icon="magnifying-glass">
    How listeners discovered your tracks: search, playlists, recommendations, direct links, or external sources.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Follower Count

**Definition:** Users who follow your producer profile.

- Followers see your new uploads in their feed
- Indicates audience building progress
- Quality of followers matters more than quantity

### Follower Growth

**Definition:** Net change in followers over time.

```
Growth Rate = (New Followers - Lost Followers) / Previous Total × 100
```

## Track-Level Metrics

### Per-Track Analytics

Each track in your catalog shows detailed performance data:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Engagement Stats" icon="chart-simple">
    - **Total Plays**: Lifetime stream count
    - **Completions**: Full listens (80%+)
    - **Saves**: Library additions
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance Trends" icon="chart-line">
    - **Peak Day**: Highest single-day plays
    - **Trend**: Rising ↑, Stable →, or Declining ↓
    - **Velocity**: Rate of change vs previous period
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Track Performance Score

**Definition:** Calculated score combining multiple signals.

```
Performance = (Plays × Weight) + (Completions × Weight) + (Saves × Weight)
```

Exact weights are not disclosed to prevent gaming.
