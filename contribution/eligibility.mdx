## Overview

To receive payouts from BeatPass, producers must meet certain eligibility requirements. These ensure compliance, prevent fraud, and maintain platform quality.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Producers
</Info>

---

## Basic Requirements

### Account Requirements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verified Email">
    Your email address must be verified.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Producer Status">
    You must have approved producer/backstage access.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Basic profile information must be filled out.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Accepted Terms">
    You must accept the Producer Agreement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Payment Setup

### Stripe Connect

Payouts are processed through Stripe Connect:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Stripe Account">
    Link or create a Stripe account from Backstage > Finances.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Provide identity verification as required by Stripe.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Payout Method">
    Connect a bank account or debit card for receiving funds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Verification Requirements

Stripe may require:

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Government ID | Identity verification |
| Address proof | Location verification |
| Tax information | Regulatory compliance |

<Note>
  Verification requirements vary by country. Stripe guides you through the process.
</Note>

## Minimum Thresholds

### Payout Minimum

You must accumulate a minimum balance before payouts are processed:

| Region | Minimum |
|--------|---------|
| US/Canada | $10 USD |
| International | Varies |

<Tip>
  If you don't reach the minimum in a month, your balance carries over to the next period.
</Tip>

### Reaching the Threshold

Strategies to reach payout thresholds:

- Upload consistently to build engagement
- Focus on quality for better contribution rates
- Build your audience for sustained plays
- Complete all metadata for visibility

## Ineligibility Reasons

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Incomplete Stripe Setup">
    Verify your Stripe Connect account is fully set up and verified.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Below Minimum Balance">
    Your contribution value hasn't reached the payout threshold.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Account Restrictions">
    Your account may have restrictions due to policy violations.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Verification Pending">
    Stripe verification may be incomplete or under review.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### How to Check Status

1. Go to **Backstage > Finances**
2. Check your payout status indicator
3. Review any pending requirements
4. Complete any outstanding verification

## Maintaining Eligibility

### Good Standing

To maintain payout eligibility:

- Follow platform community guidelines
- Don't engage in fraudulent activity
- Keep payment information current
- Respond to verification requests promptly

### Account Restrictions

Accounts may lose eligibility for:

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Artificial play inflation | Earnings forfeit, possible ban |
| Copyright infringement | Track removal, account review |
| Terms of service violation | Account restriction |
| Fraud or abuse | Permanent ban |

<Warning>
  Attempting to game the contribution system will result in loss of earnings and potential account termination.
</Warning>

## Tax Considerations

### Your Responsibility

- Report all earnings to tax authorities
- Maintain records of payouts received
- Consult a tax professional for your jurisdiction

### Platform Reporting

BeatPass may:

- Issue tax forms based on earnings thresholds
- Report to tax authorities as required by law
- Request tax identification information
