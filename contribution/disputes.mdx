## Overview

BeatPass provides processes for resolving disputes related to earnings, copyright, and user interactions. This guide explains how to raise and resolve issues.

<Info>
  **Audience:** All users
</Info>

---

## Types of Disputes

### Earnings Disputes

Issues related to:

- Missing or incorrect contribution calculations
- Payout processing problems
- License payment discrepancies
- Revenue split disagreements

### Content Disputes

Issues related to:

- Copyright infringement claims
- Duplicate content reports
- Unauthorized uploads
- Credit attribution

### User Disputes

Issues related to:

- Transaction disagreements
- Communication problems
- Service delivery issues
- Policy violations

## Earnings Disputes

### Before Filing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Dashboard">
    Review analytics in Backstage \> Insights for detailed breakdowns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand Timing">
    Contribution values update every 6 hours; payouts process monthly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Calculations">
    Compare your metrics against the contribution formula documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow Processing Time">
    New plays and earnings may take up to 24 hours to appear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filing an Earnings Dispute

If you still believe there's an error:

1. Go to **Support \> Contact**
2. Select "Earnings Dispute" category
3. Provide specific details:
   - Time period in question
   - Expected vs. actual amounts
   - Relevant track IDs
   - Supporting evidence
4. Submit and await response

### Resolution Timeline

| Stage          | Typical Duration     |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| Initial review | 2-3 business days    |
| Investigation  | 5-10 business days   |
| Resolution     | Varies by complexity |

## Content Disputes

### Copyright Claims

If you believe content infringes your copyright:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Evidence">
    Document your original work with timestamps, registration, or other proof.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File a Report">
    Use the report function on the infringing content or contact support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Documentation">
    Submit proof of ownership and specific infringement details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Await Review">
    The platform reviews claims and may contact the uploader.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Counter-Claims

If your content was removed due to a claim:

1. Review the removal notice
2. Gather proof of your rights
3. Submit a counter-claim through support
4. Await review and decision

<Warning>
  False copyright claims or counter-claims may result in account penalties.
</Warning>

## Transaction Disputes

### License Disputes

For issues with beat purchases:

| Issue              | Process                                  |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------- |
| Non-delivery       | Contact support within 48 hours          |
| Wrong files        | Request correct files from producer      |
| Quality issues     | Review before purchase; limited recourse |
| Duplicate purchase | Contact support for refund review        |

### Producer-Artist Disputes

For custom beat or collaboration issues:

1. Attempt direct resolution via messaging
2. Document all communications
3. If unresolved, contact support with full context
4. Platform may mediate but cannot enforce external agreements

## Filing a Dispute

### Required Information

Include when filing any dispute:

- Your account email/username
- Specific issue description
- Dates and times involved
- Relevant transaction or track IDs
- Screenshots or documentation
- Desired resolution

### How to File

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Through Support">
    Contact support at [support@beatpass.ca](mailto:support@beatpass.ca) with dispute details.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="In-App">
    Use the Help section in your account settings.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Report Function">
    For content issues, use the report button on the content itself.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Dispute Outcomes

### Possible Resolutions

| Outcome      | Description                                        |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Upheld       | Your dispute is validated; corrective action taken |
| Denied       | Investigation finds no issue                       |
| Partial      | Some aspects validated, others not                 |
| Inconclusive | Insufficient evidence either way                   |

### Appeals

If you disagree with a decision:

1. Review the decision rationale
2. Gather additional evidence
3. Submit a one-time appeal within 30 days
4. Appeal decisions are final
