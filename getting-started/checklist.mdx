## Universal Setup

Complete these steps regardless of whether you're a producer, artist, curator, or listener.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up at [open.beatpass.ca](https://open.beatpass.ca) with email or social login. Verify your email to unlock full features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Add a profile photo, display name, and bio. A complete profile builds trust with other users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Platform">
    Browse the beat catalog, check out trending producers, and familiarize yourself with the interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Notification Preferences">
    Configure email and in-app notifications in Settings to stay informed without overload.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Role-Specific Setup

Choose your path based on how you plan to use BeatPass:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Producer">
    ### Producer Onboarding

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Apply for Producer Access">
        Request backstage access from your profile. Include sample tracks or links to your work.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Producer Agreement">
        Review and accept the Producer Terms of Service.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload Your First Beat">
        Use the Upload Studio to add your first track with complete metadata.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set Up Payouts">
        Connect your payment method to receive earnings. Minimum payout thresholds apply.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Explore Producer Intelligence">
        Visit the Backstage Insights to understand your analytics dashboard.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Card title="Producer Guide" icon="sliders" href="/getting-started/producers">
      Detailed producer onboarding walkthrough.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Artist">
    ### Artist Onboarding

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Build Your Library">
        Browse beats and save favorites to your library for easy access.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create Your First Playlist">
        Organize saved beats by project, mood, or collaboration.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Understand Licensing">
        Review license types (non-exclusive vs exclusive) before purchasing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect with Producers">
        Follow producers whose style matches your needs. Message them for custom work.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Post a Beat Request (Optional)">
        Describe what you need and let producers come to you.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Card title="Artist Guide" icon="microphone" href="/getting-started/artists">
      Detailed artist onboarding walkthrough.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Curator">
    ### Curator Onboarding

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Public Playlists">
        Build themed playlists that showcase your taste and help others discover beats.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Follow Emerging Producers">
        Stay ahead of trends by following new talent.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Build a Following">
        Share your playlists and grow your curator audience.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply for Curator Badge (Optional)">
        Meet activity thresholds to unlock curator features.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Card title="Curator Guide" icon="list-music" href="/getting-started/curators">
      Detailed curator onboarding walkthrough.

    <Steps>

        Browse by genre to find beats matching your taste.

      <Step title="Save Favorites">
        Add beats to your library for later listening.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Follow Producers">
        Get notified when your favorite producers upload new content.
      </Step>

        Your plays and saves support producers - engagement matters!
