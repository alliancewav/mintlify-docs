## Before Your First Payout

Complete these steps to ensure you can receive earnings from BeatPass. Missing any step will delay or prevent payouts.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Producers ready to start earning
</Info>

---

## The Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Your Producer Profile">
    Go to **Backstage > Profile** and fill in:
    - Display name
    - Bio/description
    - Profile image
    - Social links (optional but recommended)
    
    <Tip>A complete profile builds trust with potential buyers.</Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Producer Approval">
    New accounts require approval to upload and sell. Check your status in **Backstage > Settings**.
    
    If pending, ensure you've:
    - Verified your email
    - Completed profile basics
    - Agreed to creator terms
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    This is **required** to receive any payouts.
    
    1. Go to **Backstage > Finances**
    2. Click **Connect with Stripe**
    3. Complete Stripe's verification:
       - Personal information
       - Identity verification (government ID)
       - Tax information (SSN/EIN or equivalent)
       - Bank account details
    
    <Warning>
      Without a connected Stripe account, you'll be marked as "Excluded" from payouts.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Your First Track">
    Go to **Backstage > Upload** and add at least one beat with:
    - Audio file (WAV or FLAC - lossless only)
    - Track artwork
    - Complete metadata (title, genre, BPM, key)
    - License pricing
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Your Payout Method">
    After connecting Stripe, verify your bank details are correct:
    
    1. Visit your [Stripe Dashboard](https://dashboard.stripe.com)
    2. Check **Settings > Bank accounts**
    3. Confirm account number and routing info
    
    Incorrect details cause failed transfers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make Your First Sale or Earn Contribution">
    Earnings come from two sources:
    
    | Source | When You're Paid |
    |--------|------------------|
    | License sales | After purchase, funds available in 2-7 days |
    | Contribution | After each paid subscription invoice, with funds flowing to your Stripe balance and then to your bank per Stripe's payout schedule |
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Checking Your Status

### In Backstage

Go to **Backstage > Finances** to see:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ **Connected** | Stripe linked and ready |
| ⏳ **Pending Verification** | Stripe needs more info |
| ❌ **Not Connected** | Must complete Stripe setup |

### Payout Eligibility

You're eligible for payouts when:

- [x] Producer account approved
- [x] Stripe Connect fully verified
- [x] At least one track uploaded
- [x] Earnings meet minimum threshold ($10 USD)

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stripe verification stuck">
    - Check email for Stripe requests
    - Log into Stripe Dashboard directly
    - Ensure ID photo is clear and matches your info
    - Contact Stripe support if stuck >48 hours
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payout shows 'Excluded'">
    Your Stripe account isn't fully connected. Go to **Backstage > Finances** and complete the setup.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Bank rejected transfer">
    - Verify account/routing numbers in Stripe
    - Ensure account accepts the payout currency
    - Try a different bank account if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Minimum threshold not met">
    Earnings under $10 carry over to the next period. Keep building your catalog and audience.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
