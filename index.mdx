## Welcome to BeatPass

BeatPass is a music licensing and beat marketplace that connects **producers** who create beats with **artists** who need them, powered by a fair contribution-based revenue system.

## Core Platform Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Beat Marketplace" icon="shop">
    Browse, preview, and license beats across genres. Filter by BPM, key, mood, and more. Every beat is audio-fingerprinted for authenticity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contribution System" icon="coins">
    Earn based on plays, saves, completions, and audience quality. Our transparent formula rewards genuine engagement over vanity metrics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Producer Analytics" icon="chart-mixed">
    Track your performance with detailed dashboards showing plays, earnings, audience demographics, and trend analysis.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Beat Requests" icon="bullhorn">
    Post requests for specific beats or respond to artist needs. Match with collaborators based on style and preferences.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
