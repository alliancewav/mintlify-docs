## Popular Topics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn money on BeatPass?" defaultOpen icon="coins">
    Producers earn through two revenue streams:

    1. **Contribution Pool** — Your share of monthly subscription revenue based on plays, engagement quality, and recency
    2. **Direct License Sales** — Keep 100% of your listed price when artists purchase exclusive or non-exclusive licenses

    <Card title="Learn the Contribution Formula" icon="calculator" horizontal href="/contribution/formula">
      
    </Card>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I upload my first beat?" icon="upload">
    Approved producers can upload unlimited beats. Each upload is audio-fingerprinted for protection.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Apply for Producer Access">
        Request backstage access from your profile settings.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload Your Audio">
        Go to Backstage → Upload and drag in your WAV or MP3 file.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Metadata">
        Fill in title, BPM, key, genre, and tags for discoverability.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Review and publish to make your beat live.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Card title="Full Upload Guide" icon="book" horizontal href="/product-guides/uploads">
      
    </Card>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do licenses work?" icon="file-contract">
    BeatPass offers two license types:

    | License           | Description                     | Best For                            |
    | ----------------- | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
    | **Non-Exclusive** | Beat stays available after sale | Mixtapes, demos, budget projects    |
    | **Exclusive**     | Beat removed from catalog       | Commercial releases, major projects |

    <Card title="Complete Licensing Guide" icon="book" horizontal href="/product-guides/licensing">
      
    </Card>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When do I get paid?" icon="credit-card">
    Payouts are processed monthly via Stripe Connect:

    - **Earnings accumulate** throughout the month
    - **Calculations finalize** on the 1st-5th
    - **Transfers sent** within 1 week
    - **Minimum threshold**: \$10 USD

    <Card title="Payout Details" icon="money-bill" horizontal href="/contribution/payouts">
      
    </Card>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
