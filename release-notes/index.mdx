## Latest Update

<Update label="February 4, 2026" tags={["platform-fee", "billing", "pricing"]} description="Platform Fee Structure Update">
  **Platform fee changed from 15% to 12% + $3**
  
  - Lower costs for beats priced $150+
  - No change at $100 price point
  - Producers still receive 100% of listed price
  
  [Read full update →](/release-notes/v3.0/3.0.8-24)
</Update>
