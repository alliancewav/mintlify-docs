# December 2025 Production Launch

<Info>
  **Version 3.0.8** is now live in production! This release brings comprehensive platform upgrades including Stripe Connect payments, a redesigned Producer Dashboard, lyrics management, mobile improvements, and much more.
</Info>

---

## Highlights

This production release combines months of development work from our beta phase into a polished, production-ready platform. Key highlights include:

- **Stripe Connect** — Producers receive direct payouts to their bank accounts
- **100% Revenue Share** — Producers keep 100% of their price; platform fee added on top
- **Lyrics Notepad** — Write, sync, and manage lyrics for any beat
- **Producer Dashboard Overhaul** — New finances, activity, progress, and album management
- **Engagement Metrics** — Track listen duration, completion rates, and quality scores
- **Mobile Player Refresh** — Redesigned mini player and overlay experience
- **Radio & Recommendations** — Personalized discovery based on listening history
- **Exclusive Licensing** — Sell and configure custom exclusive licenses
- **Messaging Improvements** — Bug fixes and performance enhancements
- **Notification Management** — Full control over email, browser, and in-app notifications
- **New Help Center** — Comprehensive documentation at [docs.beatpass.ca](https://docs.beatpass.ca)

### Stripe Connect Payments

[Learn more about Stripe Connect →](/help/earnings/stripe-connect/setup-guide)

### 100% Revenue Share

<Tip>
  **You keep 100% of your price.** The 12% + $3 platform fee is added on top for buyers, not deducted from your earnings. Set a 100 dollar beat price? You receive 100 dollars.
</Tip>

This applies to both membership subscriptions and beat sales:

| Revenue Type | Producer Gets | Platform Fee |
|--------------|---------------|--------------|
| Subscription revenue | 100% of contribution | Paid by subscriber |
| Exclusive beat sales | 100% of sale price | Added on top for buyer |
| Non-exclusive downloads | Part of subscription pool | Included in subscription |

[Learn more about earnings →](/help/earnings)

#### New Engagement Metrics (Track Insights)

Track insights now include powerful engagement tracking:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Engagement Overview" icon="chart-line">
    Four key metrics summarizing listener interaction:
    
    - **Avg Listen Duration** — How long listeners engage with your track
    - **Avg Completion Rate** — Percentage of track completed on average
    - **Total Listen Time** — Cumulative listening time across all plays
    - **Completed Listens** — Plays reaching 80%+ completion
    
    Includes a quality badge (High/Good/Moderate/Low Engagement) based on completion and listen duration.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Listen Duration Chart" icon="clock">
    Interactive bar chart showing how long listeners engage over time:
    
    - **Color-coded bars** — Green (70%+ completion), Amber (40-70%), Red (under 40%)
    - **Trend indicator** — Shows if engagement is improving or declining
    - **Tooltips** — Duration, completion rate, and play count per day
    
    Helps identify patterns and optimize your track's structure.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Completion Breakdown" icon="chart-pie">
    Distribution of how much listeners complete your track:
    
    | Category | Range | Meaning |
    |----------|-------|---------|
    | **Completed** | 80%+ | Heard almost all |
    | **Mostly** | 60-80% | Strong engagement |
    | **Half** | 40-60% | About half |
    | **Partial** | 20-40% | Lost interest |
    | **Skipped** | Under 20% | Skipped early |
    
    Includes a **Quality Score** (0-100) summarizing overall retention.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

[Learn about engagement metrics →](/help/analytics/backstage-insights/engagement-metrics)

## Unclaimed Producer Profiles

### Onboarding for Existing Artists

If your music is already on BeatPass but you don't control the profile, you can now claim it:

1. Go to the producer application flow
2. Select **Claim Existing Artist**
3. Search for your profile
4. Submit verification documents
5. Once approved, gain full control

[Learn about claiming a profile →](/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-artist)
