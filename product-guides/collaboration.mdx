## Overview

BeatPass supports collaboration between producers through track credits, split arrangements, and collaboration analytics. Build your network and create together.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Producers
</Info>

---

## Collaboration Types

### Track Credits

Add collaborators to your uploads:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Co-Producer | Joint production credit |
| Featured | Featured artist on the beat |
| Composer | Melody or composition credit |
| Mix/Master | Engineering credit |

### Adding Collaborators

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload or Edit Track">
    Access the track details form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Collaborator">
    Search for BeatPass users or enter names manually.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Role">
    Specify their contribution type.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Collaborator receives notification if they have a BeatPass account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Revenue Splits

<Warning>
  Revenue split functionality is managed manually. BeatPass does not automatically split payments between collaborators.
</Warning>

### Current Process

1. Primary uploader receives all platform earnings
2. Collaborators arrange splits privately
3. Document agreements outside the platform
4. Honor commitments to maintain trust

### Best Practices

- Discuss splits before uploading
- Put agreements in writing
- Be transparent about earnings
- Pay collaborators promptly

## Reposts

Reposts let you share tracks to your profile, helping collaborators and favorite producers gain visibility.

### How Reposts Work

When you repost a track:

1. It appears on your profile in a "Reposts" section
2. Your followers see it in their feed
3. The original creator is notified
4. You can undo at any time

### Reposting a Track

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Track">
    Navigate to any track page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Repost">
    Click the repost icon (ðŸ”„) below the track.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Track is immediately shared to your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Undoing a Repost

To remove a repost:

1. Go to the track page (or your profile > Reposts)
2. Click the repost icon again
3. The repost is removed instantly

### Where Reposts Appear

| Location | Visibility |
|----------|------------|
| Your profile | "Reposts" tab |
| Followers' feeds | Activity feed |
| Track page | Shows repost count |

### Repost Limits

To prevent spam:

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Reposts per hour | 20 |
| Reposts per day | 100 |
