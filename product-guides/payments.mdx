## Overview

BeatPass handles two types of financial transactions: payments from buyers and payouts to producers. All transactions are processed securely through **Stripe** and **Stripe Connect**.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Artists (buyers) and Producers (sellers)
</Info>

---

## For Buyers: Making Payments

### Payment Methods

BeatPass accepts:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay (where supported by browser)

### Per-Track Purchase Flow

BeatPass uses a per-track purchase modelâ€”no cart or checkout page. Each track is purchased individually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Track">
    Navigate to the track page you want to license.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select License">
    Choose your license tier (Non-Exclusive or Exclusive).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate Purchase">
    Click **Buy License**. The system calculates the final price including any platform fees.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment">
    Complete payment via Stripe's secure checkout (card or digital wallet).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Payment is processed. License is generated automatically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Files">
    Download track files and your license certificate immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  There is no shopping cart. Each license purchase is a separate transaction.
</Note>

### Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="PCI Compliance">
    All payment processing is PCI DSS compliant through Stripe.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Stored Card Data">
    BeatPass never stores your full card details. Stripe handles all sensitive data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Encryption">
    All transactions use TLS encryption.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Receipts & Records

After each purchase:

- Email receipt sent automatically
- Transaction appears in your account history
- License certificate available for download
- Records accessible for tax purposes

### Redeeming Coupons

If you have a coupon or discount code:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Purchase">
    Click **Buy License** on the track page as normal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Coupon">
    Look for "Have a coupon?" or "Promo code" field on the payment screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply">
    Enter your code and click **Apply**. The discount will reflect in the total.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Pay the discounted amount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Coupon Rules

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **First-payment-only** | Some coupons only apply to your first purchase |
| **Product-specific** | May only work for certain tracks or license types |
| **Expiration** | Coupons have expiration dates |
| **One-time use** | Most coupons can only be used once |

#### Coupon Errors

| Error | Meaning |
|-------|--------|
| "Invalid code" | Code doesn't exist or is misspelled |
| "Expired" | Coupon has passed its expiration date |
| "Already used" | You've already redeemed this coupon |
| "Not eligible" | Coupon doesn't apply to this product |

## For Producers: Receiving Payouts

### Earning Revenue

Producers earn through:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Contribution Pool** | Share of subscription revenue based on engagement |
| **License Sales** | Direct payment from track purchases via Stripe Connect |

### Stripe Connect Onboarding

<Warning>
  You must complete Stripe Connect onboarding before you can receive payouts from license sales.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Finances">
    Go to **Backstage > Finances** or **Settings > Payments**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Onboarding">
    Click **Connect with Stripe** to begin the Stripe Connect setup.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Stripe requires:
    - Legal name and date of birth
    - Address verification
    - Tax identification (SSN in US, SIN in Canada, etc.)
    - Bank account for payouts
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Return to BeatPass">
    After completing Stripe's flow, you're redirected back. Your account status updates automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Onboarding Status

Check your onboarding status via:

```http
GET /api/v1/artist/stripe-connect/status
```

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `not_started` | Haven't begun onboarding |
| `pending` | Onboarding in progress |
| `complete` | Ready to receive payouts |
| `restricted` | Account needs attention |

### Stripe Connect Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Status: Restricted">
    Your Stripe account needs attention. Common causes:
    - Missing identity verification documents
    - Bank account verification failed
    - Business information incomplete
    
    **Fix:** Click "Update Stripe Account" in Backstage > Finances to complete missing steps.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Status: Pending Verification">
    Stripe is reviewing your submitted documents. This typically takes 1-3 business days.
    
    **Action:** Wait for Stripe's review. You'll receive an email when complete.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payouts Paused">
    Stripe has paused payouts to your bank. Common causes:
    - Bank account details incorrect
    - Multiple failed payout attempts
    - Account flagged for review
    
    **Fix:** Log into your Stripe Dashboard directly to resolve banking issues.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Need to Update Documents">
    Stripe periodically requires updated verification. You'll receive an email notification.
    
    **Fix:** Click the link in the email or go to Backstage > Finances > Update Documents.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Re-onboarding">
    If your Connect account was disconnected, you'll need to re-onboard.
    
    **Steps:** Go to Backstage > Finances > Connect with Stripe to start fresh.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  For Stripe-specific issues, you can also access your connected account directly at [dashboard.stripe.com](https://dashboard.stripe.com).
</Note>

### Payout Flow for License Sales

When a buyer purchases your track:

1. **Payment collected** - Stripe processes the buyer's payment
2. **Fees deducted** - Platform fee and Stripe processing fees removed
3. **Transfer initiated** - Remaining amount transferred to your Stripe Connect account
4. **Payout to bank** - Stripe pays out to your bank per your schedule

### Payout Schedule

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **License Sales** | Transferred immediately after purchase (minus fees) |
| **Contribution Pool** | Calculated per subscription invoice and processed shortly after each successful billing (`invoice.paid`) |
| **Bank Payout** | Per your Stripe settings (typically 2-5 business days) |

### Payout Dashboard

Track your earnings in **Backstage > Finances**:

- Stripe Connect status
- License sale transactions
- Subscription contribution earnings
- Payout history
- Unified transaction view

## Subscription Payouts

In addition to license sales, producers earn from the subscription contribution pool based on engagement.

### Subscription Payout Endpoints

| Method | Endpoint | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| GET | `/artist/subscription-payouts` | List subscription payout history |
| GET | `/artist/subscription-payouts/summary` | Get payout summary stats |
| GET | `/artist/subscription-payouts/{splitId}` | Get specific payout details |
| GET | `/artist/unified-transactions` | Combined view of all earnings |

### Requirements

<Warning>
  Stripe Connect onboarding must be complete to receive subscription payouts.
</Warning>

- Complete Stripe Connect onboarding
- Have eligible tracks with engagement
- Meet minimum payout thresholds

### What's Included

Subscription payouts include:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Contribution Pool** | Your share based on track engagement |
| **Play Revenue** | Streaming revenue from subscribers |
| **Quality Bonuses** | Additional earnings for high completion rates |

### Unified Transactions

The `/artist/unified-transactions` endpoint combines:
- License sale proceeds
- Subscription payout splits
- Bonus earnings

This provides a single view of all revenue for accounting purposes.

## Fees & Splits

### Platform Fees

| Transaction Type | Fee Structure |
|------------------|---------------|
| License Sales | Platform takes percentage of sale |
| Contribution Payouts | Paid from contribution pool |

<Tip>
  Exact fee percentages are detailed in your Producer Agreement.
</Tip>

### Stripe Fees

Standard Stripe processing fees apply to payouts. These are deducted automatically.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment declined">
    Check card details, available balance, and try again. Contact your bank if issues persist.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payout not received">
    Verify your Stripe Connect setup. Check payout history for status. Allow 2-5 business days after processing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missing earnings">
    Contribution calculations run after each subscription invoice is paid (not just at month end). License sales are reflected once the payment succeeds and the webhook has been processed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong amount">
    Review the earnings breakdown in your dashboard. Contact support if discrepancies remain.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Refunds & Disputes

### Buyer Refunds

Digital licenses are generally non-refundable. Exceptions may apply for:

- Technical issues preventing download
- Duplicate purchases
- Unauthorized transactions

### Producer Disputes

If you believe earnings are incorrect:

1. Review your analytics dashboard
2. Check contribution calculation dates
3. Contact support with specific details
