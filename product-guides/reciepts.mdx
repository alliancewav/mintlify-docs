## Overview

After purchasing a license on BeatPass, you receive both a receipt and a license certificate. This guide explains how to access and use these documents.

---

## What You Receive After Purchase

### Immediately

| Document | Delivery |
|----------|----------|
| Email confirmation | Sent to your registered email |
| License certificate | Available in your account |
| Download access | Immediate access to files |

### In Your Account

- **Purchases** section shows all your licensed tracks
- Each purchase includes downloadable certificate
- Full transaction history available

---

## License Certificates

### What's Included

Your license certificate contains:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **License UUID** | Unique identifier for this license |
| **Track name** | Name of the licensed beat |
| **Producer/artist** | Creator of the beat |
| **License type** | Exclusive or Non-Exclusive |
| **Issue date** | When the license was granted |
| **Licensee info** | Your name and email |
| **License terms** | Rights granted (usage, limits, etc.) |
| **Verification URL** | Link to verify authenticity |

### Downloading Your Certificate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Purchases">
    Navigate to your account's **Purchases** section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find the Track">
    Locate the track you want the certificate for.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Certificate">
    Click **Download Certificate** or the certificate icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the PDF">
    Save the PDF to your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Verifying a Certificate

Anyone can verify a license certificate:

1. Visit the verification URL on the certificate
2. Enter the License UUID
3. View verification status and details

<Note>
  Verification confirms the license is valid and has not been revoked. Useful for distributors, labels, or resolving disputes.
</Note>

## Purchase Receipts

### Email Receipts

You receive an email receipt containing:

- Purchase amount
- Track name
- Transaction ID
- Purchase date
- License type

### Receipt History

View all receipts in **Account > Purchases** or **Settings > Purchase History**.

## What Information Is Shared with Producers

When you purchase a license, the producer sees:

| Shared | Not Shared |
|--------|------------|
| Your display name | Your full legal name (unless same) |
| That a purchase occurred | Your email address* |
| License type | Your payment details |
| Purchase amount | Your location |

*Your email is included in the license certificate, which the producer can access.

<Note>
  Producers receive a notification email that their beat was licensed, including basic transaction details.
</Note>

## Tax Considerations for Buyers

### What BeatPass Provides

- Purchase receipts/invoices
- Transaction history export (where available)
- License certificates as proof of purchase

### Tax & VAT Handling

| Region | Tax Handling |
|--------|--------------|
| **United States** | No sales tax currently collected |
| **Canada** | Prices as shown; GST/HST may apply |
| **EU** | VAT may be included based on location |
| **Other** | Local tax rules apply |

<Warning>
  **Consult a tax professional** for guidance on how to report license purchases in your jurisdiction. BeatPass provides purchase documentation but doesn't provide tax advice.
</Warning>

### For Business Purchases

If purchasing for business:

- Keep receipts for expense documentation
- License certificates serve as proof of rights
- Transaction history can support accounting records

## Stems & Deliverables

### When Stems Are Included

| License Type | Stems Included? | How to Get Them |
|--------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Non-Exclusive** (subscription download) | Usually not | Request from producer via messaging |
| **Exclusive** (separate purchase) | Yes, automatically | Included in download package |

### What You Receive by Default

| Download Type | Files Included |
|---------------|---------------|
| **Subscription download** | MP3 (320 kbps) + WAV (lossless) |
| **Exclusive purchase** | MP3 + WAV + Stems + Project files (if available) |

### Requesting Stems for Non-Exclusive Downloads

If you downloaded a beat with your subscription and need stems:

1. **Message the producer** via the in-app messaging system
2. **Explain your use case** (mixing, mastering, remix, etc.)
3. **Producer may provide stems** free or for a small fee

<Tip>
  Most producers are happy to provide stems on request. Contact them before purchasing exclusive rights if stems are your primary need.
</Tip>

### Accessing Stems

If stems are included in your purchase:

1. Go to **Purchases** in your account
2. Click on the track
3. Download stems from the **Files** section (separate ZIP or individual files)

### If Stems Are Missing

| Situation | Resolution |
|-----------|------------|
| **Non-exclusive download, no stems** | This is normal; contact producer to request |
| **Exclusive purchase, no stems** | Contact supportâ€”stems should be included |
| **Download link not working** | Try again; contact support if persistent |

<Warning>
  Stems are **not guaranteed** with subscription (non-exclusive) downloads. If stems are critical for your project, verify with the producer before downloading or purchase exclusive rights.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive my email receipt">
    - Check spam/junk folder
    - Verify your email address is correct in settings
    - Download from Purchases in your account
    - Contact support if still missing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't download my license certificate">
    - Ensure you're logged into the purchasing account
    - Try a different browser
    - Check that the purchase completed (not pending)
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Need receipt for taxes/accounting">
    - Download from Purchases section
    - Email receipts serve as documentation
    - Contact support for official transaction letters
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Certificate shows wrong information">
    - Contact support immediately
    - Do not distribute content until resolved
    - Provide correct information
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
