## Overview

This guide walks you through the complete process of purchasing a beat license on BeatPass—from discovery to download.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Artists looking to license beats
</Info>

---

## Step 1: Find Your Beat

### Browse & Search

Use the **Discover** page to find beats:

- **Genre filters** — Hip-Hop, R&B, Pop, Electronic, etc.
- **BPM range** — Filter by tempo
- **Key** — Match your vocal key
- **Mood tags** — Chill, Energetic, Dark, Uplifting
- **License type** — Non-exclusive or Exclusive available

### Preview Tracks

Click any track to:
- Play the full preview
- See license pricing
- View producer profile
- Check included files (stems, WAV, MP3)

<Tip>
  Save beats to your library (heart icon) to compare options before purchasing.
</Tip>

---

## Step 2: Choose Your License

### License Tiers

| Tier | Typical Use | What's Included |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Basic** | Demos, mixtapes, limited streams | MP3/WAV, basic rights |
| **Premium** | Commercial releases, higher stream limits | Stems, WAV, extended rights |
| **Exclusive** | Full ownership, beat removed from store | All files, exclusive rights |

### Understanding Rights

Each license specifies:
- **Stream/sales limits** — How many streams or sales allowed
- **Distribution** — Where you can release
- **Credit requirements** — Producer credit format
- **Exclusivity** — Whether others can license the same beat

<Note>
  Read the license terms carefully before purchasing. See [License Terms](/legal/license-terms) for full details.
</Note>

---

## Step 3: Complete Checkout

### Payment Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add to Cart">
    Click **Buy License** and select your tier.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Cart">
    Verify the beat and license type are correct.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment">
    Pay via credit card, debit card, or supported payment methods.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You'll receive an email confirmation with your order details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Debit cards
- Apple Pay / Google Pay (where available)

---

## Step 4: Download Your Files

### Immediate Access

After purchase, download your files immediately:

1. Go to **Library > Purchases**
2. Find your beat
3. Click **Download**
4. Choose file type (WAV, MP3, Stems if included)

### What You'll Receive

| File | Format | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| **Master WAV** | 44.1kHz/24-bit WAV | High-quality final mix |
| **MP3** | 320kbps MP3 | Preview/reference |
| **Stems** | Individual WAV tracks | Custom mixing (Premium+) |
| **License Certificate** | PDF | Proof of license rights |

<Warning>
  Download and save your files immediately. Keep your license certificate—it's your proof of rights.
</Warning>

---

## Step 5: Access Your Receipt

### Finding Receipts

1. Go to **Account > Order History**
2. Click any order to view details
3. Click **Download Receipt** for PDF

### Receipt Includes

- Order date and number
- Beat title and producer
- License type purchased
- Payment amount
- License terms summary

<Tip>
  Receipts can also be used for business expense tracking.
</Tip>

---

## After Purchase

### Using Your License

- Credit the producer as specified in your license
- Stay within your stream/sales limits
- Keep your license certificate accessible
- Upgrade your license if you exceed limits

### Need Stems Later?

If you purchased Basic and need stems:
1. Contact the producer
2. Upgrade to Premium tier (price difference may apply)
3. Producer may offer direct stem sales

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Digital licenses are generally non-refundable once files are downloaded. Contact support for issues with defective files.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I exceed my stream limit?">
    Contact the producer to upgrade your license before exceeding limits. Using beyond your license terms may result in copyright claims.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use the beat on YouTube?">
    Yes, most licenses allow YouTube. Check your specific license for monetization and Content ID policies.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when someone buys exclusive?">
    Exclusive purchases remove the beat from the store. Your existing license remains valid for its terms, but no new licenses will be sold.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
