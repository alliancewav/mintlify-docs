## Still Need Help?

If you can't find a solution to your problem:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search the Help Center">
    Use the search bar at the top of this page to find related articles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    Email us at [**contact@beatpass.ca**](mailto:contact@beatpass.ca) with a detailed description of your issue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When contacting support, please include:
- Your account email address
- A description of what you were trying to do
- Any error messages you received
- Screenshots if possible
- Your browser and device type
