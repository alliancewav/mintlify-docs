Deleting your BeatPass account permanently removes your profile and associated data from the platform. This action cannot be undone, so please read this guide carefully before proceeding.

---

## Before You Delete

### Consider These Points

Before deleting your account, think about:

- **Downloaded content** — Have you downloaded any purchased beats or licenses?
- **Uploaded content** — Your tracks, albums, and playlists will be removed
- **Subscriptions** — Active subscriptions will be cancelled
- **Account data** — Everything associated with your account will be deleted

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. All your data, content, and history will be removed and cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently removed:

| Data Type | What Happens |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Profile** | Your user profile and public page |
| **Uploaded Content** | All tracks, albums, and uploaded files |
| **Playlists** | Your created playlists |
| **Social Connections** | Followers, following, linked accounts |
| **Messages** | Your conversation history |
| **Notifications** | All notifications |
| **Comments** | Comments you've made |
| **Likes & Reposts** | Your engagement history |
| **Subscriptions** | Cancelled and removed |
| **Files** | Any files stored in your account |
| **Sessions** | All active login sessions |
| **API Tokens** | Any created access tokens |

---

## What Happens to Subscriptions

If you have an active subscription:

- It will be **immediately cancelled**
- You will not be charged further
- Remaining subscription time is forfeited
- No prorated refunds are provided for partial periods

<Note>
  If you just want to cancel your subscription without deleting your account, you can do so from the [Billing page](/help/billing).
</Note>

## How to Delete Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Click your **profile avatar** → **Edit Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Danger Zone">
    Scroll to the bottom to find the **Danger zone** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Warnings">
    Read the information about what will be deleted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Decision">
    You may be asked to enter your password or confirm in another way.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Deletion">
    Confirm the deletion. Your account will be removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Deletion

### Immediate Effects

- You're logged out immediately
- You cannot log back in with those credentials
- Your profile page returns a "not found" error
- Your username becomes available for others

### Data Retention

- Most data is deleted immediately
- Some data may be retained briefly for technical or legal reasons
- Backup systems may retain data temporarily before purging

---

## Alternatives to Deletion

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, consider these alternatives:

### Take a Break

Instead of deleting, you can:
- Log out and stop using the platform
- Remove your uploaded content manually
- Disconnect social accounts

### Cancel Subscription Only

If cost is the issue:
- Cancel your subscription but keep the account
- Your account remains active on the free tier
- Your content and followers are preserved

### Contact Support

If you have specific concerns:
- Reach out to support before deleting
- The support team may be able to address your issues
- Some problems have solutions short of deletion

---

## Re-registering After Deletion

If you delete your account and later want to return:

- You can create a **new account**
- You'll need to use the same or different email
- Your old data, followers, and content **cannot be restored**
- You start fresh as a new user

<Info>
  Deleted accounts cannot be recovered. If you think you might want to return, consider keeping your account rather than deleting it.
</Info>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I recover my account after deletion?">
    No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will my uploaded tracks still be accessible?">
    No. All your uploaded content is deleted along with your account. Anyone who had access to your tracks will no longer be able to play or download them.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What about purchases I made?">
    Make sure you've downloaded any purchased content before deleting. After deletion, you won't be able to access purchase history or re-download content.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I delete my account without canceling my subscription first?">
    Yes. Deleting your account automatically cancels any active subscriptions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does deletion take?">
    Account deletion is typically processed immediately. Some background cleanup may continue for a short time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Need Help?

If you're experiencing issues with your account that are prompting you to delete:

- **Technical problems** — Contact support for troubleshooting help
- **Privacy concerns** — Review your privacy settings; deletion may not be necessary
- **Billing issues** — The support team can help resolve payment problems
