The **Location and language** section stores the regional preferences for your BeatPass account.

---

## Available Settings

| Setting | What It Controls |
|---------|------------------|
| **Language** | Interface text where translations are available |
| **Country** | Your saved account region |
| **Timezone** | How dates and times are shown to you |

---

## Update Your Preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Open the [Account Settings](https://open.beatpass.ca/account-settings) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Location and language">
    Scroll to the **Location and language** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your values">
    Select your preferred **Language**, **Country**, and **Timezone**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If times across BeatPass look wrong, check your **Timezone** first.
</Tip>

---

## How Localization Works

### Language

Your selected language is used for interface labels and other BeatPass text where translations exist.

<Note>
  User-created content is not automatically translated.
</Note>

### Country

Your country is stored as part of your account preferences.

### Timezone

BeatPass uses your saved timezone when showing dates and times throughout the product.

---

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Times still look wrong after changing timezone">
    Make sure you clicked **Save**, then refresh the page. If the problem continues, confirm that you selected the timezone closest to your location.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My language isn't available">
    Only the languages currently configured for BeatPass appear in the dropdown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Some text is still in English">
    That usually means a translation is not available for that part of the interface yet, or the text was created by another user.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
