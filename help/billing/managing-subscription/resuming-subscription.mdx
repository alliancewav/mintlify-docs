If you've cancelled your subscription but the paid period has not ended yet, BeatPass lets you renew it from the Billing page.

---

## How to Resume

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Page">
    Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation bar, then select **Billing** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Renew Option">
    In the cancelled **Current plan** card, click **Renew plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the renewal page">
    BeatPass opens a renewal confirmation page for the same plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish renewal">
    Click **Confirm Renewal**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What Happens When You Resume

### Immediate Effects

- **Subscription reactivated** — Status changes back to active
- **Features continue** — No interruption in access
- **Renewal restored** — The pending cancellation is removed

### Billing

- **No immediate charge** — You've already paid for the current period
- **Next charge at renewal** — Normal billing resumes on the same schedule

---

## Requirements for Resuming

You can only resume if:

- **Before the access end date** — Your subscription hasn't fully expired yet
- **Same account** — You're logged into the account that had the subscription

<Warning>
  Once the grace period ends, you cannot resume. You'll need to start a new subscription, which may be at current pricing.
</Warning>

---

## When You Can't Resume

Resuming is **not available** if:

- Your grace period has ended

In this case, you'll need to subscribe again as a new subscription.

---

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see a resume option">
    The **Renew plan** button only appears before the access end date. If that date has passed, use **View Plans** to start a new paid subscription.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I resumed but my features aren't back">
    If the Billing page shows the plan as active again but features appear unchanged, contact support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I be charged when I resume?">
    No. You've already paid for your current billing period. The next charge happens at your scheduled renewal date, which remains unchanged.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
