## Where to Manage Your Subscription

All subscription changes are made in one place:

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation bar
2. Select **Billing** from the menu
3. In the **Current plan** section, choose the action you want to take:
   - **Change plan** — To upgrade or downgrade
   - **Cancel plan** — To cancel your subscription
   - **Renew plan** — To undo a cancellation before access ends

---

## How Plan Changes Work

| Action | What BeatPass Does |
|--------|--------------------|
| **Upgrade to a higher-priced plan** | Can be applied immediately or scheduled for renewal, depending on the confirmation page and payment method state |
| **Downgrade to a lower-priced plan** | Scheduled for your renewal date |
| **Cancel plan** | Stops future renewal but keeps access until the current paid period ends |
| **Renew plan** | Removes the cancellation before the end date |

<Note>
  Current paid plans are billed monthly.
</Note>

---

## Pending Changes

If you schedule a future plan change, Billing shows **Scheduled Plan Change** with a **Cancel scheduled change** button.

The scheduled card also shows the date when the change will take effect.
