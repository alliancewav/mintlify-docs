The Billing page changes depending on whether you have no paid plan, an active paid plan, or a cancelled plan that has not ended yet.

---

## Open Billing

1. Click your **profile avatar**
2. Select **Billing**

---

## Subscription States

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="No Paid Plan">
    Billing shows:

    - A **Subscription** panel
    - A **View Plans** button
    - **Payment history**

    <Note>
      You will not see **Current plan** or **Payment method** until you have a paid subscription.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Active Subscription">
    Billing shows a **Current plan** card with:

    - The current plan name
    - The monthly price
    - **Active Subscription**
    - **Renews on** date
    - **Change plan**
    - **Cancel plan**

    Active paid subscriptions also show **Payment method** and **Payment history**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Cancelled Subscription">
    If you cancelled but your paid period is still running, the **Current plan** card changes to:

    - **Subscription Cancelled**
    - **Access ends** date
    - **Renew plan**

    You keep the plan until the end date shown on the card.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Pending Plan Changes

If you scheduled a future plan change, Billing shows a second card titled **Scheduled Plan Change**.

That card includes:

- The upcoming plan name
- Its monthly price
- **Takes effect on** date
- **Cancel scheduled change**

---

## Payment Method on File

When a paid subscription has a saved card, Billing also shows:

- **Card brand** — Visa, Mastercard, etc.
- **Last 4 digits** — For identification
- **Expiration date** — When the card expires

<Warning>
  If your payment method expires or fails, your subscription may not renew. Keep your payment information up to date to avoid service interruption.
</Warning>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I see my next billing date?">
    Click your **profile avatar** → **Billing**. Your next renewal date is shown in the Current plan section with the text "Renews on [date]".
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I know which plan I'm on?">
    Your current plan name and price are displayed in the **Current plan** section of your Billing page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when my subscription expires?">
    Your paid plan ends and Billing returns to the non-paid **Subscription** state with **View Plans** and **Payment history**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see past subscriptions?">
    Your invoices show your billing history. Click your **profile avatar** → **Billing** and scroll to the **Payment history** section.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see a Payment method section?">
    That usually means you do not currently have an active paid subscription with a saved card.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
