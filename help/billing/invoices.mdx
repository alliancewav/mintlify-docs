The **Payment history** section on Billing shows your past payment records, including subscription payments and track purchases.

---

## Viewing Your Invoices

To access your invoice history:

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation bar
2. Select **Billing** from the menu
3. Scroll down to the **Payment history** section
4. Browse your payment history

---

## Types of Invoices

BeatPass tracks two types of payments in your invoice history:

### Subscription Invoices

For recurring subscription payments:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Plan name** | The subscription plan you paid for |
| **Amount** | The amount shown on the payment-history entry for that invoice |
| **Date** | When the payment was processed |
| **Status** | Whether the payment was successful |

### Track Purchase Invoices

For individual beat/track purchases:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Track name** | The beat or track you purchased |
| **Amount** | Total paid (including platform fee) |
| **Purchase date** | When you bought it |
| **License type** | The type of license purchased |

---

## Invoice Details

The payment history list currently shows:

- **Date** — When the payment occurred
- **Amount** — The amount shown on that payment-history entry
- **Description** — What the charge was for
- **Payment status** — **Paid** or **Unpaid**

---

## Viewing a Specific Invoice

When the open-in-new icon appears next to an invoice:

1. Go to the **Payment history** section on your billing page
2. Click the open icon
3. The invoice opens in a new tab

---

## Downloading Invoices

After you open an invoice in a new tab, use your browser's print or save tools if you need a copy for your records.

<Tip>
  Downloaded invoices are useful for expense tracking, tax purposes, or business accounting.
</Tip>

---

## Invoice Retention

Your payment history remains on the Billing page for the account that made the payment.

---

## Understanding Invoice Amounts

### For Subscriptions

When you open a subscription invoice, BeatPass can show separate line items for your plan and the platform fee (`12% + $3.00 flat`).

### For Beat Purchases

When you open a track-purchase invoice, BeatPass can show:
- **Track price** — Set by the producer
- **Platform fee** — `12% + $3.00 flat` added to the price

For example, a \$100 beat shows as \$115 on your invoice.

---

## Failed Payments

If a payment failed:

- The list shows **Unpaid**
- Your subscription may not renew until resolved
- Update your payment method and try again

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't find an invoice">
    Check that you're logged into the correct account. Invoices are only visible to the account that made the payment. If you recently made a purchase, it may take a few moments to appear.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The amount doesn't match what I expected">
    For subscriptions, remember the total charge includes the platform fee on top of the plan price. For exclusive purchases, the final amount includes the track price plus the platform fee shown during checkout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I need an invoice for tax purposes">
    Open the invoice in a new tab when the open icon is available, then save or print it from your browser.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund for an invoice?">
    Refund policies vary. Contact support if you believe you're entitled to a refund. See the [refund policy](/help/billing/refund-policy) for more details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
