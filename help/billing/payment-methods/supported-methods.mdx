BeatPass accepts various payment methods to make subscribing and purchasing convenient for users worldwide.

---

## Credit & Debit Cards

BeatPass accepts major credit and debit cards through Stripe:

| Card Type | Supported |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Visa** | Yes |
| **Mastercard** | Yes |
| **American Express** | Yes |
| **Discover** | Yes |
| **Other major cards** | May be supported depending on region |

### Card Requirements

- Must be valid and not expired
- Must have sufficient funds or credit available
- Must allow online/international transactions

<Note>
  Some cards may be declined if your bank's fraud protection is triggered. If your card is declined, try contacting your bank to authorize the transaction.
</Note>

---

## PayPal

PayPal is available as an alternative payment option:

- **Linked bank account or card** — Pay via your PayPal balance or linked payment method
- **PayPal Credit** — Where available
- **Buyer protection** — Standard PayPal protections apply

### PayPal Availability

PayPal may not be available for all subscription types or in all regions. Check if it's offered during checkout.

---

## Supported Currencies

For purchases such as beat licensing, BeatPass supports:

| Currency | Code |
|----------|------|
| **US Dollar** | USD |
| **Euro** | EUR |
| **British Pound** | GBP |
| **Canadian Dollar** | CAD |

Subscription pricing is typically displayed in your local currency or the platform's default currency.

---

## What's Not Accepted

Currently, BeatPass does not accept:

- Cash or check payments
- Wire transfers
- Cryptocurrency
- Gift cards (unless specifically noted)
- Prepaid cards that don't support recurring payments

---

## Regional Considerations

Payment method availability may vary by country:

- **Some cards** may not work for international transactions
- **PayPal** availability depends on your country
- **Currency conversion** may apply if paying in a different currency

<Tip>
  If your usual payment method doesn't work, try an alternative card or PayPal if available.
</Tip>

---

## Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely:

- **Stripe** — Primary payment processor for cards
- **PayPal** — Alternative payment option
- **SSL encryption** — All transactions are encrypted
- **PCI DSS compliant** — Industry-standard security

---

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My card type isn't listed">
    If your card is from a major issuer (Visa, Mastercard, etc.), it should work. Try adding it during checkout. Regional or prepaid cards may have limitations.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use a prepaid card?">
    Some prepaid cards work, but many don't support recurring payments. If you're using a prepaid card for a subscription, it may fail at renewal.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="PayPal isn't showing as an option">
    PayPal may not be available for all transactions or in all regions. If you don't see it, use a credit or debit card instead.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
