## Quick Reference

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="No Paid Plan">
    When you do not have an active paid subscription, **Billing** shows:

    - A **Subscription** panel with **View Plans**
    - A **Payment history** panel

    <Note>
      You will not see a **Payment method** section until you have a paid subscription with a saved card.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Active Paid Plan">
    When you have an active paid subscription, **Billing** shows:

    - **Current plan**
    - **Payment method**
    - **Payment history**

    The **Current plan** card includes **Change plan** and **Cancel plan**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Cancelled Plan">
    If you cancelled but your paid period has not ended yet, **Billing** shows:

    - **Current plan** with **Subscription Cancelled**
    - An **Access ends** date
    - A **Renew plan** button
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How Billing Works

### Open Billing

To manage billing:

1. Click your **profile avatar**
2. Select **Billing**

### Monthly Billing

Current paid plans are billed monthly. If you keep a paid plan active, BeatPass renews it automatically using the saved payment method on file.

### Subscription Changes

- **Upgrade** — Higher-priced plans can be applied immediately or scheduled for renewal, depending on the checkout state
- **Downgrade** — Lower-priced plans are scheduled for your renewal date
- **Cancel** — Stops future renewal but keeps access until the current period ends
- **Renew** — Removes a pending cancellation before access ends

## Payment Processing

BeatPass currently accepts subscription card payments through **Stripe**.

Accepted card brands currently configured on BeatPass:

- **Visa**
- **Mastercard**
- **American Express**
- **Discover**

<Note>
  PayPal is not currently enabled on BeatPass.
</Note>

If you do not already have a saved card, you usually add it during checkout when starting a paid subscription.
