## Overview

Artist insights (`/backstage/artists/{id}/insights`) show aggregate analytics across your entire catalog under that artist profile. This is the broadest view of your content performance, combining data from every track you've released.

<Info>
  **Access:** Click **Insights** in the Backstage navigation, or navigate directly to `/backstage/artists/{artistId}/insights`
</Info>

---

## Available Charts

Artist insights display the following analytics:

| Chart | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Plays Over Time** | Combined plays across all your tracks |
| **Top Devices** | Device breakdown for your audience |
| **Top Platforms** | Platform breakdown for your audience |
| **Top Locations** | Where your listeners are located worldwide |
| **Most Played Tracks** | Top performers in your catalog |
| **Top Listeners** | Your biggest fans across all content |

<Note>
  **Engagement metrics** (Listen Duration, Completion Breakdown) are **not available** at the artist level. For engagement data, view individual track insights.
</Note>

---

## Plays Over Time

An interactive bar chart showing total plays across your entire catalog.

### What It Shows

- **All-track aggregate** — Sum of plays from every track under this artist
- **Trend indicator** — Growth/decline compared to previous period
- **Career overview** — See your overall performance trajectory

### Chart Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Bars** | Each bar represents total catalog plays for that period |
| **Trend badge** | Green (+) for growth, red (-) for decline |
| **Total plays** | Aggregate count for the selected date range |
| **Footer stats** | Peak Day, Daily Avg, Data Points |

### Why It Matters

The artist-level plays chart helps you:
- **Identify overall trends** — Is your career growing or declining?
- **Measure campaign impact** — See how promotions affect total engagement
- **Set benchmarks** — Use peak days as targets for future performance

---

## Most Played Tracks

A scrollable card-style list showing your top-performing tracks across your entire catalog.

### What It Shows

- **Catalog-wide ranking** — Your best performers overall
- **Up to 10 tracks** — In a scrollable container
- **Play counts** — For the selected date range

### Card Contents

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Rank Badge** | Position (#1, #2, etc.) |
| **Cover Art** | Circular thumbnail |
| **Track Name** | Clickable link to track insights |
| **Exclusive Icon** | Diamond badge for exclusive-only tracks |
| **Artist Name(s)** | Includes collaborators if applicable |
| **Play Count Pill** | Compact pill with play icon and count |

### Reading Patterns

| Pattern | What It Means | Action |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| **Single track dominates** | Hit-driven catalog | Diversify with more content |
| **Even distribution** | Consistent quality | Continue current approach |
| **Older tracks on top** | Legacy catalog | Push new releases harder |
| **Recent tracks rising** | Fresh momentum | Capitalize on attention |

<Tip>
  Click any track to view its detailed insights page with engagement metrics, listen duration, and completion rates.
</Tip>

## Top Devices

Breakdown of device types used by your audience across all tracks.

### Layout

Vertical list of high-contrast cards:
- **Left:** Device icon and name with percentage
- **Right:** Total play count
- **Bottom:** Color-coded progress bar

### Why It Matters

Understanding device preferences helps you:
- **Optimize cover art** — High mobile usage means small thumbnails matter
- **Context awareness** — Mobile = on-the-go, Desktop = focused listening
- **Marketing decisions** — Target ads based on device preferences

---

## Top Platforms

Breakdown of operating systems used by your audience.

### Layout

2x2 grid showing top 4 platforms:
- **Header:** Platform icon and play count
- **Body:** Platform name and percentage
- **Footer:** Progress bar

### Platform Types

| Platform | Implication |
|----------|-------------|
| **Web** | Casual browsing, social media referrals |
| **iOS** | Apple ecosystem users |
| **Android** | Broader mobile market |
| **Desktop OS** | Dedicated listening sessions |

---

## Top Locations

Geographic distribution of your audience worldwide.

### Layout

- **Hero Section:** #1 location with large country flag
- **Country name** and play count prominently displayed
- **Grid:** Remaining top countries with flags and progress bars
- Shows up to 10 locations

### Strategic Uses

| Use Case | How Location Data Helps |
|----------|------------------------|
| **Touring** | Know where your fans are concentrated |
| **Release timing** | Drop content when your top regions are awake |
| **Marketing** | Focus ad spend on engaged regions |
| **Localization** | Consider content for specific markets |

---

## Top Listeners

Your biggest fans across all your content.

### Layout

Scrollable card list showing:
- **Rank badge** (#1, #2, etc.)
- **Avatar** — Circular user avatar
- **Username** — Links to user profile
- **Percentage** — Share of your total plays
- **Play count pill**

### Building Fan Relationships

Your top listeners represent your core fanbase:
- **Recognize super fans** — Engage with users who love your work
- **Find collaborators** — Discover potential partners
- **Build community** — Nurture relationships with dedicated listeners
- **Personalize outreach** — Tailor promotions to engaged users

## Artist vs Album vs Track Insights

| Feature | Artist Insights | Album Insights | Track Insights |
|---------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Scope** | Entire catalog | Single album | Single track |
| **Engagement metrics** | | | ✓ |
| **Most Played Tracks** | ✓ All tracks | ✓ Album tracks | |
| **Device/Platform** | ✓ Aggregate | ✓ Aggregate | ✓ Per-track |
| **Locations** | ✓ Aggregate | ✓ Aggregate | ✓ Per-track |
| **Top Listeners** | ✓ All content | ✓ Album content | ✓ Per-track |
