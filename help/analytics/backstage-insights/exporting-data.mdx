The standard producer-facing **Backstage Insights** pages do **not** currently show a built-in export button in the page UI.

<Info>
  If you were expecting CSV, JSON, or PDF buttons from older documentation, that guidance is out of date for the current routed producer experience.
</Info>

## What You Can Do Right Now

- Use the date range selector to isolate the period you want to review.
- Share the direct insight page link with teammates who already have access to that content.
- Capture screenshots or manual notes when you need to reference a specific chart view.

## What This Page Means

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No standard export button">
    Current track, album, and artist insight pages do not expose a visible export action for the normal producer workflow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Different internal or custom workflows may exist">
    If your team has a separate admin or internal reporting process, follow that process instead of relying on the standard producer UI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Docs will be updated if exports become visible">
    When a producer-facing export action is added to the routed insights UI, this help page should describe the exact steps and formats available.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
