Album Insights rolls performance up across the tracks in one album or collection. Use it when you want one page for the release as a whole instead of opening tracks one by one.

<Info>
  Direct route pattern: `/backstage/albums/{albumId}/insights`
</Info>

## Header Information

The current album header can include:

- release date
- track count
- total duration
- genres
- plays
- likes

## What Album Insights Does Not Show

- No track-level engagement section.
- No completion breakdown chart.
- No listen-duration chart.

For engagement analysis, open a specific track's insights page instead.
