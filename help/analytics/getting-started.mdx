## Prerequisites

Before you can access analytics, you need:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="BeatPass Account" icon="user">
    A registered BeatPass account. [Sign up](https://beatpass.ca/register) if you haven't already.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Artist Profile" icon="microphone">
    An artist profile linked to your account. Create one in your account settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="At Least One Track" icon="music">
    Upload at least one track to start seeing analytics data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 1: Access Your Producer Dashboard

The Producer Dashboard is your command center for career-level analytics.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Go to `/producer-dashboard` or click **Producer Dashboard** in the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Overview">
    The home page shows your key stats at a glance:

    - **Total Tracks** — Your catalog size
    - **Total Plays** — Lifetime engagement
    - **Followers** — Your audience size
    - **Level** — Your producer rank
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Sections">
    Use the navigation grid or sidebar to access:

    - **Progress** — XP and achievements
    - **Tracks** — Catalog management
    - **Albums** — Collections
    - **Activity** — Upload history
    - **Finances** — Earnings
    - **Leaderboards** — Rankings
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 2: View Content Analytics

Backstage Insights shows detailed analytics for individual tracks, albums, or artists.

### Quick Access Methods

| Method             | How                                               |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **Context Menu**   | Right-click any track/album/artist → **Insights** |
| **Dashboard Link** | Producer Dashboard → Tracks → Click chart icon    |
| **Direct URL**     | Navigate to `/backstage/tracks/{id}/insights`     |

### What You'll See

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Track Insights">
    - **Plays Over Time** — Interactive trend chart
    - **Engagement Metrics** — Listen duration, completion rates
    - **Top Devices** — Where listeners play from
    - **Top Locations** — Geographic distribution
    - **Top Listeners** — Your biggest fans
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Album Insights">
    - **Aggregate Plays** — Combined across all tracks
    - **Most Played Tracks** — Top performers in the album
    - **Device/Location Breakdown** — Audience demographics
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Artist Insights">
    - **Catalog-wide Analytics** — All your content combined
    - **Performance Trends** — Overall growth patterns
    - **Audience Overview** — Who's listening
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Understand Your Key Metrics

### Performance Metrics

| Metric              | What It Means                     | Good Sign             |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| **Plays**           | Times your tracks were played     | Growing over time     |
| **Downloads**       | Licensed downloads of your tracks | Active buyer interest |
| **Completion Rate** | % of track listeners complete     | 60%+ is strong        |
| **Growth Rate**     | Change in performance over time   | Positive percentage   |

### Engagement Metrics (Track-level)

| Metric                  | What It Means            | Target                |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------ | --------------------- |
| **Avg Listen Duration** | How long listeners stay  | Close to track length |
| **Completed Listens**   | Plays reaching 80%+      | Higher is better      |
| **Quality Score**       | Weighted retention score | 70+ is excellent      |

### Gamification Metrics

| Metric                     | What It Means                    |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **XP (Experience Points)** | Earned through platform activity |
| **Level**                  | Your rank based on total XP      |
| **Achievements**           | Milestones you've unlocked       |
| **Leaderboard Rank**       | Position vs other producers      |

## Step 4: Set Up Finances

To receive earnings from your music, connect Stripe:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Finances">
    Navigate to `/producer-dashboard/finances`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click **Connect with Stripe** to start onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Follow Stripe's prompts to verify identity and add bank info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Earning">
    Once connected, track sales and subscription payouts appear automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for New Users

<Tip>
  **Start with your Progress page** — It shows your XP, level, and which achievements you can easily unlock.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Complete your profile** — Profile achievements (Photogenic, Oracle, Linked, Model) are easy wins for quick XP.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Fill in all metadata** — Complete track metadata earns +50 XP per track and improves discoverability.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Check back regularly** — Analytics update frequently, and consistent engagement improves your rankings.
</Tip>

## Common First Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see any plays?">
    New tracks may take a few minutes to start showing analytics. If you uploaded recently, check back in 15-30 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn XP quickly?">
    The fastest ways:

    - Upload tracks (+100 XP each)
    - Complete all metadata (+50 XP per track)
    - Get plays (+1 XP per play)
    - Unlock achievements (varies by rarity)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see my finances?">
    You need to complete Stripe Connect onboarding. Go to `/producer-dashboard/finances` and follow the setup flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often does data update?">
    - **Plays**: Near real-time
    - **XP**: Immediate
    - **Leaderboards**: Every 30 minutes
    - **Financial data**: Synced with Stripe
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
