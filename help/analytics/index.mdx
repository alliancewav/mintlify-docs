## Backstage Insights

Deep analytics for individual content items. Access by clicking the **Insights** button on any track, album, or artist page.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Track Insights" icon="record-vinyl" href="/help/analytics/backstage-insights/track-insights">
    <Badge color="blue">/backstage/tracks/{id}/insights</Badge>
    
    Engagement metrics, completion rates, listen duration
  </Card>
  <Card title="Album Insights" icon="layer-group" href="/help/analytics/backstage-insights/album-insights">
    <Badge color="purple">/backstage/albums/{id}/insights</Badge>
    
    Aggregate plays, top tracks, listener locations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Artist Insights" icon="user-music" href="/help/analytics/backstage-insights/artist-insights">
    <Badge color="green">/backstage/artists/{id}/insights</Badge>
    
    Catalog-wide analytics, audience overview
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Content">
    Go to any track, album, or artist page you own.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Insights">
    Click the **Insights** button in the header to open Backstage Insights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Metrics">
    Use the tabs and date range selector to explore plays, engagement, and audience data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
