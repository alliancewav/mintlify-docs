## Backstage Insights

Deep analytics for individual content items. Access by clicking the **Insights** button on any track, album, or artist page.

| Content Type | URL Pattern | What You'll See |
|--------------|-------------|-----------------|
| [**Track Insights**](/help/analytics/backstage-insights/track-insights) | `/backstage/tracks/{id}/insights` | Engagement metrics, completion rates, listen duration |
| [**Album Insights**](/help/analytics/backstage-insights/album-insights) | `/backstage/albums/{id}/insights` | Aggregate plays, top tracks, listener locations |
| [**Artist Insights**](/help/analytics/backstage-insights/artist-insights) | `/backstage/artists/{id}/insights` | Catalog-wide analytics, audience overview |

## When to Use Each System

| Need | Use This | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| See plays for a specific track | **Backstage Insights** | Drills into individual content |
| View listener engagement | **Backstage Insights** | Engagement metrics are per-track |
| See geographic distribution | **Backstage Insights** | Available on any content type |
| See total plays across all tracks | **[Producer Dashboard](/help/producer-dashboard)** | Aggregates your entire catalog |
| Manage track uploads | **[Producer Dashboard](/help/producer-dashboard/tracks)** | Catalog management |
| Check earnings | **[Producer Dashboard](/help/producer-dashboard/finances)** | Financial data is career-level |
| Check your XP and level | **[Gamification](/help/gamification)** | XP/levels are career-level |
| Compare yourself to others | **[Gamification](/help/gamification/leaderboards)** | Rankings are platform-wide |

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Content">
    Go to any track, album, or artist page you own.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Insights">
    Click the **Insights** button in the header to open Backstage Insights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Metrics">
    Use the tabs and date range selector to explore plays, engagement, and audience data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
