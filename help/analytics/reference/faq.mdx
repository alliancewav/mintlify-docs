## Overview

Common questions and answers about BeatPass analytics, the XP system, leaderboards, and financial reporting.

---

## Data & Updates

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often is my data updated?">
    | Data Type | Update Frequency |
    |-----------|------------------|
    | Play counts | Near real-time |
    | Download events | Near real-time |
    | XP & Level | Immediately on each action |
    | Leaderboard rankings | Every 30 minutes (cached) |
    | Progress data | Every 5 minutes (cached) |
    | Financial balances | Synced with Stripe |
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why do my play counts seem low?">
    Possible reasons:
    - Plays are tracked via analytics events and require the track to actually be played
    - There may be a slight delay in data processing during high-traffic periods
    - Check the time period filter selected in the Activity page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see any data in my dashboard?">
    You need:
    - An artist profile linked to your account
    - At least one track uploaded
    - Upload permissions enabled
    
    If you just created your profile or uploaded tracks, wait a few minutes for data to sync.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## XP & Leveling

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn XP?">
    XP is earned through various actions:
    
    | Action | XP Earned |
    |--------|-----------|
    | Upload a track | +100 XP |
    | Track plays | +1 XP per play |
    | Complete metadata | +50 XP per track |
    | Collaborate on tracks | +25 XP per collaboration |
    | Unlock achievements | Variable (based on achievement rarity) |
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why isn't my XP increasing?">
    Check that:
    - You have an artist profile with a completed user profile
    - Your tracks are actually being played (not just loaded)
    - XP deduplication prevents double-awarding for the same action
    
    Each play, upload, or action is only awarded XP once.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is my level calculated?">
    Your level is based on total XP using this formula:
    
    ```
    XP Required = 100 Ã— ((Level - 1) ^ 1.5)
    ```
    
    The maximum level is 100 (Legendary Producer).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do collaborators earn XP from my tracks?">
    Yes! When a track with collaborators gets plays:
    - The track owner earns XP
    - All credited collaborators with completed user profiles also earn XP
    
    Collaborators without completed profiles won't receive XP until they complete their profile setup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Leaderboards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is the Overall leaderboard calculated?">
    The Overall leaderboard uses a weighted composite score:
    
    | Category | Weight |
    |----------|--------|
    | Points (XP) | 25% |
    | Plays | 20% |
    | Uploads | 15% |
    | Quality | 15% |
    | Collaboration | 15% |
    | Earnings | 10% |
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why did my ranking drop even though my plays increased?">
    Rankings are relative to other producers. If other producers grew faster, your rank may drop even with personal growth. 
    
    Focus on improving your absolute metrics rather than just ranking position.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How often do leaderboards update?">
    Leaderboard data is cached for 30 minutes to ensure consistent performance. Your rank will update at most every 30 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does the Quality leaderboard measure?">
    The Quality leaderboard ranks producers by metadata completion percentage:
    - Complete title, BPM, key, scale, genre, description, and cover art
    - Higher percentage = higher ranking
    - Encourages well-organized catalogs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Achievements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I unlock achievements?">
    Achievements are unlocked by meeting specific criteria:
    - **Upload achievements**: Upload a certain number of tracks
    - **Trending achievements**: Get tracks in the top percentiles by play count
    - **Metadata achievements**: Maintain high metadata completion
    - **Collaboration achievements**: Complete collaborative tracks
    - **Milestone achievements**: Reach account age milestones
    - **Seasonal achievements**: Upload during specific holidays
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What XP do achievements give?">
    Achievement XP varies by rarity:
    
    | Rarity | XP Range |
    |--------|----------|
    | Common | 150-500 XP |
    | Uncommon | 800-1,500 XP |
    | Rare | 2,000-3,500 XP |
    | Epic | 4,000-5,000 XP |
    | Legendary | 6,000+ XP |
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I lose achievements?">
    No. Once earned, achievements are permanent and cannot be lost even if the underlying criteria changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Earnings & Finances

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I access financial data?">
    Navigate to `/producer-dashboard/finances` to:
    1. Connect your Stripe account (if not already connected)
    2. View account balances
    3. See transaction history
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="When are earnings finalized?">
    - **Track purchases**: Finalized when payment succeeds
    - **Subscription payouts**: Finalized when Stripe marks the invoice as paid and the subscription split job completes
    
    Payouts to your bank account depend on your Stripe payout schedule.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see my finances?">
    You need to:
    1. Have an artist profile
    2. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding
    3. Enable both charges and payouts in Stripe
    
    If Stripe onboarding is incomplete, you'll see the onboarding prompt instead of financial data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does 'In Transit' mean?">
    Funds marked "In Transit" have been sent to your bank but haven't arrived yet. Transfer times depend on your bank and Stripe payout schedule (typically 2-7 business days).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Downloads Analytics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do download analytics show?">
    The Downloads section in Activity shows:
    - **Total Downloads**: All-time download count
    - **Unique Users**: Number of distinct downloaders
    - **Recent Downloads**: Downloads in selected time period
    - **Top Track**: Your most downloaded track
    - **Download List**: Individual download events with user info
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I see who downloaded my tracks?">
    Yes. The downloads list shows:
    - User name and avatar
    - Track that was downloaded
    - Download timestamp
    - License certificate (if applicable)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What time periods are available?">
    You can filter downloads by:
    - 7 Days
    - 30 Days (default)
    - 90 Days
    - All Time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My dashboard isn't loading">
    Try:
    1. Refresh the page
    2. Clear browser cache
    3. Check your internet connection
    4. Try a different browser
    5. Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I see outdated data">
    Some data is cached for performance:
    - Leaderboards: 30-minute cache
    - Progress data: 5-minute cache
    
    Use the "Refresh" button on the Progress page to force an update, or wait for the cache to expire.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug?">
    Contact support via the Help section or email contact@beatpass.ca with:
    - Description of the issue
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Screenshots
    - Browser and device info
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Help

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I think my data is wrong">
    If you believe there's an error:
    1. Check the time period filters
    2. Wait for caches to refresh (up to 30 minutes)
    3. Try refreshing the page
    4. Document the issue with screenshots
    5. Contact support with specific details
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Where can I learn more about metrics?">
    See the [Metric Definitions](/help/analytics/reference/metrics-glossary) page for detailed explanations of each metric tracked by BeatPass.
