## July 2020 - Version 2.4.7

### Summary

- Upgrade smoothness and mail configuration reliability improvements.

### Enhancements

- Force subscription setting removed in favor of role based permissions.
- Improved update process when upgrading from versions older than 2.4.6.

### Fixes

- Mail credential validation, older channel editability, deprecated mail driver removal, Google Analytics validation, and legacy image loading issues resolved.
