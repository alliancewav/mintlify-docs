## Summary

A complete restoration of the mobile player overlay, bringing back the polished glassmorphism design and fixing critical usability issues.

## What's New

- **Glassmorphism**: Restored the translucent, blurred background effect for a modern, premium feel.
- **Queue Redesign**: Simplified the mobile queue with clear "Now Playing" and "Up Next" sections, matching the desktop experience.
- **Performance**: Removed heavy video background handling to improve battery life and performance on mobile devices.
- **Visual Polish**: Fixed cover art sizing, touch targets, and transition animations.

## Why It Matters

- **Usability**: The queue is now much easier to read and manage on small screens.
- **Consistency**: The mobile experience now matches the high visual standards of the desktop application.
- **Responsiveness**: The player opens and closes smoothly without lag.

## Technical Details

- **Component**: `MobilePlayerQueue` completely rewritten for simplicity and performance.
- **Styling**: Re-applied `backdrop-blur-xl` and `bg-paper/90` classes.
