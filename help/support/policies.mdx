## Content Policies

### Acceptable Content

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What You Can Upload">
    - Original beats and instrumentals you created
    - Content you have rights to distribute
    - Properly licensed samples and loops
    - Quality audio meeting technical standards
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's Prohibited">
    - Copyrighted material without permission
    - Offensive or illegal content
    - Spam or duplicate uploads
    - Misleading metadata
    - Content violating others' rights
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Copyright Policy

- You must own or have rights to all uploaded content
- Copyright claims are investigated and acted upon
- Repeat infringers may lose platform access
- See our DMCA process for copyright disputes

## Community Guidelines

### Expected Behavior

- Treat all users with respect
- Engage honestly and professionally
- Honor agreements and commitments
- Report violations rather than retaliate

### Prohibited Behavior

- Harassment or threats
- Spam or promotional abuse
- Fraud or deceptive practices
- Attempting to game the system
- Sharing others' private information

### Consequences

| Violation         | Possible Action             |
| ----------------- | --------------------------- |
| First offense     | Warning                     |
| Repeated minor    | Temporary restriction       |
| Serious violation | Account suspension          |
| Severe/illegal    | Permanent ban, legal action |

## Licensing Policies

### License Terms

- Licenses are non-transferable
- Terms are as specified in the license certificate
- Producers set their own pricing
- Platform facilitates but does not guarantee transactions

### Refund Policy

<Warning>
  Digital licenses are generally non-refundable.
</Warning>

Exceptions may include:

- Technical issues preventing download
- Duplicate purchases (same track, same license)
- Unauthorized transactions (fraud)

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of purchase.
