## Incident Communication

### How We Communicate

During incidents, we communicate through:

1. **Status Page** - Real-time updates
2. **In-App Banners** - For widespread issues
3. **Social Media** - Major announcements
4. **Email** - For affected users if needed

### Incident Levels

| Level | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Operational | All systems normal | - |
| Degraded | Reduced performance | Slow loading times |
| Partial Outage | Some features unavailable | Uploads disabled |
| Major Outage | Core functionality down | Site unreachable |

## Scheduled Maintenance

### When We Schedule Maintenance

- Non-urgent: Announced 48+ hours in advance
- Urgent: Announced as soon as possible
- Emergency: May occur without advance notice

### Maintenance Windows

Preferred maintenance times:
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM EST (off-peak hours)

---

## What to Do During Issues

### During Degraded Performance

- Wait and retry after a few minutes
- Check status page for updates
- Avoid repeated actions (can worsen issues)

### During Outages

- Check status page for estimated resolution
- Do not attempt to re-upload or re-process
- Wait for confirmation before resuming

<Warning>
  Avoid repeated upload or payment attempts during outages to prevent duplicates.
</Warning>

### Critical Actions to Avoid During Incidents

| Action | Why to Avoid |
|--------|--------------|
| **Retry purchases** | May result in duplicate charges |
| **Re-upload tracks** | May create duplicates; files may corrupt |
| **Change account settings** | Changes may not save correctly |
| **Request payouts** | Processing may fail or delay |
| **Start new checkouts** | Session may expire unexpectedly |

### Who to Contact

| Situation | Contact |
|-----------|---------|
| General status questions | Check status page first |
| Account-specific issue during outage | support@beatpass.ca |
| Urgent security concern | security@beatpass.ca |
| Payment charged but not received | support@beatpass.ca (after incident resolved) |

<Tip>
  During incidents, support response times may be longer than usual. For non-urgent issues, wait until the incident is resolved before contacting support.
</Tip>

## Incident Communication Cadence

### Update Frequency

| Incident Severity | Update Frequency |
|-------------------|------------------|
| Major outage | Every 15-30 minutes |
| Partial outage | Every 30-60 minutes |
| Degraded performance | Every 1-2 hours |
| Scheduled maintenance | Start, progress, completion |

### What Updates Include

- Current status
- Affected services
- Estimated time to resolution (when known)
- Workarounds (if available)
- Next update time

### Post-Incident

After resolution:

1. **All Clear** notice posted
2. **Post-mortem** published within 48 hours for major incidents
3. **Affected users** may receive direct communication
4. **Compensation** considered for extended outages affecting paid features
