## Audio Issues

### Audio Won't Play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Volume">
    Ensure device volume and player volume are not muted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh Page">
    A simple refresh often resolves playback issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Browser">
    Try a different browser. We recommend Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Clear browser cache and cookies, then reload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable Extensions">
    Ad blockers or privacy extensions may interfere with playback.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Audio Quality Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Choppy or buffering playback" icon="wifi">
    **Cause**: Slow or unstable internet connection.

    **Solutions**:

    - Test your connection speed at [fast.com](https://fast.com)
    - Move closer to your WiFi router
    - Switch to a wired connection if possible
    - Close other bandwidth-heavy applications
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Distorted or corrupted audio" icon="volume-xmark">
    **Cause**: Usually a source file issue or processing error.

    **Solutions**:

    - Refresh the page and try again
    - Try a different browser
    - If you're the producer: re-upload from the original source file
    - [Report to support](/support/contact) with the track URL
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Wrong audio playing" icon="shuffle">
    **Cause**: Browser cache serving outdated content.

    **Solutions**:

    - Hard refresh: `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
    - Clear browser cache completely
    - Try incognito/private browsing mode
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Upload Issues

### Upload Fails or Times Out

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check File Size">
    WAV: Max 100MB, MP3: Max 50MB. Compress or convert if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Check Format">
    Only WAV and FLAC (lossless formats) are accepted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Check Connection">
    Ensure stable internet. Wired connections are more reliable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Try Again">
    Wait a minute and retry. Server may have been temporarily busy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Upload Rejected

| Reason             | Solution                                  |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------- |
| Low quality        | Use 320kbps+ MP3 or lossless format       |
| Duplicate detected | Track fingerprint matches existing upload |
| Missing metadata   | Complete all required fields              |

## Account Issues

### Can't Log In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Credentials">
    Verify email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Use "Forgot Password" to reset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    Ensure your email is verified.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cookies">
    Delete site cookies and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Restricted

If your account is restricted:

1. Check email for notification with reason
2. Review platform policies
3. Contact support to appeal if you believe it's an error

## Payment Issues

### Payment Declined

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check Card Details">
    Verify card number, expiry, and CVV.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Check Balance">
    Ensure sufficient funds are available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contact Bank">
    Your bank may be blocking the transaction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Try Another Card">
    Use a different payment method.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Payout Not Received

| Timeframe        | Action                                   |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Less than 5 days | Wait, processing takes 2-5 business days |
| 5-10 days        | Check Stripe Connect status              |
| Over 10 days     | Contact support with details             |

## Display Issues

### Page Not Loading Correctly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh">
    Hard refresh: Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Clear browser cache completely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Incognito">
    Open in private/incognito window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Different Browser">
    Try Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Missing Content

| Issue              | Possible Cause   | Solution                    |
| ------------------ | ---------------- | --------------------------- |
| Tracks not showing | Processing delay | Wait 5-10 minutes           |
| Analytics empty    | New account      | Data appears after activity |
| Images broken      | Cache issue      | Clear cache and refresh     |

## Error Codes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="400 - Bad Request" icon="circle-xmark">
    **Meaning**: The server couldn't understand your request.

    **Common causes**:

    - Invalid characters in form fields
    - Incomplete required data
    - File format not supported

    **Fix**: Check your input and try again. Ensure all required fields are filled correctly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="401 - Not Authorized" icon="lock">
    **Meaning**: You need to be logged in.

    **Common causes**:

    - Session expired
    - Not logged in
    - Token refresh needed

    **Fix**: Log out, clear cookies, and log back in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="403 - Forbidden" icon="ban">
    **Meaning**: You don't have permission to access this.

    **Common causes**:

    - Trying to access another user's private content
    - Feature requires subscription upgrade
    - Account restrictions

    **Fix**: Check your account status and permissions. Contact support if you believe this is an error.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="404 - Not Found" icon="ghost">
    **Meaning**: The page or content doesn't exist.

    **Common causes**:

    - Content was deleted or removed
    - Broken or outdated link
    - Typo in URL

    **Fix**: Check the URL. If you followed a link, the content may have been removed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="500 - Server Error" icon="server">
    **Meaning**: Something went wrong on our end.

    **Common causes**:

    - Temporary server issues
    - Maintenance in progress
    - Unexpected bug

    **Fix**: Wait a minute and try again. Check [status.beatpass.ca](https://status.beatpass.ca) for known issues. Report if persistent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
