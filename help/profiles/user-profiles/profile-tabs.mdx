# Profile Tabs

Your user profile is organized into public tabs. Each tab exposes a different kind of listening or social activity.

## Available Tabs

### Liked Beats

The **Liked Beats** tab shows tracks you have liked.

- Use it to showcase your taste
- Visitors can open tracks directly from this list

<Tip>
Likes are both personal bookmarks and public signals. If you do not want a track attached to your public identity, remove the like.
</Tip>

### Public Playlists

The **Public Playlists** tab only shows playlists you marked public.

- Private playlists stay in your library
- Public playlists become part of your profile presence

### Reposts

The **Reposts** tab appears when reposts are enabled on the platform.

- It lists tracks or playlists you reposted
- It is public activity, not a private bookmark area

### Liked Collections

The **Liked Collections** tab shows collections you liked.

### Liked Producers

The **Liked Producers** tab shows producer profiles you liked.

This is different from the **Following** tab.

### Followers

The **Followers** tab lists the user accounts following your user profile.

### Following

The **Following** tab lists the user accounts you follow.

Use **Liked Producers** for producer pages you want to surface on your profile.

## Navigating Between Tabs

Tabs appear as pill-shaped buttons at the top of your profile content area. Simply click any tab to switch to that section.

On mobile devices, you may need to scroll horizontally to see all available tabs.

## Why Some Tabs May Be Missing or Empty

A tab can be empty because you have not used that feature yet. Some tabs can also be absent when the related feature is disabled or has no content to show.

<Note>
Private playlists are never shown on your profile. Only you can see them in your Library.
</Note>
