# Profile Tabs

Your user profile is organized into tabs. Each tab shows a different kind of activity.

## Available Tabs

### Liked Beats

The **Liked Beats** tab shows tracks you have liked.

- Visitors can open tracks directly from this list

<Tip>
Likes are both personal bookmarks and public signals. If you do not want a track associated with your public profile, remove the like.
</Tip>

### Public Playlists

The **Public Playlists** tab shows playlists associated with your account.

- Private playlists stay in your library and are not shown on your profile

### Reposts

The **Reposts** tab appears when reposts are enabled on the platform.

- It lists tracks and collections you reposted
- Reposts are public activity

### Liked Collections

The **Liked Collections** tab shows collections (albums) you liked.

### Liked Producers

The **Liked Producers** tab shows producer profiles you liked.

This is different from the **Following** tab, which tracks user accounts you follow.

### Followers

The **Followers** tab lists the user accounts following your profile.

### Following

The **Following** tab lists the user accounts you follow.

## Navigating Between Tabs

Tabs appear as pill-shaped buttons at the top of your profile content area. Click any tab to switch views.

On mobile, you may need to scroll horizontally to see all available tabs.

## Why a Tab May Be Empty

A tab can be empty if you have not used that feature yet. Some tabs may also be absent when the related feature is not enabled or has no content to display.

<Note>
Private playlists are never shown on your profile. Only you can see them in your Library.
</Note>
