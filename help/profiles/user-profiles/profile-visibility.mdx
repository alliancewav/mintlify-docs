# Profile Visibility

BeatPass user profiles are public by design. This page covers what is visible today and which parts you can actually control.

## What can appear on your user profile

Depending on what you have filled out and what content you have, visitors can see:

- Your avatar and username
- Your first and last name, if set
- Your city and country, if set
- Your description
- Your profile links
- Your followers and following lists
- Your liked beats
- Your liked collections
- Your liked producers
- Your public playlists
- Your reposts, when reposts are enabled on the platform

If a field is blank, it will not appear on your public profile.

## What you can control today

BeatPass does not currently expose a dedicated privacy panel for user profiles. The practical controls available in the product are:

- What information you fill into your profile editor
- Whether a playlist is public or private

### Hide information by removing it

If you do not want something shown publicly, remove it from your profile editor:

- Bio
- City
- Country
- External links
- Profile image

### Keep playlists off your public profile

When a playlist is private:

- It stays in your library
- It does not appear in **Public Playlists** on your profile

<Tip>
Use private playlists for work-in-progress curation or personal listening lists. Only make a playlist public when you want it attached to your public identity.
</Tip>

## What visitors can do from your profile

Visitors to your profile can:

- Follow you
- Open a message when messaging is available for that account state
- Browse your public tabs

<Note>
This page intentionally avoids documenting controls that are not present in the current UI, such as profile-hide toggles or block lists.
</Note>
