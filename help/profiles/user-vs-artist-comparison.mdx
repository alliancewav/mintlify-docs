# User vs Producer Profiles

BeatPass has two distinct profile types designed for different needs. Here's a clear breakdown of what each offers.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | User Profile | Producer Profile |
|---------|--------------|------------------|
| **Who it's for** | Listeners, beat collectors, music enthusiasts | Beat makers, producers, artists |
| **Upload beats** | No | Yes |
| **Sell licenses** | No | Yes |
| **Analytics dashboard** | No | Yes |
| **Verified badge** | Shows if producer profile is verified | Apply for verification |
| **Photo gallery** | No | Yes |
| **Custom cover image** | No | Yes |
| **Pinned track** | No | Yes |
| **Follow others** | Yes | Yes |
| **Like beats & playlists** | Yes | Yes |
| **Create playlists** | Yes | Yes |
| **Message users** | Yes | Yes |
| **Producer Profile button** | Shows if user has a producer profile | N/A (this is the producer profile) |

## User Profile Features

A **User Profile** is your personal space on BeatPass. When you sign up, this is what you get automatically.

### Connecting to Your Producer Profile

If you have a producer profile, your user profile displays a **Producer Profile** button. This button:

- Appears at the top of your user profile header
- Links directly to your producer profile page
- Helps visitors discover your music from your user profile

Additionally, if your producer profile is verified, the **verified badge** (blue checkmark) automatically appears on your user profile next to your name.

### What You Can Do

- **Follow producers** — Stay updated when your favorite producers drop new beats
- **Like beats** — Save beats you love to your "Liked Beats" collection
- **Like producers** — Keep track of producers whose style you enjoy
- **Create playlists** — Organize beats into custom playlists
- **Share playlists** — Make your playlists public for others to discover
- **Connect with others** — Message other users and producers directly
- **Build your network** — Gain followers who want to see what you're into

### Profile Sections

Your user profile displays several tabs that visitors can browse:

- **Liked Beats** — Beats you've liked
- **Public Playlists** — Playlists you've created and made public
- **Reposts** — Content you've shared with your followers
- **Liked Collections** — Albums you've liked
- **Liked Producers** — Producers you've liked
- **Followers** — People following you
- **Following** — People you follow

## Producer Profile Features

A **Producer Profile** is a full storefront for beat makers. You need to apply through the Producer Program to get one.

### What You Can Do

Everything a User Profile offers, plus:

- **Upload beats** — Share your music with the world
- **Sell licenses** — Earn money from your beats
- **Track analytics** — See plays, downloads, and earnings data
- **Showcase your work** — Display your discography prominently
- **Build your brand** — Add a cover image, photo gallery, and detailed bio
- **Get verified** — Apply for a verified badge to stand out
- **Pin your best track** — Feature one beat at the top of your profile
- **Earn achievements** — Unlock badges as you grow on the platform

### Profile Sections

Producer profiles display different tabs focused on your music:

- **Discography** — Your complete catalog of beats and albums
- **Beats** — All your individual tracks
- **Albums** — Beat collections you've released
- **Gallery** — Photos showcasing you, your studio, or your work
- **Followers** — People following your music
- **Similar Producers** — Other producers with a similar style
- **Achievements** — Badges and milestones you've earned

## How to Upgrade to a Producer Profile

If you're a beat maker ready to share your music, you can apply for a producer account:

1. Visit the **Producer Program** page on BeatPass
2. Click **Apply to Join** or **Become a Producer**
3. Complete the application form with your information
4. Wait for approval from the BeatPass team

<Note>
The Producer Program application helps verify that you're a legitimate producer. Once approved, you'll have full access to upload beats, set prices, and start earning.
</Note>

## Which Should I Choose?

**Choose User Profile if you:**
- Primarily listen to and collect beats
- Want to organize beats into playlists
- Don't plan to upload your own music

**Choose Producer Profile if you:**
- Create your own beats
- Want to build an audience for your music
- Plan to license or sell your beats
