# Verified Badge <Icon icon="circle-check" color="#449eff" />

The verified badge marks a producer profile that BeatPass has confirmed as authentic.

## Where It Appears

When a producer profile is verified, the badge appears:

- Next to your producer name on the producer profile header
- On producer listing cards when browsing producers
- On the linked user profile for that verified producer

## What Verification Means

The badge indicates:

- **Authentic identity** — BeatPass staff has verified the profile as the real producer or brand
- **Community participation** — The producer is active in the BeatPass Discord community
- **Account trust** — Visitors can distinguish the page from impersonation or confusion

<Note>
Verification is about authenticity and community involvement, not popularity. It is not a ranking badge.
</Note>

## How to Get Verified

Verification happens through a combination of the BeatPass app and Discord community participation. You must already have an approved producer profile to begin the verification process.

### The Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Producer Program page">
    Go to [https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-program](https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-program) and click **Apply to Join** (if logged in) or **Apply Now** (if not logged in).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Get Verified">
    On the request selector page at [https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests](https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests), select **Get Verified** from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Discord community">
    The verification page at [https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests/verify-artist](https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests/verify-artist) shows **Discord Community Required** and a **Join Discord Community** button. Click this button to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participate in Discord">
    Join the BeatPass Discord server at [https://discord.com/invite/N257QBkSeg](https://discord.com/invite/N257QBkSeg). Use a Discord name that matches your BeatPass producer profile name when possible—this helps staff identify you faster.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for staff verification">
    BeatPass staff will identify active producers in Discord, grant you a BeatPass producer Discord role, and manually mark your profile as verified. The blue check appears shortly after staff completes this process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Typical Timeline

- **Fast path**: If your Discord name matches your producer profile and staff recognize you, verification can happen within hours
- **Standard path**: Most verifications complete within a few days
- **Extended path**: If staff cannot match your Discord account to your producer profile, it may take up to 7-10 business days. If you haven't received verification after this period, contact support at [https://open.beatpass.ca/contact](https://open.beatpass.ca/contact) with your producer profile link and Discord username

<Tip>
**Speed up verification**: Use the same name on Discord as your BeatPass producer profile. A complete producer profile with bio, links, imagery, and public catalog also makes manual review easier.
</Tip>

<Note>
There is no in-app submission form for verification. The **Get Verified** flow is a handoff to Discord, where staff manually verify producers through community participation.
</Note>

## Verified-Only Features

Once verified, you gain access to exclusive platform capabilities:

- **Audio Recon** — Advanced audio analysis and beat matching tools
- **Platform notifications** — New Subscribers, Analytics Reports, and Payout Updates
- **Notification groups** — Join the Audio Recon and Producer Dashboard notification channels
- **Beat request notifications** — Automatically receive notifications when new beat requests match your style

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I lose my verified badge?

Yes. BeatPass can revoke verification if the account no longer meets platform requirements, authenticity standards, or community guidelines.

### Does verification cost anything?

No. Verification is free and handled through Discord community participation.

### Do I need a producer profile first?

Yes. The **Get Verified** option only appears if you already have a producer profile. If you don't have one yet, choose **Become a Producer** instead.

## Verification vs Subscription

Verification and paid plans serve different purposes:

| Feature | Verification | Subscription |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Purpose** | Identity confirmation and community standing | Paid platform benefits |
| **How you get it** | Discord community participation | Billing page signup |
| **Cost** | Free | Monthly fee |
| **Visual result** | Blue verified badge | Plan status on account |
| **Main benefits** | Audio Recon, exclusive notifications | Varies by plan tier |

<Note>
You do not need a paid plan to request verification, and verification does not replace subscription features. They work independently.
</Note>
