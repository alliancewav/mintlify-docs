# Bio & Links

Your biography, location, and links shape the public "about" experience on your producer profile.

## Writing Your Biography

Your producer bio appears inside the public profile's about content.

### What to Include

- **Your background** — How you got into making beats
- **Your style** — What makes your sound unique
- **Your influences** — Artists and genres that inspire you
- **Your achievements** — Notable placements, collaborations, or milestones
- **What you're working on** — Current projects or upcoming releases

### How to Add Your Bio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your producer profile">
    Navigate to your producer profile page on BeatPass.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the editor">
    Click the **More** button on your profile, then select **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Biography tab">
    Open **Biography** in the producer editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your public details">
    Add or edit your description, city, and country.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Save the profile to publish the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Keep your bio engaging but concise. Visitors should be able to quickly understand who you are and what you're about. Break up long text into paragraphs for easier reading.
</Tip>

### Bio Tips

- **Be authentic** — Write in your own voice
- **Stay professional** — Remember this represents your brand
- **Update regularly** — Refresh your bio as your career evolves
- **Avoid links in bio** — Use the Links section instead (links in the description are not clickable)

<Warning>
URLs and links are not allowed in the Description field. They won't be clickable. Use the dedicated Links section to add your social profiles and website.
</Warning>

## Adding Social Links

Profile links appear in the public connect area of your producer page.

### How links work

BeatPass supports general web links, including:

- Instagram
- Twitter / X
- YouTube
- SoundCloud
- Spotify
- TikTok
- Your personal website
- Linktree or similar link aggregators

### How to Add Links

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the producer editor">
    Go to **More -> Edit** on your producer profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Links tab">
    Open **Links** in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Links">
    Add the full URL for each destination you want to publish.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Save the profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Where they appear

Your biography and links support the public profile areas below:

| Element | Location |
| --- | --- |
| **Bio text** | About content in the profile view |
| **Location** | About content when city or country is set |
| **Connect links** | Public link row on the profile |
| **Genres** | Separate profile metadata set from the **Details** tab |

## Best Practices

### For Your Bio

- Keep the first few sentences compelling—they appear before any "Show more" link
- Mention your main genres and style early
- Include any notable accomplishments
- Update it when you have news to share

### For Your Links

- Prioritize active social accounts
- Remove links to inactive profiles
- Keep links current—broken links look unprofessional
- Consider what platforms your target audience uses most

<Note>
Use full URLs so BeatPass can render the links correctly.
</Note>
