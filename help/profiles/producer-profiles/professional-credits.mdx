# Professional Credits

Professional Credits let you publish Spotify-linked work you contributed to outside your BeatPass catalog.

## What Credits Are For

Credits are meant for verified outside work such as:

- Production
- Engineering
- Writing
- Performance
- Other credited creative roles

## Adding Your First Credit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your producer editor">
    Go to **More -> Edit** on your producer profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Credits tab">
    Use **Credits** in the producer editor. This tab is available for existing producer profiles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new credit">
    Use the add-credit flow to begin searching Spotify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Spotify">
    Search for the song or album you want to attach to your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your roles">
    Choose one or more roles that match your contribution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the credit">
    Confirm the entry and save it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding Album Credits

Album credits let you apply roles across a project and then fine-tune them per track.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for the album">
    Switch to album search and find the project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply broad roles">
    Apply the roles that fit most tracks on the release.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize track-by-track when needed">
    Adjust roles on individual tracks when your contributions vary within the album.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the album credits">
    Confirm the final album credit set.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where Credits Appear Publicly

Professional Credits are displayed inside the public **Discography** view, not as a separate top-level producer tab.

Visitors can:

- See credited songs and albums
- Filter by role when filters are available
- Open the underlying release in Spotify

## Credit Hygiene

Only add credits you can stand behind publicly:

- Use accurate roles
- Prefer official Spotify releases
- Remove entries that no longer reflect your brand
- Keep the list selective so it strengthens the profile instead of diluting it

<Note>
Credits work best as proof of professional history. They should complement your BeatPass catalog, not repeat it.
</Note>
