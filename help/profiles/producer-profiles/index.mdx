# Producer Profiles

Your producer profile is BeatPass's public artist page for approved producers. It is where listeners browse your catalog, view your brand assets, and decide whether to follow or license your work.

## What makes it different from a user profile

Producer profiles are built around your music and public identity:

- A full **Discography** tab
- Dedicated **Beats** and **Albums** tabs
- A cover image, avatar, genres, biography, and external links
- Optional gallery photos
- Professional credits
- A pinned featured track
- Producer-only editing and dashboard tools

## Profile Sections

Your producer profile includes these tabs:

| Tab | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Discography** | Your pinned track, popular and recent beats, albums, about section, and professional credits |
| **Similar artists** | Related producer pages when BeatPass has matching data |
| **Gallery** | Your uploaded gallery images |
| **Beats** | Your track catalog |
| **Albums** | Your collections |
| **Followers** | People following this producer profile |
| **Achievements** | Producer achievements for the owner or admins |

## Profile Header

At the top of your producer profile, visitors see:

- Your cover image
- Your avatar
- Your producer name
- A verified badge when applicable
- Visitor actions such as **Follow** and **Message** when available

If you own the profile, the edit entry point is in **More -> Edit**.

## Editing Your Producer Profile

To edit your producer profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your producer profile">
    Navigate to your producer profile page on BeatPass.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the More menu">
    Click the **More** button on your profile header, then select **Edit** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the editor tabs">
    The current editor includes **Details**, **Links**, **Biography**, **Images**, and **Credits** for existing profiles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Save the profile after you finish updating the relevant sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the discography view includes

The **Discography** tab is more than a track grid. It can include:

- Your pinned track
- Popular and recent beats
- Albums
- About information
- Professional credits

<Tip>
A complete producer profile helps visitors understand both your sound and your credibility. Update your profile editor as your catalog and credits change.
</Tip>
