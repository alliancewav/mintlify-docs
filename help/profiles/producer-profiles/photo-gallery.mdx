# Photo Gallery

Your gallery adds visual context to your producer profile. Use it for brand photos, studio shots, performances, or other producer-facing imagery.

## Why Add Photos?

Photos make your profile feel complete and credible:

- **Build trust** with listeners and buyers
- **Show your setup** and creative environment
- **Give your profile personality**
- **Support your brand** beyond cover art alone

## Adding Photos

You can add photos from two places:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the public Gallery tab">
    On your own producer profile, open **Gallery** and use **Add Photo** when it is available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Or use the producer editor">
    Navigate to your producer profile page on BeatPass.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the editor">
    Click the **More** button on your profile, then select **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage images">
    Open **Images** in the producer editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add images and save">
    Upload one or more gallery images, then save the profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Photo Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Formats** | JPEG, JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP |
| **Max file size** | 2MB per image |

## How the Gallery Behaves

- The **Gallery** tab is part of the producer profile when the gallery feature is enabled on the site
- If you have not uploaded anything yet, BeatPass shows an empty state instead of hiding the workflow from you
- Recent gallery images can also be previewed in profile about content

## What to Share

Good gallery candidates include:

- Headshots
- Studio photos
- Performance or session photos
- Behind-the-scenes material
- Visuals that fit your producer brand

<Warning>
All photos must follow BeatPass community guidelines. Inappropriate images will be removed and may result in account action.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

- **Quality over quantity** — A few strong images beat a cluttered gallery
- **Stay current** — Replace outdated shots over time
- **Be consistent** — Match the aesthetic of your profile and catalog
- **Keep it relevant** — Use visuals that support your music identity

<Tip>
Your gallery is often one of the first things visitors explore after hearing your beats. Treat it as part of your producer branding, not as a dumping ground for random images.
</Tip>
