# Photo Gallery

Your gallery adds visual context to your producer profile. Use it for brand photos, studio shots, performances, or other producer-facing imagery.

## Adding Photos

You can add photos from two places:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the public Gallery tab">
    On your own producer profile, open the **Gallery** tab and use the **Add Photo** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Or use the producer editor">
    On your producer profile, click **More**, then select **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Images">
    In the editor, go to the **Images** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and save">
    Add one or more gallery images, then save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Photo Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Formats** | JPEG, JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP |
| **Max file size** | 2 MB per image |

## How the Gallery Works

- The **Gallery** tab appears on your producer profile when the gallery feature is enabled
- If you have not uploaded any images yet, visitors see an empty state rather than a hidden tab
- Gallery images are displayed on the Gallery tab itself

## What to Share

Good gallery candidates include:

- Headshots and portraits
- Studio photos
- Performance or session photos
- Behind-the-scenes material
- Visuals that fit your producer brand

<Warning>
All photos must follow BeatPass community guidelines. Inappropriate images may be removed and could result in account action.
</Warning>

## Tips

- **Quality over quantity** — A few strong images beat a cluttered gallery
- **Stay current** — Replace outdated shots over time
- **Be consistent** — Match the aesthetic of your profile and catalog
- **Keep it relevant** — Use visuals that support your music identity
