# Your Profile on BeatPass

BeatPass has two profile layers:

- A **user profile** for every account
- A **producer profile** for approved producers with a public artist page

## Profile Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User Profile" icon="user" href="/help/profiles/user-profiles">
    Your personal public account page for likes, playlists, reposts, followers, and profile details.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Producer Profile" icon="music" href="/help/profiles/producer-profiles">
    Your public producer page for catalog, credits, gallery, branding, and follower growth.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Which Profile Do I Have?

When you create an account, BeatPass gives you a **user profile** automatically. That profile lets you:

- Follow your favorite producers
- Like and save beats
- Create and share playlists
- Message other users
- See who follows you

If you want a **producer profile**, you need to use the Producer Program flow. That is the route for uploading tracks, managing your public producer page, and using producer-only tools.

<Tip>
Not sure which page does what? Use the [user vs producer comparison](/help/profiles/user-vs-producer-comparison).
</Tip>
