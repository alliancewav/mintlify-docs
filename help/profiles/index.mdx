# Your Profile on BeatPass

BeatPass has two profile types:

- A **user profile** for every account
- A **producer profile** for approved producers with a public artist page

## Profile Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User Profile" icon="user" href="/help/profiles/user-profiles">
    Your public account page showing your avatar, username, likes, playlists, reposts, and followers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Producer Profile" icon="music" href="/help/profiles/producer-profiles">
    Your public artist page for your catalog, credits, gallery, branding, and follower growth.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Which Profile Do I Have?

Every BeatPass account gets a **user profile** automatically. Your user profile lets you:

- Follow your favourite producers
- Like and save beats
- Create and share playlists
- Message other users
- See who follows you

If you want a **producer profile**, apply through the Producer Program. That gives you access to upload tracks, manage a public artist page, and use producer-only tools like analytics and finances.

<Tip>
Not sure which page does what? See the [user vs producer comparison](/help/profiles/user-vs-producer-comparison).
</Tip>
