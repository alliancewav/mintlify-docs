BeatPass organizes notifications into categories based on what they're about. Some notifications are available to everyone, while others are specifically for producers or verified producers.

---

## Following

Notifications about producers you follow.

| Notification | What It Means | Who Gets It |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| **New Uploads** | A producer you follow released new beats | Everyone |

<Note>
  Follow your favorite producers to get notified when they release new music!
</Note>

---

## Social

Notifications about your social activity.

| Notification | What It Means | Who Gets It |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| **New Follower** | Someone started following your profile | Everyone |

---

## Messages

Notifications about direct communication.

| Notification | What It Means | Who Gets It |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Direct Messages** | Someone sent you a private message | Everyone |
| **Playlist Invites** | You've been invited to collaborate on a playlist | Everyone |

<Info>
  Direct messages are private conversations between you and another user.
</Info>

---

## Beat Requests

Notifications about custom beat requests.

| Notification | What It Means | Who Gets It |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Beat Submissions** | A producer submitted a beat to your request | Everyone |
| **Matching Requests** | A new beat request matches your production style | Producers only |

---

## Promotions

Marketing and promotional communications.

| Notification | What It Means | Who Gets It |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Special Offers** | Exclusive discounts, deals, and promotional content | Everyone |

<Note>
  You can turn off promotional emails if you don't want marketing communications.
</Note>

---

## Producer Activity

Notifications about engagement with your beats. **Available to producers only.**

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|--------------|
| **Track Likes** | Someone liked one of your beats |
| **Comments & Replies** | Someone commented on your beat or replied to your comment |
| **Reposts** | Someone shared your beat to their followers |

These notifications help you understand how your audience is engaging with your music.

---

## Earnings & Licensing

Notifications about your sales and downloads. **Available to producers only.**

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|--------------|
| **License Granted** | Someone was granted a license for your beat (exclusive or non-exclusive) |
| **Track Downloads** | A subscriber downloaded your beat |
| **Play Milestones** | Your beats reach play count milestones (100, 1K, 10K, etc.) |

<Info>
  License Granted notifications include both subscription downloads (non-exclusive licenses) and exclusive purchases.
</Info>

---

## Achievements

Notifications about producer milestones. **Available to producers only.**

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|--------------|
| **Achievement Unlocked** | You earned a new producer achievement or badge |

Achievements recognize your milestones and contributions to the platform.

---

## Producer Dashboard

Advanced notifications for verified producers. **Available to verified producers only.**

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|--------------|
| **New Subscribers** | A new user subscribed to BeatPass |
| **Analytics Reports** | Your weekly or monthly performance summary is ready |
| **Payout Updates** | Information about your earnings payout (processed or needs attention) |

<Warning>
  These notifications appear as a locked preview for regular producers. Apply for verification to unlock them.
</Warning>

## System Notifications

Some notifications are sent automatically and cannot be turned off:

- **Password Reset** — When you request to reset your password
- **Security Alerts** — Unusual account activity warnings
- **Account Changes** — Email or password change confirmations
- **Important Updates** — Critical platform announcements

These are always sent via email to keep your account secure.

---

## Customizing Your Notifications

For each notification type, you can choose which channels to enable:

- **Email** — Receive notifications in your email inbox
- **Browser** — Real-time browser pop-ups (requires permission)
- **In-App** — Show in your notification center (bell icon)

### Recommended Settings by Priority

| Priority | Recommended Channels |
|----------|---------------------|
| **High** (licenses, payouts) | Email + Browser + In-App |
| **Medium** (followers, likes) | Email + In-App |
| **Low** (promotional) | In-App only |
| **Not needed** | All off |
