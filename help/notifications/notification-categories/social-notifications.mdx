Social notifications keep you connected with the BeatPass community. Know when someone follows you, sends you a message, or invites you to collaborate.

---

## What's Included

### Social

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **New Follower** | Someone started following your profile |

### Messages

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **Direct Messages** | Someone sent you a private message |
| **Playlist Invites** | You've been invited to collaborate on a playlist |

### Beat Requests

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **Beat Submissions** | A producer submitted a beat to your request |

---

## Who Gets These

All BeatPass users can receive social notifications. These help you stay connected with the community.

---

## Recommended Settings

| Notification Type | Recommended Channels |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| **New Follower** | In-App only (high volume) |
| **Direct Messages** | Email + Browser + In-App |
| **Playlist Invites** | Email + In-App |
| **Beat Submissions** | Email + In-App |

<Tip>
  Enable all channels for Direct Messages if you want to respond quickly. Messages from potential collaborators are time-sensitive.
</Tip>

---

## Tips

- **Enable messages on all channels** — Don't miss important communication
- **Consider In-App only for followers** — If you have many followers, this reduces email volume
- **Respond to invites promptly** — Playlist collaborations work best with active participants
