The **Messages** category currently contains:

| Notification | Who can receive it | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Direct Messages** | Everyone | Someone sends you a direct or group message |
| **Playlist Invites** | Everyone | Someone invites you to collaborate on a playlist |

Playlist invite notifications can include inline **Accept** and **Decline** actions in the in-app notification list.
