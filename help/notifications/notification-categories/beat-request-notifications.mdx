The **Beat Requests** category mixes request-owner updates with producer opportunity alerts.

| Notification | Who can receive it | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Beat Submissions** | Everyone | A producer submits a beat to your request |
| **No Submissions Received** | Everyone | Your request closes without any submissions |
| **Matching Requests** | Producers | A beat request matches your style |
| **Request Expiring** | Producers | A beat request you may want to answer is about to expire |

---

## How To Read This Category

- If you create requests, the owner-facing notifications are **Beat Submissions** and **No Submissions Received**
- If you are a producer looking for opportunities, the producer-facing notifications are **Matching Requests** and **Request Expiring**
