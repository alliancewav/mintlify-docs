BeatPass organizes notifications into categories based on what they're about. This makes it easy to manage which updates you want to receive.

---

## Categories for Everyone

These notification categories are available to all BeatPass users:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Following" icon="heart" href="/help/notifications/notification-categories/following-notifications">
    Get notified when producers you follow release new beats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social" icon="users" href="/help/notifications/notification-categories/social-notifications">
    Know when someone follows you or invites you to collaborate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Categories for Producers

These categories are available only to users with producer accounts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Producer Activity" icon="chart-line" href="/help/notifications/notification-categories/producer-notifications">
    Track likes, comments, and reposts on your beats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Earnings & Licensing" icon="dollar-sign" href="/help/notifications/notification-categories/earnings-notifications">
    Stay informed about licenses, downloads, and milestones.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
