Producer Activity notifications help you understand how people are engaging with your music. Track likes, comments, reposts, and discover when beat requests match your style.

<Note>
  These notifications are only available to users with producer accounts.
</Note>

---

## What's Included

### Producer Activity

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **Track Likes** | Someone liked one of your beats |
| **Comments & Replies** | Someone commented on your beat or replied to your comment |
| **Reposts** | Someone shared your beat to their followers |

### Beat Requests

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **Matching Requests** | A new beat request matches your production style |

### Achievements

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **Achievement Unlocked** | You earned a new producer achievement or badge |

---

## Who Gets These

Only users with producer accounts can receive these notifications. If you see these categories locked in your settings, you need to become a producer to unlock them.

---

## Recommended Settings

| Notification Type | Recommended Channels |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Track Likes** | In-App only (high volume) |
| **Comments & Replies** | Email + In-App |
| **Reposts** | In-App only |
| **Matching Requests** | Email + Browser + In-App |
| **Achievement Unlocked** | In-App only |

<Tip>
  Enable all channels for Matching Requests — these are opportunities to submit your beats to potential buyers.
</Tip>

---

## Understanding Your Engagement

Producer Activity notifications give you insights into:
- **What's popular** — Which beats are getting the most attention
- **Community feedback** — What people are saying about your music
- **Growth indicators** — Reposts help spread your music to new listeners

---

## Tips

- **Respond to comments** — Engage with your audience to build relationships
- **Check matching requests quickly** — Submitting early gives you an advantage
- **Use In-App for high-volume notifications** — Likes can add up quickly for popular beats
