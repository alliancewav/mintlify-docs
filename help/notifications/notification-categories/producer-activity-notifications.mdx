The **Producer Activity** category is available to producer accounts and covers engagement on your beats.

| Notification | Who can receive it | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Track Likes** | Producers | Someone likes your beat |
| **Comments & Replies** | Producers | Someone comments on your beat or replies in that thread |
| **Reposts** | Producers | Someone reposts your beat |
