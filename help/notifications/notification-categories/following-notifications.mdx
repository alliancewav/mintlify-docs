Following notifications keep you updated when producers you follow release new music. Never miss a beat from your favorite artists.

---

## What's Included

| Notification | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **New Uploads** | A producer you follow uploaded new beats |

---

## Who Gets These

Everyone on BeatPass can receive Following notifications. You just need to follow at least one producer.

---

## How to Get These Notifications

1. **Follow producers** — Click the Follow button on any producer's profile
2. **Enable notifications** — Go to `/notifications/settings` and enable the channels you want for "New Uploads"

---

## Recommended Settings

| Priority | Recommended Channels |
|----------|---------------------|
| **For casual listeners** | In-App only |
| **For active fans** | Email + In-App |
| **For instant updates** | Browser + In-App |

---

## Tips

- **Follow your favorite producers** — The more you follow, the more new music you'll discover
- **Use In-App to reduce email volume** — Check the bell icon to see new releases
- **Enable Browser for real-time alerts** — Get notified the moment new music drops
