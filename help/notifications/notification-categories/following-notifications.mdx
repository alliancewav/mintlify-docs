The **Following** category currently contains a single notification type:

| Notification | Who can receive it | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **New Uploads** | Everyone | A producer you follow uploads new beats |

To receive this notification, you need to follow the producer first.

---

## Recommended Use

- Enable **In-App** if you want low-friction update tracking
- Add **Email** or **Browser** if you want faster release alerts from producers you follow closely
