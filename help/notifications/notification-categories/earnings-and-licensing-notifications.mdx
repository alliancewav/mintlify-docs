The **Earnings & Licensing** category is available to producer accounts.

| Notification | Who can receive it | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **License Granted** | Producers | Someone is granted a license for your beat |
| **Track Downloads** | Producers | A subscriber downloads your beat |
| **Play Milestones** | Producers | Your beat reaches a configured play milestone |
