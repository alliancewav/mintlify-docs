The Notification Settings page at `/notifications/settings` controls which notification types you receive and which channels deliver them.

---

## Accessing Notification Settings

You can open notification settings from:

- the bell menu gear icon
- the full notifications page
- the direct route: `/notifications/settings`

---

## What The Settings Page Shows

### Role badge

At the top of the page, BeatPass labels your current account context as:

- **Member**
- **Producer**
- **Verified Producer**

### Bulk controls

The page includes bulk toggles for:

- **Enable All**
- **Disable All**
- all **Email** toggles
- all **Browser** toggles
- all **In-App** toggles

### Individual groups

Each category is shown as a grouped set of notification types, with one toggle per channel.

---

## Locked and Hidden Groups

Current role behavior in the UI:

- **Members** do not see producer-only groups
- **Producers** can see verified-only groups as locked previews
- **Verified producers** can configure the full set

If browser notifications are blocked by your browser, the page also shows a warning banner at the top.

---

## Saving Changes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Adjust the channels you want">
    Toggle channels at the group level or notification level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review browser permission if needed">
    If you enable browser notifications, your browser may request permission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Preferences">
    Click **Save Preferences** to persist the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
