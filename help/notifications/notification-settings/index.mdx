The **Notification Preferences** page controls which notification types you receive and which channels deliver them.

---

## Accessing Notification Settings

You can open notification settings from:

- the gear icon in the bell menu
- the full notifications page
- a direct link in notification emails

---

## What The Settings Page Shows

### Role badge

At the top of the page, BeatPass labels your current account context as:

- **Member**
- **Producer**
- **Verified Producer**

### Bulk controls

The page includes bulk toggles for:

- **Enable All**
- **Disable All**
- all **Email** toggles
- all **Push** toggles
- all **In-App** toggles

### Individual groups

Each category is shown as a grouped set of notification types, with one toggle per channel.

---

## Locked and Hidden Groups

Current role behavior in the UI:

- **Members** do not see producer-only groups
- **Producers** can see verified-only groups as locked previews
- **Verified producers** can configure the full set

If push notifications are blocked by your browser, the page also shows a warning banner at the top.

---

## Saving Changes

Changes are saved automatically as you toggle channels — there is no separate save button. If you enable **Push** notifications for the first time, your browser may request permission.
