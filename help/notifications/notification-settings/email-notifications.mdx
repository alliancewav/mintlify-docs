Email notifications send BeatPass updates to the email address on your account. This channel is controlled per notification type from `/notifications/settings`.

---

## How Email Preferences Work

- Turn **Email** on or off per notification type
- Use bulk email toggles at the top of the settings page when needed
- Save the changes with **Save Preferences**

---

## Unsubscribe Links

BeatPass notification emails include unsubscribe actions for notification email categories:

- **Unsubscribe from this type**
- **Unsubscribe from all**
- **Manage preferences**

These links map back to the notification preference system and are designed to work without first opening the settings page manually.

---

## System Emails

Some emails are not controlled by notification preferences, including security and account-management emails such as password resets.

<Note>
  Disabling email notifications does not disable account-security emails or other required account communications.
</Note>

---

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm not getting email notifications">
    Confirm that the notification type's **Email** toggle is enabled and check your spam or junk folder.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I unsubscribed and want emails again">
    Re-enable the relevant **Email** toggles in `/notifications/settings`, then save your preferences.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
