Never miss a beat! BeatPass keeps you informed about new music, messages, followers, and more. You're in complete control of what notifications you receive and how you receive them.

<Info>
  You can customize your notification preferences anytime at `/notifications/settings`.
</Info>

---

## How Notifications Work

BeatPass sends you updates through three different channels:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope">
    Notifications delivered to your email inbox. Great for important updates you don't want to miss.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Browser" icon="bell">
    Real-time pop-ups in your browser. Perfect for instant alerts when you're online.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In-App" icon="inbox">
    Collected in your notification center (the bell icon). Check them anytime you're on BeatPass.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What You'll Be Notified About

Depending on your account type, you'll see different notification categories:

### Everyone Gets

- **New Uploads** — When producers you follow release new beats
- **New Follower** — When someone follows your profile
- **Direct Messages** — When you receive a private message
- **Playlist Invites** — When you're invited to collaborate on a playlist
- **Beat Submissions** — When a producer submits a beat to your request
- **Special Offers** — Promotions and exclusive deals

### Producers Also Get

- **Matching Requests** — When a new beat request matches your style
- **Track Likes** — When someone likes your beats
- **Comments & Replies** — When someone comments on your beats or replies
- **Reposts** — When someone shares your beats
- **License Granted** — When someone gets a license for your beat
- **Track Downloads** — When subscribers download your beats
- **Play Milestones** — When your beats hit 100, 1K, 10K plays, and more
- **Achievement Unlocked** — When you earn producer achievements

### Verified Producers Also Get

- **New Subscribers** — When new users subscribe to the platform
- **Analytics Reports** — Weekly and monthly performance summaries
- **Payout Updates** — When payments are processed or need attention

## Managing Your Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notification Settings">
    Navigate to `/notifications/settings` directly, or click the **settings icon** (gear) in the notification dropdown.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Categories">
    You'll see notification categories organized by type. Some may be locked if they're for producers only.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Channels">
    For each notification type, toggle on/off which channels you want:
    - **Email** — On/Off
    - **Browser** — On/Off
    - **In-App** — On/Off
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Quick Controls">
    Use **Enable All** or **Disable All** buttons at the top to quickly adjust all notifications at once. You can also toggle entire channels (all Email, all Browser, or all In-App).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Preferences** to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browser Notifications

To receive real-time browser notifications:

1. When you enable browser notifications in your settings, your browser will ask for permission
2. Click **Allow** when prompted
3. If you accidentally blocked them, you'll need to enable them in your browser settings

<Warning>
  If browser notifications are blocked by your browser, you'll see a warning message on your notification settings page. You'll need to manually enable them in your browser settings to receive real-time alerts.
</Warning>

## Tips

- **Don't want marketing emails?** Turn off "Special Offers" in the Promotions category
- **Too many emails?** Keep Email off but In-App on — check notifications when convenient
- **Missing important updates?** Make sure Email is enabled for categories you care about
- **Want instant alerts?** Enable Browser notifications for real-time pop-ups
