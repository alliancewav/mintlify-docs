BeatPass notifications can appear in three delivery channels and are organized by account role. Members get core account notifications, producers unlock creator-specific alerts, and verified producers unlock additional advanced categories.

---

## Delivery Channels

| Channel | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Email** | Sends notification emails to the address on your BeatPass account |
| **Browser** | Sends browser push alerts on supported browsers after permission is granted |
| **In-App** | Shows notifications in the bell menu and the `/notifications` page |

---

## Where Notifications Appear

| Surface | What it is for |
| --- | --- |
| **Bell icon** | Quick view of recent notifications and unread count |
| **`/notifications`** | Full notification list page |
| **`/notifications/settings`** | Notification preferences and channel toggles |

---

## Role-Based Access

| Account role | Categories available |
| --- | --- |
| **Member** | Following, Social, Messages, Beat Requests, Promotions |
| **Producer** | Everything above plus Producer Activity, Earnings & Licensing, Achievements |
| **Verified Producer** | Everything above plus Audio Recon and Producer Dashboard |

<Note>
  In the current settings UI, member accounts do not see artist-only groups, and non-verified producers can see verified-only groups as locked previews.
</Note>
