## Welcome, Curators

Curators play a vital role in the BeatPass ecosystem by discovering great beats and helping them find their audience.

<Info>
  **Time to complete:** 10-15 minutes for basic setup
</Info>

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin:

- Active BeatPass account with verified email
- Passion for discovering and organizing music
- Time to consistently update and maintain playlists

---

## Step-by-Step Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Your Curator Profile">
    Establish your identity:

    - Upload a recognizable profile photo
    - Write a bio about your taste and curation focus
    - Indicate the genres and styles you specialize in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Catalog">
    Find hidden gems:

    - Browse beyond the trending section
    - Check new uploads regularly
    - Follow emerging producers before they blow up
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Public Playlist">
    Start building your reputation:

    1. Navigate to your Library
    2. Create a new playlist
    3. Set visibility to "Public"
    4. Add 10-20 carefully selected beats
    5. Write a compelling description
    6. Add relevant tags
  </Step>
  <Step title="Develop a Curation Strategy">
    Stand out from other curators:

    - Choose a niche (genre, mood, use case)
    - Maintain consistent quality standards
    - Update regularly with fresh content
    - Build themed playlists and highlight related albums within those playlists
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Your Following">
    Grow your audience:

    - Share playlists on social media
    - Engage with artists and producers
    - Collaborate with other curators
    - Cross-promote your discoveries
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply for Curator Badge (Optional)">
    Unlock additional features:

    - Meet minimum follower thresholds
    - Maintain active playlist updates
    - Demonstrate consistent engagement
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Playlist Best Practices

### Playlist Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mood Playlists">
    Group beats by emotional tone:

    - "Late Night Vibes"
    - "High Energy Workout"
    - "Chill Study Session"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Genre Playlists">
    Focus on specific styles:

    - "Underground Drill"
    - "Lo-Fi Essentials"
    - "Trap Soul Selections"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Discovery Playlists">
    Highlight new talent:

    - "Rising Producers This Week"
    - "Fresh Uploads Worth Hearing"
    - "Hidden Gems Under 100 Plays"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Use Case Playlists">
    Serve specific needs:

    - "Beats for Freestyles"
    - "Perfect for Hooks"
    - "Commercial Ready"
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Quality Standards

- **Cohesion**: Every beat should fit the playlist theme
- **Length**: 15-30 beats is ideal for most playlists
- **Flow**: Order tracks for a smooth listening experience
- **Freshness**: Update regularly to keep followers engaged
- **Description**: Explain what makes this collection special

## Growing Your Audience

### Engagement Strategies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Consistency">
    Update at least weekly. Followers expect fresh content.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Niche Focus">
    Specialists attract dedicated audiences. General curators get lost in the crowd.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Building">
    Comment on tracks, follow producers, engage with other curators.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cross-Promotion">
    Share on Instagram, Twitter, TikTok with track previews.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Curator Metrics

Track your impact:

| Metric               | What It Measures         |
| -------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Playlist Followers   | Audience size            |
| Playlist Plays       | Engagement volume        |
| Saves from Playlists | Conversion effectiveness |
| Follower Growth      | Momentum                 |

## Curator Badge Benefits

Meeting curator thresholds unlocks:

- Featured placement in curator directory
- Priority in playlist recommendations
- Curator-only notifications on new releases
- Potential future monetization features

<Note>
  Badge requirements and benefits may evolve as the platform grows.
</Note>
