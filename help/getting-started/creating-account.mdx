Creating a BeatPass account is free and only takes a minute. Once you're signed up, you can start exploring beats, creating playlists, and engaging with the community.

## How to Sign Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the registration page">
    Click [**Sign up**](https://open.beatpass.ca/register) in the top navigation bar, or visit the registration page directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in the registration form:

    - **Email address** — Use an email you have access to (you'll need to verify it)
    - **Password** — Create a secure password
    - **Confirm password** — Re-enter your password to confirm

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/assets/images/image-1.png"
        alt="Image"
        title="Image"
        style={{ width:"100%" }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the terms">
    Check the box to confirm you agree to the [Terms of Service](https://open.beatpass.ca/pages/terms-of-service) and [Privacy Policy](https://open.beatpass.ca/pages/privacy-policy). You can click the links to read them before agreeing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create account">
    Click the **Create account** button to submit your registration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from BeatPass. Click the link in the email to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email Verification

After signing up, you'll need to verify your email address before you can fully use your account.

### What to Expect

- A verification email will be sent to the address you provided
- The link in the email expires after **24 hours**
- Until you verify, you'll see a reminder when you visit BeatPass

### Didn't Receive the Email?

If you don't see the verification email:

1. Check your spam or junk folder
2. Make sure you entered your email address correctly
3. Click **Resend email** on the verification reminder page to request a new link

<Tip>
  Add [contact@beatpass.ca](mailto:contact@beatpass.ca) to your contacts to ensure future emails from BeatPass don't end up in spam.
</Tip>

## What You Can Do With a Free Account

Once your account is verified, you can:

- **Stream** — Listen to the entire beat catalog
- **Save favorites** — Like beats to find them later
- **Create playlists** — Organize beats into custom playlists
- **Follow producers** — Stay updated when your favorite producers upload new beats
- **Comment** — Leave feedback on beats you enjoy
- **Browse history** — See what you've listened to recently

<Info>
  To download beats with commercial licenses, you'll need to upgrade to a paid subscription. See our [pricing page](https://open.beatpass.ca/pricing) for plan options.
</Info>

## Already Have an Account?

If you already have an account, click [**Sign in**](https://open.beatpass.ca/login) instead of Sign up, or visit the [login page](https://open.beatpass.ca/login).
