Welcome to BeatPass! Whether you're looking to discover new beats, download music for your projects, or share your own productions, this guide will help you get started.

## What is BeatPass?

BeatPass is a music platform where independent artists can discover and download high-quality beats for their projects, and producers can share and monetize their music. With a subscription, you get unlimited access to download beats with commercial licenses.

## Choose Your Path

BeatPass offers different ways to engage with the platform:

### Explore for Free

Create a free account to browse the entire beat catalog, stream music, save favorites, and create playlists. Perfect for discovering new sounds and finding producers you love.

### Subscribe for Downloads

Upgrade to a paid plan to download beats with commercial licenses. Subscribers get unlimited downloads and can use beats in their own music releases, videos, and performances.

### Become a Producer

If you make beats, apply to become a producer. Once approved, you can upload your music, earn money from plays and downloads, and build your audience on the platform.
