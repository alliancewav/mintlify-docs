BeatPass supports three main account states:

- **Free account** (Explorer plan)
- **Paid subscriber** (Classic, Plus, or Pro plan)
- **Approved producer**

You can hold more than one state at the same time — for example, a producer who also has a paid subscription.

## Free Accounts (Explorer)

Every new account starts on the free **Explorer** plan.

With a free account, you can:

- Stream the full catalog
- Like tracks and collections
- Create playlists
- Follow producers and other users
- Post comments
- Browse your listening history
- Create one <Tooltip tip="Credits that control how many beat requests you can create each month. Your plan determines your allowance.">beat request</Tooltip> per month (one token)
- Apply to the Producer Program

<Info>
  Downloads and license certificates require a paid plan. Free accounts cannot download beats.
</Info>

## Paid Subscribers

Paid plans add licensed download access and additional features on top of the free account.

Subscribers can:

- Download eligible beats under BeatPass's standard licensing
- Access license certificates in **Your library** → **Licenses**
- Keep the licenses they obtained while subscribed
- Create more beat requests per month based on plan tier

### Available Plans

| Plan | Beat Request Tokens | Downloads |
|------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Classic** ($29 CAD/month) | 1 per month | Yes |
| **Plus** ($45 CAD/month) | 3 per month | Yes |
| **Pro** ($59 CAD/month) | 5 per month | Yes |

<Note>
  No yearly billing is available. See [Plans & Pricing](/help/plans-and-pricing) for the full feature comparison.
</Note>

## Approved Producers

Approved producers receive a public producer profile plus access to producer-only tools. You become a producer by applying through the [Producer Program](/help/producer-program).

Producer access includes:

- Uploading tracks and collections
- Editing your producer profile
- **Backstage** access (the Producer Dashboard)
- Performance analytics and progress tracking
- Stripe Connect onboarding for payouts

### Producer Dashboard

Click your **profile avatar**, then select **Backstage** to open the Producer Dashboard.

| Section | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your producer overview |
| **Finances** | Earnings, payouts, and Stripe Connect |
| **Activity** | Performance analytics |
| **Progress** | XP, levels, and achievements |
| **Leaderboards** | Producer rankings |
| **My Beats** | Manage your track catalog |
| **My Collections** | Organize your albums and collections |

### Producer Downloads

Producers can always download their own tracks. To download beats from other producers, you need an active paid subscription — producer status alone does not include download access to the broader catalog.

## Comparison

| Capability | Explorer (Free) | Paid Subscriber | Approved Producer |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Stream the catalog | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Like tracks and collections | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Create playlists | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Follow producers and users | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Beat request tokens/month | 1 | 1–5 (by plan) | Based on subscription |
| Download licensed beats | No | Yes | Own tracks only (unless subscribed) |
| License certificates | No | Yes | When downloads are available |
| Upload tracks | No | No | Yes |
| Producer profile | No | No | Yes |
| Producer Dashboard | No | No | Yes |
| Finances and analytics | No | No | Yes |

## Upgrading

### From Free to Paid Subscriber

Click your **profile avatar**, then select **Billing** to choose a plan. You can also visit the [Plans & Pricing](/help/plans-and-pricing) page to compare options.

### From User to Producer

Use the [Producer Program](/help/producer-program) to apply, claim an existing artist page, or request verification. The review process takes one to three days.
