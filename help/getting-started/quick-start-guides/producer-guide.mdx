This guide is for approved producers who need the fastest path to your dashboard, payouts, uploads, and profile.

<ProducerOnly />

## Open the Producer Dashboard

Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation bar, then select **Backstage**.

The dashboard is your central hub for managing everything as a producer. Here's what you'll find in the sidebar:

| Section | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your producer overview |
| **Finances** | Earnings, payouts, and Stripe Connect |
| **Activity** | Performance analytics |
| **Progress** | XP, level, and achievements |
| **Leaderboards** | Producer rankings |
| **My Beats** | Manage your track catalog |
| **My Collections** | Organize your albums and collections |

## Connect Payouts

Before you can receive earnings, set up Stripe Connect from your **Finances** page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Finances">
    In the Producer Dashboard sidebar, click **Finances**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Click **Connect Stripe Account** and confirm your country.

    <Warning>
      Choose your country carefully — it cannot be changed after your Stripe account is created.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe onboarding">
    You'll be redirected to Stripe to provide your identity verification, bank details, and business information. This takes about five minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Your First Beat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the upload page">
    Click **Upload beats** in the top navigation bar, or navigate directly to the upload page from the backstage area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your audio file">
    Drag and drop your file or click **Choose Files to Upload**. BeatPass only accepts lossless formats (WAV, FLAC) to maintain premium audio quality.

    <Info>
      MP3 and other compressed formats are not accepted. Upload WAV or FLAC files for the best results.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in track details">
    Complete the required fields:

    - **Track Name**
    - **Key** and **Scale**
    - **BPM** (between 50 and 200)

    You can also add cover art, a description, tags, and assign the track to a collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your track">
    Licensing defaults to **Standard Licensing** (non-exclusive) automatically — you don't need to configure it before saving. If you want to offer exclusive licensing, expand the **Licensing Options** section to set your terms and pricing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  To upload multiple tracks as a collection, enable the **Create Collection** toggle before selecting your files.
</Tip>

## Edit Your Producer Profile

Your public producer profile is managed separately from the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the edit page">
    From the backstage area, go to your artist profile edit page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your information">
    Use the tabs to manage different sections of your profile:

    - **Details** — Your producer name and genres
    - **Links** — Social media and website links
    - **Biography** — Country, city, and a description of your style
    - **Images** — Profile photos and gallery images
    - **Credits** — Professional credits and collaborations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save Artist** to publish your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Monitor Your Performance

Once your tracks are live, use these sections in the Producer Dashboard to track your growth:

- **Activity** — View play counts, downloads, and engagement analytics
- **Progress** — Track your XP, level up, and unlock achievements
- **Leaderboards** — See how you rank among other producers
- **Finances** — Monitor your earnings, payout status, and transaction history
