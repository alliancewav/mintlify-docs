This guide is for approved producers who need the fastest path to dashboard access, payouts, uploads, and profile management.

## Step 1: Reach the Producer Dashboard

The producer dashboard lives at `/producer-dashboard`.

You may also see a menu entry for it depending on how BeatPass is configured for your account.

### Current Core Areas

| Area | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Home** | Overview |
| **Progress** | XP and achievements |
| **Tracks** | Track management |
| **Activity** | Producer activity analytics |
| **Albums** | Collection management |
| **Leaderboards** | Rankings |
| **Finances** | Stripe Connect and transactions |

## Step 2: Connect Payouts

Before you can receive payouts, connect Stripe Connect from **Producer Dashboard -> Finances**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Finances">
    Go to the **Finances** page in the producer dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your country">
    BeatPass currently asks you to confirm the country before the Stripe flow starts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to Stripe">
    Finish the Stripe Connect onboarding flow and provide the information Stripe requires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Upload Your First Beat

The upload flow lives at `/backstage/upload`.

When preparing your first track, the current product expects:

- A **WAV** audio file
- Cover art
- BPM between **60** and **200**
- Your metadata and licensing choices before saving

<Tip>
If you want to create a collection instead of single-track uploads, use the collection mode inside the upload flow.
</Tip>

## Step 4: Manage Your Producer Profile

Your public producer profile is separate from the dashboard.

To edit it:

1. Open your producer profile
2. Click **More**
3. Choose **Edit**

From there you can manage:

- Cover image and avatar
- Biography and links
- Gallery images
- Genres and location
- Professional credits

## Step 5: Monitor Performance

After you start publishing, use:

- **Activity** for performance analytics
- **Progress** for XP and achievements
- **Leaderboards** for ranking views
- **Finances** for Stripe status and transactions
