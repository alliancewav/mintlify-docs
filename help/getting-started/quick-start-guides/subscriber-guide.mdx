This guide is for paid subscribers who want to download licensed beats and manage plan-based features correctly.

## Step 1: Know What Your Subscription Unlocks

Paid plans add:

- Licensed downloads for eligible tracks
- License certificates in **Your library** → **Licenses**
- Perpetual rights for the beats you downloaded while subscribed
- Plan-based premium features such as beat request tokens

<Note>
Check [Plans & Pricing](/help/plans-and-pricing) for the current plan matrix. Avoid relying on old summaries that treat every paid plan as identical.
</Note>

## Step 2: Download Your First Beat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a track you want to use">
    Browse the catalog, search, or open a producer page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the download action">
    On an eligible track, click **Download**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let BeatPass complete the license flow">
    BeatPass will generate the appropriate licensed download flow for that track and your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your certificate">
    Open **Your library** → **Licenses** to view the license entry and certificate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Understand the Standard Rules

BeatPass's standard subscription license is not "do anything forever" access. A few practical rules matter immediately:

- Attribution is required
- Each download is tied to BeatPass licensing terms
- Content ID registration is not allowed under the standard non-exclusive license

<Card title="Read the licensing guide" href="/help/downloads-and-licensing" icon="file-contract">
  Review the current terms before release.
</Card>

## Step 4: Manage Billing

Use **Billing** from the avatar menu to:

- Review **Current plan**
- Use **Change plan**
- Use **Cancel plan**
- Update your card from the payment-method section

## Step 5: Use Subscriber-Specific Tools

Depending on your plan, you may also want to:

- Create beat requests with your plan's token allowance
- Track your licenses from the library
- Save playlists for release planning or creative direction

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I keep beats I downloaded after I cancel?">
    Yes. Licenses obtained while subscribed remain valid after cancellation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I download any track I see?">
    No. Exclusive-only and sold-exclusive tracks are not available through the standard subscription download flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are my certificates?">
    Open **Your library** → **Licenses**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
