This guide is for users who want to discover music, save favorites, and build playlists without starting with a paid plan.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Create a free account and verify your email.

<Card title="Create your account" href="/help/getting-started/creating-account" icon="user-plus">
  Full signup and verification guide.
</Card>

## Step 2: Explore Music

Start with the current discovery surfaces:

- Homepage recommendations
- Channels
- Search
- Producer pages
- Collections

<Tip>
Use homepage modules, channels, and search together instead of relying on one fixed catalog entry point.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Play and Save Beats

As you browse:

- Play tracks from cards or track pages
- Like tracks you want to revisit
- Save collections you enjoy
- Open producer pages when you find a sound you want more of

## Step 4: Build Your Library

Use your free account to organize what you discover:

- Create playlists
- Keep public or private playlists depending on how you want to share them
- Revisit liked tracks and collections from your library

## Step 5: Follow Producers

When you find a producer you like:

1. Open their producer page
2. Click **Follow**
3. Keep track of their catalog through your own library and profile activity

## Step 6: Know What Requires a Paid Plan

With a free account, you can browse and save music, but paid plans are required for:

- Licensed downloads
- License certificates
- Plan-based beat request tokens

<Info>
  If you want to request beats or download licensed tracks, move next to a paid plan.
</Info>
