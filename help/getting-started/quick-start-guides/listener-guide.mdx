Welcome to BeatPass! This guide will help you discover great beats, organize your favorites, and connect with producers — all for free.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

If you haven't already, [create a free account](https://open.beatpass.ca/register). You'll need to verify your email to access all features.

<Card title="Need help signing up?" href="/help/getting-started/creating-account" icon="user-plus">
  Full guide to creating your account.
</Card>

## Step 2: Explore the Beat Catalog

### Browse New Beats

Click **All Beats** in the sidebar to see the latest uploads. New beats are added daily by producers around the world.

### Discover by Genre

Click **Genres** to filter beats by style:

- Hip Hop, Trap, R&B
- Pop, Electronic, Afrobeats
- And many more

### Find Curated Collections

Click **Collections** to browse beat packs. These are groups of beats organized by theme, mood, or style — perfect for finding inspiration.

## Step 3: Play and Preview Beats

### Playing a Beat

- Hover over any beat card and click the **play button**
- Or click directly on the beat to open its page

### Using the Player

The player bar appears at the bottom of the screen:

- **Play/Pause** — Start or stop playback
- **Skip** — Jump to the next beat
- **Volume** — Adjust the sound level
- **Progress bar** — Scrub through the beat

<Tip>
  Press the spacebar to quickly play or pause without clicking.
</Tip>

## Step 4: Save Your Favorites

### Like a Beat

Click the **heart icon** on any beat to add it to your Liked Beats. Find all your liked beats in **Your Library** → **Liked Beats**.

### Create Playlists

Organize beats into custom playlists:

1. Click the **+** button in the Playlists section of the sidebar
2. Name your playlist
3. Add beats by clicking the **add to playlist** option on any beat

## Step 5: Follow Producers

When you find a producer you like:

1. Click on their name to visit their profile
2. Click **Follow** to stay updated

When they upload new beats, you'll see them in your feed and can receive notifications if enabled.

## Step 6: Submit Beat Requests

Looking for a specific type of beat? Submit a request:

1. Click **Requests** in the sidebar
2. Describe what you're looking for (genre, tempo, mood, etc.)
3. Producers who see your request may create beats that match

## What Free Users Can't Do

With a free Explorer account, you can stream everything, but you cannot:

- Download beats
- Use beats commercially

<Info>
  To download beats with commercial licenses, you'll need a subscription. Plans start at \$29 CAD/month.
</Info>
