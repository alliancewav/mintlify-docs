Once you have a BeatPass account, signing in is quick and easy. You can use your email and password, or sign in with a connected social account.

## Standard Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Click [**Sign in**](https://open.beatpass.ca/login) in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    - **Email address** — The email you used to create your account
    - **Password** — Your account password

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/assets/images/image-9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose to stay signed in (optional)">
    Check **Stay signed in for a month** if you want to remain logged in on this device. Leave it unchecked if you're on a shared or public computer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign in">
    Click the **Sign in** button to access your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Two-Factor Authentication

If you've enabled two-factor authentication on your account, you'll be asked to enter a verification code after entering your password. Open your authenticator app to get the current code.

<Info>
  Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. You can enable it in your [account settings](https://open.beatpass.ca/account-settings).
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    Click **Forgot password?** on the login page to reset your password. You'll receive an email with a link to create a new password.

    [Learn more about password reset](/help/getting-started/password-reset)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm not receiving the password reset email">
    - Check your spam or junk folder
    - Make sure you're using the correct email address
    - Wait a few minutes and try again
    - Contact support if the problem persists
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My password isn't working">
    - Make sure Caps Lock is off
    - Check that you're using the correct email address
    - Try resetting your password if you're unsure
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see 'Email not verified'">
    You need to verify your email before logging in. Check your inbox for the verification email, or request a new one from the verification page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm logged out frequently">
    If you want to stay logged in longer, make sure to check **Stay signed in for a month** when you log in. Also check that your browser isn't set to clear cookies when you close it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account Security Tips

- Use a strong, unique password for your BeatPass account
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
- Don't share your login credentials with others
- Log out when using shared or public computers
- Review your active sessions periodically in account settings
