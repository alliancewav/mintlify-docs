Once you have a BeatPass account, you can sign in with your email and password or with Google if your account uses Google sign-in.

## Standard Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Click [**Sign in**](https://open.beatpass.ca/login) in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Provide the email address and password for your BeatPass account.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/assets/images/image-9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose whether to stay signed in">
    You can enable **Stay signed in for a month** on trusted devices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign in">
    Submit the form to continue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Google Login

If your account is connected to Google, you can also use **Continue with Google** on the login page.

## Two-Factor Authentication

If 2FA is enabled on your account, BeatPass will ask for your authenticator code after you enter your password.

<Info>
  You can manage 2FA from [account settings](https://open.beatpass.ca/account-settings).
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    Click **Forgot password?** on the login page to request the password email.

    [Learn more about password reset](/help/getting-started/password-reset)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My password is not working">
    Confirm you are using the right email address, check for typos, and make sure Caps Lock is off. If needed, request a reset link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I signed up with Google">
    Use **Continue with Google** and sign in with the same Google account you connected to BeatPass.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see an email-verification screen">
    BeatPass currently requires email confirmation. Open the verification email or use **Resend email** on the verification page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I keep getting logged out">
    Use **Stay signed in for a month** on trusted devices and make sure your browser is not clearing cookies automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account Security Tips

- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share your login credentials
- Log out on shared devices
- Review your active sessions in account settings
