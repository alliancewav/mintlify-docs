## Welcome, Listeners

Even if you're not producing or licensing beats, your engagement matters. Every play, save, and follow supports producers and helps great music surface.

<Info>
  **Time to complete:** 5 minutes for basic setup
</Info>

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin:

- Free BeatPass account (email or social login)
- Device with audio capability

---

## Step-by-Step Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for free:
    - Visit [open.beatpass.ca](https://open.beatpass.ca)
    - Register with email or social login
    - Verify your email for full features
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore the Catalog">
    Start discovering:
    - **Trending**: See what's popular right now
    - **Genres**: Browse by musical style
    - **New Releases**: Catch the latest uploads
    - **Search**: Find specific producers or styles
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Learn the Player">
    Navigate the listening experience:
    - Click any track to preview
    - Use the waveform to skip through sections
    - Adjust volume and playback settings
    - Queue multiple tracks for continuous play
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Your Favorites">
    Build your library:
    - Click the heart icon to save beats
    - Access saved tracks in your Library
    - Organize with personal playlists
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Follow Producers">
    Stay updated:
    - Follow producers whose style you enjoy
    - Get notified when they upload new beats
    - Build your feed with personalized content
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigating the Platform

### Discovery Tools

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Browse">
    Explore curated sections:
    - **Trending**: Most played this week
    - **Featured**: Editor picks and highlights
    - **Genres**: Hip Hop, R&B, Trap, Lo-Fi, and more
    - **Moods**: Dark, Uplifting, Chill, Hard
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Search">
    Find exactly what you want:
    - Search by track title
    - Search by producer name
    - Search by genre or tags
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Filter">
    Narrow your results:
    - BPM range
    - Key and scale
    - Duration
    - Release date
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Player Features

| Feature | How to Use |
|---------|------------|
| Play/Pause | Click the track or use spacebar |
| Seek | Click anywhere on waveform |
| Volume | Slider in player controls |
| Queue | Click "Add to Queue" on any track |
| Repeat | Toggle in player settings |

## Your Impact

### How Engagement Helps

Your listening activity directly supports producers:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Plays" icon="play">
    Every play counts toward a producer's contribution value.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Completions" icon="check">
    Listening to 80%+ of a track is weighted higher than skips.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Saves" icon="heart">
    Saving a beat signals quality and boosts its visibility.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Follows" icon="user-plus">
    Following producers helps them grow their audience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Authentic engagement matters. The platform detects and discounts artificial plays.
</Note>

## Building Your Library

### Saving & Organizing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Save Beats">
    Click the heart icon on any track to add it to your library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Playlists">
    Group saved beats by mood, project, or any theme you choose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Curators">
    Discover curated playlists from tastemakers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Discoveries">
    Share tracks or playlists with friends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Playlist Ideas

- "Workout Beats" - High energy tracks for the gym
- "Study Vibes" - Lo-fi and chill instrumentals
- "Car Music" - Hard-hitting bass and energy
- "Late Night" - Atmospheric and moody beats
