# Landing Page

When you visit BeatPass without signing in, you land on the public marketing and discovery entry point for the platform.

## What the Public Landing Page Does

The landing page is designed to:

- Introduce BeatPass
- Link you to sign up or sign in
- Surface plans and producer-program entry points
- Send you deeper into public discovery pages

## What You Can Expect to See

The exact sections can change over time, but the public landing experience generally includes:

- A hero area with account actions
- Messaging about BeatPass plans and licensing
- Producer-focused value propositions
- Public discovery links into the catalog or featured content

## What You Can Do Without Logging In

Before you create an account, you can still:

- Browse public music surfaces
- Open producer pages
- Review plans and pricing
- Read about the Producer Program

## What Changes After Login

Once you sign in, BeatPass moves you into the app experience:

- Your account and library become available
- Notifications and profile actions appear
- Homepage and recommendation modules can change based on your account context

<Note>
Homepage and landing-page content are intentionally flexible. BeatPass can rotate sections, channels, and promotional modules without changing the core account flows.
</Note>
