# Landing Page

When you visit BeatPass without being logged in, you'll see the landing page — a welcome experience designed to introduce you to the platform and help you get started.

## What You'll See

The landing page showcases everything BeatPass has to offer:

### Hero Section

The top of the page features:

- **Platform tagline** — A brief description of what BeatPass is
- **Call-to-action buttons** — Sign up or log in to get started
- **Featured visuals** — Preview of the platform experience

## Getting Started

From the landing page, you can:

### Create an Account

Click **Sign up** to create your free account:

1. Enter your email and password
2. Confirm your email address
3. Start exploring beats

[Learn about creating an account →](/help/getting-started/creating-account)

### Log In

Already have an account? Click **Sign in** to access your account:

1. Enter your email and password
2. Complete 2FA if enabled
3. Continue where you left off

[Learn about logging in →](/help/getting-started/logging-in)

### Explore Without Signing Up

You can browse the platform before creating an account:

- View beat catalogs
- Listen to previews
- Explore producer profiles
- Check out pricing

<Tip>
  To download beats, follow artists, or save favorites, you'll need to create a free account.
</Tip>

## After Logging In

Once you log in, the landing page is replaced by your personalized homepage:

- **For You** recommendations
- **Recent activity** from followed producers
- **Continue listening** from where you left off
- **Quick access** to your library
