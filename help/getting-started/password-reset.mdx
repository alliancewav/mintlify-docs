If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it for security reasons, you can create a new one using your email address.

## How to Reset Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the forgot password page">
    On the login page, click **Forgot password?** to open the password reset form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address associated with your BeatPass account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send reset link">
    Click **Send reset link**. A password reset email will be sent to your address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Look for an email from BeatPass about resetting your password. Click the link in the email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your new password">
    On the password reset page:

    - Enter your **New password**
    - Enter **Confirm password**

    Click **Reset password** to save your new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your new password">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your email and new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive the reset email">
    - Check your spam or junk folder
    - Make sure you entered the correct email address
    - Wait a few minutes — emails can sometimes be delayed
    - Try requesting a new reset link
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The reset link isn't working">
    Password reset links expire for security reasons. If your link has expired or isn't working, return to the login page, click **Forgot password?**, and request a new link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't remember which email I used">
    Try any email addresses you commonly use. Each attempt will only send a reset link if the address matches an existing account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I keep getting 'invalid token' errors">
    This usually means the reset link has expired or has already been used. Request a fresh reset link and use it right away.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Never share your password reset links with anyone. BeatPass will never ask for your password or reset links.
</Warning>
