BeatPass is a beat licensing and music collaboration platform built to serve both producers and artists. Here's what makes it different.

---

## For Artists & Listeners

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Unlimited Downloads" icon="download">
    Every paid plan includes unlimited beat downloads with a commercial license — no per-beat fees.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Perpetual Licenses" icon="infinity">
    Your license never expires. Cancel your subscription and you keep every license you obtained.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transparent Terms" icon="file-check">
    Every download generates a verifiable license certificate with clear usage terms — no guesswork.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Fingerprinting" icon="fingerprint">
    Every track has a unique BeatPassID acoustic fingerprint, linking your license to the exact audio file for easy verification.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## For Producers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="100% Revenue Share" icon="dollar-sign">
    Producers keep 100% of their listed exclusive beat price. Subscription revenue is shared based on your tracks' contribution value.
  </Card>
  <Card title="You Retain Your Rights" icon="shield-check">
    Non-exclusive licensing means you always own your music. Your beats stay yours — subscribers get a usage license, not ownership.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unlimited Uploads" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    Upload as many beats as you want in WAV format. No storage limits, no hidden costs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Built-In Analytics" icon="chart-bar">
    Track plays, engagement, audience demographics, and earnings in real time through the Producer Dashboard and Backstage Insights.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### More Producer Tools

- **Gamification system** — Earn XP, unlock achievements, and climb leaderboards to increase your visibility
- **Collaboration support** — Credit co-producers on tracks and share contribution value fairly
- **Exclusive license presets** — Configure your exclusive pricing, publishing splits, and terms once and apply them to any track
- **Professional credits** — Showcase your work on releases outside BeatPass, linked via Spotify
- **Beat requests** — Receive custom beat requests from subscribers looking for specific sounds

## The BeatPass Model

| Traditional Beat Stores | BeatPass |
|------------------------|----------|
| Per-beat pricing | Subscription-based unlimited downloads |
| Revenue splits vary | 100% to producer on exclusives |
| License terms vary by seller | Standardized, verifiable license certificates |
| No fingerprinting | BeatPassID acoustic fingerprint on every track |
| License expires or is unclear | Perpetual license — yours forever |

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    Explore beats by genre, mood, BPM, or producer. Listen to full tracks — no previews or watermarks for subscribers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download with a license">
    One click downloads the beat and generates a license certificate tied to your account and the track's fingerprint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use commercially">
    Release your song on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, or anywhere else. Your license certificate proves your rights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
