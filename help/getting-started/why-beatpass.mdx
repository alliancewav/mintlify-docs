BeatPass is built around licensed beat access for subscribers and public growth tools for producers.

## For Artists, Listeners, and Subscribers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Licensed Downloads" icon="download">
    Paid plans unlock downloads tied to standardized BeatPass licensing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Perpetual Licenses" icon="infinity">
    Licenses obtained while subscribed remain valid after cancellation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="License Certificates" icon="file-lines">
    Qualifying downloads generate verifiable certificates in your library.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Beat Discovery" icon="compass">
    Search, channels, producer pages, playlists, and library tools in one place.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## For Producers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Keep 100% of Your Listed Exclusive Price" icon="dollar-sign">
    The platform fee is added on top of the buyer price instead of deducted from the listed exclusive price.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contribution-Based Subscription Revenue" icon="chart-bar">
    Producer payouts from subscriptions are based on contribution value rather than fixed catalog quotas.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Producer Profile Tools" icon="music">
    Manage catalog, branding, credits, gallery, achievements, and follower-facing identity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics and Finances" icon="chart-line">
    Track performance and connect Stripe Connect from the producer dashboard.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Platform Comparison

| Traditional beat-store pattern | BeatPass approach |
| --- | --- |
| Per-beat nonexclusive checkout every time | Subscription-based access for standard licensed downloads |
| Seller-by-seller license language | Standard BeatPass subscription licensing |
| Limited post-download verification | Library-based certificates and verification flow |
| Producer identity split across platforms | Public producer profiles with credits, gallery, and stats |

## How BeatPass Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover music">
    Browse the catalog, search, producer pages, channels, or collections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or license what fits">
    Free accounts can save and organize music. Paid plans can download eligible tracks with licensing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grow on the producer side">
    Approved producers upload tracks, manage their public profile, and monitor activity in the producer dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
