BeatPass has a consistent layout across pages — a top navigation bar, a sidebar, a player bar, and different content areas depending on where you are. What you see may vary slightly by account type and screen size.

## Top Navigation Bar

The top bar is a floating pill at the top of the screen. From left to right, you'll find:

- **Search** — type to find beats, producers, collections, playlists, and tags
- **Action buttons** — these change based on your account:
  - Not a producer? You'll see **Upload Beats** (links to the Producer Program) and **Subscribe** (links to pricing)
  - Approved producer? You'll see **Upload beats** (goes directly to the upload page)
- **Messages** — a chat icon for messaging (when logged in)
- **Notifications** — a bell icon showing your unread notification count
- **Profile avatar** — opens your account menu

## Profile Avatar Menu

Click your **profile avatar** to open the account menu. The available items include:

| Item | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **Edit Profile** | Opens your account settings |
| **Backstage** | Opens the Producer Dashboard (producers only) |
| **Profile page** | Opens your public user profile |
| **Artist profile** | Opens your producer page (producers only) |
| **Notifications** | Opens notifications (on mobile) |
| **Billing** | Opens subscription and payment settings |
| **Dark mode** / **Light mode** | Switches the interface theme |
| **Log out** | Signs you out |

<Note>
  Some menu items only appear for specific account types. For example, **Backstage** and **Artist profile** are only visible to approved producers.
</Note>

## Sidebar

On desktop, the left sidebar gives you quick access to:

- **Site logo** — click to return to the homepage
- **Primary navigation** — links to main sections like discovery, your library, and other areas
- **Your Music** — quick links to your personal content
- **Playlists** — your playlists with a button to create new ones

If you're on a free plan, a **Go Pro** card appears at the bottom of the sidebar with an **Unlock** button linking to pricing.

## Your Library

Your library is where saved and account-linked content lives. Access it from the sidebar. Available sections include:

| Section | What's inside |
|---------|-------------|
| **Beats** | Tracks you've liked |
| **Playlists** | Playlists you've created |
| **Collections** | Albums and collections you've saved |
| **Producer(s)** | Producers you follow |
| **Play history** | Your recent listening activity |
| **Beat requests** | Beat requests you've created |
| **Licenses** | Your download license certificates |
| **Lyrics** | Lyrics you've viewed |

<Info>
  Some sections like **Licenses** only show content if you have a paid subscription and have downloaded beats.
</Info>

## Discovery

Use discovery surfaces to explore and find new music:

- **Homepage** — curated recommendations and featured content
- **Search** — find beats, producers, collections, playlists, and tags
- **Producer pages** — browse a producer's catalog, biography, and credits
- **Track pages** — view details, play, and access licensing for individual beats
- **Collections** — browse grouped tracks (albums and collections)

## Producer Tools

If you're an approved producer, you also have access to:

- **Backstage** (Producer Dashboard) — click your **profile avatar**, then select **Backstage**
- **Upload page** — click **Upload beats** in the top navigation bar

See the [Producer Quick Start](/help/getting-started/quick-start-guides/producer-guide) for a full walkthrough.

## Player Bar

When music is playing, a player bar appears at the bottom of the screen with these controls:

| Control | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Shuffle** | Randomize play order |
| **Previous** | Go to previous track |
| **Play / Pause** | Start or pause playback |
| **Next** | Skip to the next track |
| **Repeat** | Loop the current track or queue |
| **Seekbar** | Scrub through the current track |
| **Lyrics** | Open lyrics for the current track (when available) |
| **Download** | Download the current track (paid subscribers) |
| **Queue** | View and manage what's playing next |
| **Volume** | Adjust playback volume |
| **Expand** | Open the full-screen player overlay |

The player bar also shows the current track's artwork, name, and artist — click any of these to navigate to that track or producer's page.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Space` or `K` | Play or pause |
| `Ctrl` + `→` or `Shift` + `→` | Next track |
| `Ctrl` + `←` or `Shift` + `←` | Previous track |

## Mobile Navigation

On smaller screens, BeatPass adjusts the layout:

- The **top bar** becomes a compact pill with search, notifications, messages, and your avatar
- A **more actions** button reveals upload and subscription links
- The **bottom bar** provides quick navigation between main sections
- The **player** expands into a full-screen mobile playback view when tapped
