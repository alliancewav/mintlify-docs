## Main Navigation

### Sidebar Menu

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation hub. It's organized into sections:

#### Discover Section

| Menu Item       | What You'll Find                                                  |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Discover**    | Featured beats, trending tracks, and personalized recommendations |
| **All Beats**   | Browse the complete beat catalog with latest uploads              |
| **Genres**      | Filter beats by genre like Hip Hop, R&B, Pop, and more            |
| **Collections** | Curated beat packs grouped by theme or style                      |
| **Requests**    | Community beat requests from artists looking for specific sounds  |
| **Producers**   | Browse and discover producers on the platform                     |
| **Community**   | Connect with other users and see community activity               |

#### Your Library Section

When you're logged in, you'll see your personal library:

| Menu Item             | What You'll Find                      |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **My Requests**       | Beat requests you've submitted        |
| **My Licenses**       | Beats you've downloaded with licenses |
| **Liked Beats**       | Beats you've saved to your favorites  |
| **Liked Collections** | Beat packs you've saved               |
| **Following**         | Producers you're following            |
| **History**           | Your recently played beats            |

#### Playlists Section

Below Your Library, you'll find your playlists. Click the **\+** button to create a new playlist.

### Top Navigation Bar

The top bar gives you quick access to:

- **Search** — Find beats, producers, and collections
- **Notifications** — See updates and alerts
- **Profile menu** — Access your account, settings, and more

### Profile Menu

Click your **profile avatar** in the top right to access:

| Menu Item           | What It Does                                    |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Profile page**    | View your public profile                        |
| **Edit Profile**    | Change your account settings                    |
| **Backstage**       | Access your Producer Dashboard (producers only) |
| **Billing**         | Manage your subscription                        |
| **Dark/Light mode** | Switch between dark and light themes            |
| **Log out**         | Sign out of your account                        |

## Browsing Beats

### Beat Cards

Beats are displayed as cards showing:

- Beat artwork
- Beat title
- Producer name
- BPM (tempo)
- Key

Hover over a beat card to see play controls and quick actions.

### Playing Beats

- **Click the play button** on any beat card to start playback
- The **player bar** appears at the bottom of the screen
- Use the player controls to play, pause, skip, and adjust volume

### Beat Actions

When viewing a beat, you can:

- **Like** — Save it to your favorites
- **Add to playlist** — Add it to one of your playlists
- **Share** — Get a link to share the beat
- **Download** — Download with license (subscribers only)

## Mobile Navigation

On mobile devices, the navigation adapts to a bottom tab bar with:

- **Home** — Return to the main page
- **New** — Browse latest beats
- **Mood** — Browse by genre
- **Library** — Access your saved content
- **Find** — Search for beats and producers

## Keyboard Shortcuts

For faster navigation:

| Shortcut | Action              |
| -------- | ------------------- |
| `Space`  | Play/Pause          |
| `→`      | Skip to next beat   |
| `←`      | Go to previous beat |
| `↑`      | Increase volume     |
| `↓`      | Decrease volume     |

## Customization

### Theme

Switch between dark and light modes from your profile menu. BeatPass will remember your preference.

### Language

Change your language preference in **Edit Profile** → **Location and language**.
