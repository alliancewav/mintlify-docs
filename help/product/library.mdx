## Overview

Your library is your personal collection of saved music on BeatPass. Save tracks, create playlists, and organize your favorites.

---

## Saving Tracks

### How to Save

| Location | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Track page | Click the **heart** icon |
| Player bar | Click **heart** on current track |
| Search results | Hover and click **heart** |
| Playlist view | Click **heart** next to track |

### Where Saved Tracks Appear

- **Library > Songs** — All your saved tracks
- **Library > History** — Recently played (separate from saves)

### Save Limits

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Maximum saved tracks | 10,000 |

<Tip>
  If you hit the limit, remove tracks you no longer need to make room for new saves.
</Tip>

## Following Artists

### How to Follow

1. Navigate to an artist/producer profile
2. Click the **Follow** button
3. Their new releases appear in your feed

### What Following Does

| Feature | Behavior |
|---------|----------|
| Feed updates | New releases from followed artists |
| Notifications | Optional alerts for new drops |
| Discovery | Influences your recommendations |

### Following Limits

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Maximum following | 5,000 accounts |
| Rate limits | Apply to prevent spam |

## Creating Playlists

### Steps to Create

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Library">
    Go to **Library** from the navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Create Playlist">
    Click the **+** or **Create Playlist** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name Your Playlist">
    Give it a descriptive name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Tracks">
    Add tracks from browse, search, or your library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Playlist Limits

You can create up to 100 playlists, with up to 500 tracks each.

## Playlist Visibility

### Privacy Settings

| Setting | Who Can See | Who Can Find |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Public** | Anyone | Searchable, shareable |
| **Private** | Only you | Not searchable |

### Changing Visibility

1. Open the playlist
2. Click **Edit** or the settings icon
3. Toggle **Public** / **Private**
4. Save changes

<Note>
  Private playlists can still be shared via direct link if you choose to share.
</Note>

## Collaborative Playlists

### Inviting Editors

You can invite others to add tracks to your playlist:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Playlist Settings">
    Click the settings icon on your playlist.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Collaboration">
    Toggle **Allow collaborators**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Invite Users">
    Search for users and send invitations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="They Accept">
    Invited users receive a notification to accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editor Permissions

| Can Do | Cannot Do |
|--------|-----------|
| Add and remove tracks on collaborative playlists they're invited to | Change playlist name/image |
| Reorder tracks | Delete the playlist |
| Leave the collaboration | Change privacy settings |

## Managing Your Library

### Removing Saved Tracks

- Click the **heart** again to unsave
- Or go to **Library > Songs** and click remove

### Deleting Playlists

1. Open the playlist
2. Click **Delete Playlist**
3. Confirm deletion

<Warning>
  Deleted playlists cannot be recovered. Collaborative playlists are deleted for all editors.
</Warning>

### Reordering Playlists

Drag and drop playlists in your library sidebar to reorder.

## Follower Notifications

### When You're Followed

You receive a notification when someone follows you (if enabled).

### Notification Settings

Control follower notifications in **Settings > Notifications**:

| Option | Default |
|--------|---------|
| Email on new follower | Off |
| Push on new follower | On |
| In-app notification | Always on |

## Syncing Across Devices

Your library syncs automatically across all devices when logged in:

- Saved tracks
- Created playlists
- Follow list
- Listening history

<Note>
  Changes may take a few seconds to sync across devices.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Saved track disappeared">
    - The track may have been removed by the producer
    - Check if you're logged into the right account
    - Try refreshing the page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't create more playlists">
    You may have hit the playlist limit. Delete unused playlists to make room.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Playlist not showing for collaborator">
    - Ensure they accepted the invitation
    - Invitations expire after 7 days
    - Check they're logged into the right account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't follow more artists">
    You may have hit a rate limit or the maximum following count (5,000). Try again later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
