## Overview

Your account settings control your profile, security, and preferences on BeatPass. This guide covers all configuration options available in your account.

---

## Accessing Settings

Navigate to settings:

1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown
3. Or go directly to: `open.beatpass.ca/settings`

---

## Settings Panels

Your account settings are organized into the following sections:

| Panel | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Account Details** | Profile information, display name, bio, avatar |
| **Social Login** | Connect/disconnect your Google account |
| **Password** | Change your password |
| **Two Factor Authentication** | Enable/disable 2FA |
| **Active Sessions** | View and manage logged-in devices |
| **Location and Language** | Set your locale and language preferences |
| **API Access Tokens** | Manage personal access tokens |
| **Danger Zone** | Account deletion |

<Note>
  Email verification is required to access all platform features. If you haven't verified your email, you'll see a prompt to do so.
</Note>

---

## Account Details

### Profile Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Display Name** | Your public name shown across the platform |
| **First Name / Last Name** | Your real name (optional) |
| **Avatar** | Profile photo (recommended: 400x400px square) |

### Updating Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Account Details**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Fields">
    Update your display name, bio, or other information.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Avatar">
    Click on your avatar to upload a new image.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to apply changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Social Login

Connect your Google account for faster login.

### Connecting Google

1. Go to **Settings > Social Login**
2. Click **Enable** next to Google
3. Sign in with your Google account in the popup
4. Your accounts are now linked

### Disconnecting Google

1. Go to **Settings > Social Login**
2. Click **Disable** next to Google
3. The account is disconnected

<Tip>
  You can still log in with your email and password after disconnecting your Google account.
</Tip>

## Password

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Password Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Password**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    Enter your existing password to verify your identity.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Enter and confirm your new password.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Update Password** to apply the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you signed up with a social account and never set a password, you can set one by using the "Forgot Password" flow.
</Note>

## Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your account using TOTP-based two-factor authentication.

### Setting Up 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 2FA Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Two Factor Authentication**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click **Enable** to start the setup process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Password">
    Enter your password to verify your identity.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, etc.) and scan the QR code.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Verification Code">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app to confirm setup.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Recovery Codes">
    Download and securely store your recovery codes. These are your backup if you lose access to your authenticator.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Save your recovery codes!** Without them, you may permanently lose access to your account if you lose your 2FA device.
</Warning>

### Disabling 2FA

1. Go to **Settings > Two Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Disable**
3. Confirm with your password

### Regenerating Recovery Codes

1. Go to **Settings > Two Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Regenerate recovery codes**
3. Confirm with your password
4. Save your new codes securely

<Note>
  BeatPass uses TOTP-based 2FA only. SMS authentication is not available.
</Note>

## Active Sessions

View and manage devices logged into your account.

### Viewing Sessions

1. Go to **Settings > Active Sessions**
2. See all devices with active sessions
3. Each session shows:
   - Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
   - Browser and platform
   - Location (city, country)
   - IP address
   - Last active time

### Logging Out Other Sessions

If you see unrecognized sessions or want to secure your account:

1. Go to **Settings > Active Sessions**
2. Click **Logout other sessions**
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. All sessions except your current one will be terminated

<Tip>
  Sessions marked as "This device" are your current session and cannot be revoked.
</Tip>

## API Access Tokens

If you need programmatic access to BeatPass, you can create personal access tokens.

### Creating a Token

1. Go to **Settings > API Access Tokens**
2. Click **Create Token**
3. Give your token a descriptive name
4. Copy and save the token (it won't be shown again)

### Revoking a Token

1. Go to **Settings > API Access Tokens**
2. Click **Revoke** next to the token
3. The token is immediately invalidated

<Warning>
  Keep your API tokens secure. Anyone with your token can access your account programmatically.
</Warning>

## Danger Zone

### Account Deletion

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Confirm the final deletion

This permanently removes:
- All profile information
- Uploaded tracks (if producer)
- Library and playlists
- Transaction history
- Messages
- All associated data

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't change email">
    Verify you have access to your current email. If locked out, contact support for assistance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Settings not saving">
    Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't enable 2FA">
    Ensure your authenticator app is synced with the correct time. Try reinstalling the app if issues persist.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missing producer settings">
    Producer settings (Backstage) only appear after your producer application is approved.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Social login not working">
    Ensure popups are enabled in your browser. Try clearing cookies and attempting again.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
