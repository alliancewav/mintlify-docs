## Overview

BeatPass gives you control over your personal information and how it's shared. This guide explains your privacy options and how to manage your data.

---

## Profile Visibility

BeatPass is a public platform for artists and producers. Profiles are designed to be discoverable to facilitate collaboration.

| Information | Visibility |
|-------------|------------|
| Display Name | Always public (required) |
| Username | Always public |
| Bio | Public on your profile |
| Profile Photo | Public on your profile |
| Location | Optional—leave blank to hide |
| Social Links | Optional—remove to hide |
| Email Address | Always private |
| Earnings Data | Always private |

### Updating Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings > Account Details**
2. Edit your information
3. Click **Save** to apply changes

<Note>
  Your email address is never displayed publicly. Other users can contact you through BeatPass messaging.
</Note>

## Producer Privacy

### What's Private

As a producer, the following data is visible only to you:

| Data | Visibility |
|------|------------|
| Total earnings | Private (your Backstage only) |
| Per-track revenue | Private |
| Contribution percentages | Private |
| Payout history | Private |
| Detailed analytics | Private |

### What's Public

| Data | Visibility |
|------|------------|
| Play counts | Shown on tracks |
| Track metadata | Public (title, BPM, key, tags) |
| Upload date | Public |
| Follower count | Public |

## Notification Preferences

Control which notifications you receive and how you receive them:

1. Click the **bell icon** in the navigation bar
2. Click the **Settings** (gear icon) button
3. Toggle notification types on/off for Browser and Email channels
4. Click **Update preferences**

You can independently control:
- **Browser notifications** — In-app and real-time alerts
- **Email notifications** — Sent to your registered email

See [Notification Preferences](/help/account-security/notifications) for detailed options.

## Data You Can Access

### Viewing Your Data

Access your account data through Settings:

- **Account Details** — Profile information, email, location
- **Social Login** — Connected social accounts
- **Active Sessions** — Devices logged into your account
- **Subscription** — Current plan and billing history

### Deleting Specific Data

| Data Type | How to Delete |
|-----------|---------------|
| Uploaded Tracks | Backstage > Tracks > Delete |
| Playlists | Library > Playlist > Delete |
| Saved Beats | Remove from library |
| Messages | Delete within conversation |

---

## Account Deletion

### How to Delete Your Account

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Confirm the deletion

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, uploaded tracks, earnings history, and content will be removed.
</Warning>

### What Happens After Deletion

| Data | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| Profile | Immediately removed |
| Uploaded Tracks | Removed from catalog |
| Playlists | Deleted |
| Messages | Removed from conversations |
| Earnings History | Retained anonymously for tax/legal compliance |
| Analytics | Aggregated data retained (anonymized) |

<Note>
  If you have pending payouts, contact support before deleting your account to ensure you receive your earnings.
</Note>

## Connected Accounts

### Google Login

You can connect your Google account for faster login.

Manage your connection at **Settings > Social Login**.

### Disconnecting Google

1. Go to **Settings > Social Login**
2. Click **Disable** next to Google
3. Your Google account will be disconnected

<Tip>
  You can still log in with your email and password after disconnecting your Google account.
</Tip>

## Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies

Required for platform functionality:

- Session management (keeping you logged in)
- Security features (CSRF protection)
- User preferences (theme, language)

### Analytics

BeatPass uses analytics to improve the platform:

- Page view tracking
- Feature usage metrics
- Performance monitoring

### Cookie Settings

Users in regions with cookie consent laws (EU, UK, etc.) will see a cookie notice banner. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

BeatPass is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** — Request a copy of your data
- **Correction** — Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your data
- **Portability** — Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact support.
