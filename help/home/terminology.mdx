## Glossary

A reference guide to BeatPass-specific terminology.

---

## A-C

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Beat">
    An instrumental track uploaded by a producer, available for streaming, licensing, or purchase.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="BPM">
    Beats Per Minute - the tempo measurement of a track. Used for filtering and matching beats to projects.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Completion">
    When a listener plays a track to at least 80% of its duration. Completions are weighted higher than partial plays in contribution calculations.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Contribution Value">
    The calculated value a track generates based on engagement metrics. Used to determine a producer's share of platform revenue.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Curator">
    A user who creates playlists and showcases beats from various producers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## D-G

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Exclusive License">
    A licensing option where the producer removes the beat from public sale after the transaction. Typically higher priced.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Fingerprint">
    A unique audio signature generated for each uploaded track to detect duplicates and verify authenticity.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Genre">
    Musical category used to organize and filter beats (e.g., Hip Hop, R&B, Trap, Lo-Fi).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## H-L

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Key">
    The musical key of a track (e.g., C Major, A Minor). Helps artists find beats matching their vocal range.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Leaderboard">
    Rankings of producers based on performance metrics like plays, earnings, or quality scores.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Library">
    A user's personal library of saved beats, purchased tracks, and created playlists.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="License Certificate">
    Official documentation proving a user's right to use a licensed beat. Includes terms, dates, and verification codes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## M-P

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Metadata">
    Information attached to a track: title, BPM, key, genre, mood, tags, and description.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Non-Exclusive License">
    A licensing option where the beat remains available for others to license. Lower priced than exclusive.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payout">
    Scheduled distribution of earnings to producers, typically monthly based on contribution values.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Play">
    A streaming event where a user listens to a track. Partial and full plays are tracked separately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Producer">
    A user who uploads original beats to the platform.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Producer Intelligence">
    Analytics suite showing XP, achievements, leaderboard position, and growth insights.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Q-S

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Quality Score">
    A metric based on metadata completeness, audio quality, and engagement patterns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Request (Beat Request)">
    A public post by an artist describing the type of beat they need, inviting producer responses.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Save">
    When a user adds a beat to their library. Saves indicate interest and contribute to track value.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Scale">
    Whether a track is in a Major or Minor key. Combined with Key for complete tonal identification.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Skip">
    When a listener stops playback before significant duration. High skip rates may indicate quality issues.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## T-Z

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tag">
    Descriptive keywords attached to tracks for search and discovery (e.g., "dark", "melodic", "hard hitting").
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Level">
    Producer ranking (1-100) based on XP accumulation, with titles like Novice, Apprentice, Skilled, Expert, Master, and Legendary.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Track">
    General term for any audio file on the platform, though most commonly refers to beats.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Waveform">
    Visual representation of audio amplitude over time, displayed on the player for navigation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="XP (Experience Points)">
    Points earned through platform activity: uploads, plays, achievements, and engagement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
