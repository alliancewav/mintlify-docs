## Popular Topics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn money on BeatPass?" icon="coins" defaultOpen>
    Producers earn through two revenue streams:
    
    1. **Contribution Pool**  Your share of subscription revenue based on plays, engagement quality, and recency, funded from each paid subscription invoice
    2. **Direct License Sales**  Earnings from exclusive license purchases. Buyers pay your listed price plus a platform fee; you receive the price you set minus Stripe processing fees.
    
    <Card title="Learn the Contribution Formula" icon="calculator" href="/help/earnings/formula" horizontal />
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I upload my first beat?" icon="upload">
    Approved producers can upload unlimited beats. Each upload is audio-fingerprinted for protection.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Apply for Producer Access">
        Request backstage access from your profile settings.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload Your Audio">
        Go to Backstage â†’ Upload and drag in your WAV or FLAC file (lossless only).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Metadata">
        Fill in title, BPM, key, genre, and tags for discoverability.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Review and publish to make your beat live.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Card title="Full Upload Guide" icon="book" href="/help/product/uploads" horizontal />
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do licenses work?" icon="file-contract">
    BeatPass offers two license types:
    
    | License | Description | Best For |
    |---------|-------------|----------|
    | **Non-Exclusive** | Beat stays available after sale | Mixtapes, demos, budget projects |
    | **Exclusive** | Beat removed from catalog | Commercial releases, major projects |
    
    <Card title="Complete Licensing Guide" icon="book" href="/help/product/licensing" horizontal />
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="When do I get paid?" icon="credit-card">
    Payout timing depends on what you're earning from:
    
    - **License sales**  Funds flow to your Stripe Connect account after each successful purchase (minus fees), then to your bank according to your Stripe payout schedule
    - **Subscription splits**  Calculated after each paid subscription invoice (`invoice.paid`) and transferred via Stripe Connect
    - **Minimum threshold**: $10 USD (or regional equivalent)
    
    <Card title="Payout Details" icon="money-bill" href="/help/earnings/payouts" horizontal />
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
