BeatPass supports multiple currencies for producer pricing and payout tracking. The most important rule is simple: your listed track price is your price, and the platform fee is added on top at checkout.

<Snippet file="callouts/platform-fee.mdx" />

## Supported Pricing Currencies

BeatPass currently supports these currencies for track purchase pricing:

| Currency | Code |
|----------|------|
| US Dollar | USD |
| Euro | EUR |
| British Pound | GBP |
| Canadian Dollar | CAD |

<Note>
  Current paid BeatPass subscription plans are listed in **CAD**.
</Note>

## For Producers

### Setting Track Prices

- Choose the pricing currency that fits your audience and catalog strategy
- Buyers see your listed price plus the platform fee at checkout
- BeatPass does not deduct the platform fee from your listed price

### What You See in Finances

**Producer Finances** can show:

- A primary balance currency
- Multi-currency warnings when your account has balances in more than one currency
- Mixed-currency transaction history depending on what you sold and how Stripe recorded it

## For Buyers

If the purchase currency does not match the card or bank's currency, conversion can happen outside BeatPass. The exact outcome depends on Stripe and the buyer's bank.

## Payout Currency Expectations

Your payout flow depends on your Stripe Connect account and the balances Stripe is managing for you.

<Info>
  BeatPass does not publish a fixed currency-conversion fee in help docs because conversion behavior can vary by Stripe, currency pair, and payout destination.
</Info>

## Practical Tips

- Use a pricing currency your buyers already understand
- Check **Producer Finances** for mixed-currency warnings
- Expect Stripe and your bank to control most conversion details after checkout
- Review your supported-country and Stripe setup if you need a different payout arrangement

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I price a track in more than one currency at the same time?">
    Each priced track uses one configured pricing currency at a time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my earnings history contain more than one currency?">
    Yes. BeatPass can surface a multi-currency warning in **Producer Finances** when your account has balances in more than one currency.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who controls currency conversion after checkout?">
    Stripe and the payout destination handle most of that behavior, not the BeatPass help system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are subscriptions shown in the same currency as track pricing?">
    Not necessarily. Current paid BeatPass plans are listed in CAD, while track pricing supports USD, EUR, GBP, and CAD.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
