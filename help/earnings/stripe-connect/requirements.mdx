Before you start Stripe Connect onboarding, make sure you are ready for the information Stripe is likely to request.

## Core Requirements

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Producer Profile" icon="user">
    You need a producer profile to access **Finances** and connect a payout account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Supported Country" icon="globe">
    You must choose a country BeatPass currently supports in the onboarding list.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bank Account Context" icon="building-columns">
    BeatPass asks you to choose the country where you reside and have a bank account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stripe Verification" icon="id-card">
    Stripe may ask for identity, payout, tax, or business information depending on your setup.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Information Stripe Commonly Requests

| Category | Typical Examples |
|----------|------------------|
| **Personal details** | Legal name, date of birth, address, email, phone number |
| **Payout details** | Bank account details or other payout information Stripe allows for your country |
| **Identity verification** | Government-issued ID or other proof Stripe requests |
| **Tax or business details** | Additional information required by Stripe for your country or account type |

<Note>
  BeatPass does not maintain a permanent country-by-country document checklist in help docs because Stripe can change requirements. The live Stripe onboarding flow is the source of truth for what your account needs right now.
</Note>

## Country Rules That Matter

- Choose the country where you reside and have a bank account
- Read the country prompt carefully before confirming
- Treat the country choice as permanent for the completed setup flow
