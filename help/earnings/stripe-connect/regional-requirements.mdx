This page focuses on what BeatPass verifies directly in the current onboarding flow: the supported country list, the regional grouping, and the rules around country confirmation.

<Warning>
  Choose the country where you reside and have a bank account. BeatPass warns during onboarding that this country choice cannot be changed after setup is finalized.
</Warning>

## What Can Vary by Region

Even though BeatPass keeps the country list fixed in the UI, Stripe can ask for different details depending on the region or account type.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Identity Checks" icon="id-card">
    Some regions need more identity review than others. Stripe decides the exact documents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bank Detail Format" icon="building-columns">
    Different regions use different banking formats and payout rules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tax or Business Info" icon="file-lines">
    Stripe may ask for tax or business details depending on local requirements and the type of account you are creating.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  BeatPass does not publish a fixed country-by-country document matrix in help docs because those requirements are controlled by Stripe and can change over time.
</Info>
