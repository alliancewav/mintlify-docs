BeatPass uses Stripe Connect for producer payouts. It is the payout layer behind **Producer Finances**, subscription splits, and direct producer sales.

## What Stripe Connect Enables

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Track Sales" icon="shopping-bag">
    Producer sales move through Stripe once the buyer completes checkout.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscription Payouts" icon="repeat">
    Subscription revenue can only move to eligible producers with a working payout setup.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Producer Finances" icon="chart-bar">
    BeatPass uses Stripe-backed data to show balances, statuses, and payout history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Payout Flow" icon="chart-line">
    Once money reaches Stripe and your account is ready, Stripe handles the transfer to your payout destination.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What Matters Most Before You Start

- BeatPass uses **Stripe Express**, not a regular Stripe dashboard account
- You must choose a supported country during setup
- BeatPass asks you to confirm the country where you reside and have a bank account
- Incomplete setup can prevent payouts and can cause subscription splits to appear as **Excluded**

<Warning>
  If your payout setup is incomplete, BeatPass can still calculate activity, but it cannot safely treat you as payout-ready.
</Warning>

## Start From Finances

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Backstage">
    Click your **profile avatar** and open **Backstage**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Finances">
    Open **Finances** and click **Connect Stripe Account Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish the Stripe flow">
    Confirm your country and complete the Stripe-hosted onboarding process.
  </Step>
</Steps>
