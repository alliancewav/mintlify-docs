If your country is not shown in the BeatPass onboarding list, the country confirmation dialog currently surfaces four alternative ideas. This page documents those options at a high level without overstating provider approval, fees, or legal outcomes.

<Warning>
  BeatPass cannot guarantee approval for any alternative method. Eligibility, compliance requirements, fees, and availability are controlled by Stripe and the third-party provider you use.
</Warning>

## Options BeatPass Currently Surfaces

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stripe Atlas" icon="building">
    BeatPass highlights Stripe Atlas as a path for producers who want a formal US company setup tied to Stripe's ecosystem.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wise Multi-Currency Account" icon="chart-line">
    BeatPass references Wise for producers who need supported banking details and multi-currency handling.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payoneer Receiving Account" icon="money-check-dollar">
    BeatPass also references Payoneer as another payout-details option for some international creators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trusted Contact in a Supported Country" icon="users">
    BeatPass mentions this as a possible path, but it carries the highest legal, tax, and trust risk.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Important Rules Before You Proceed

- Use real information and only follow a setup path that Stripe accepts for your situation
- Do not assume BeatPass support text overrides Stripe's own rules
- Review the provider's current documentation before relying on it
- Understand who legally controls the payout account and who is responsible for taxes

<Note>
  The BeatPass dialog includes these alternatives as guidance, not as a promise that every producer will qualify for every option.
</Note>

## How to Choose Carefully

| Option | Best Fit |
|--------|----------|
| **Stripe Atlas** | Producers who want a formal company-based path |
| **Wise** | Producers exploring supported banking details with multi-currency support |
| **Payoneer** | Producers who prefer another receiving-account provider |
| **Trusted Contact** | Only when the legal and tax responsibility is clear and fully agreed |

## Before Restarting Onboarding

1. Re-check the current BeatPass supported country list
2. Confirm that Stripe accepts the path you want to use
3. Read the provider's own documentation for current requirements and costs
4. Keep your legal, tax, and payout responsibilities clear before submitting anything to Stripe
