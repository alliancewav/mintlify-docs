BeatPass uses <Tooltip tip="Stripe's platform-managed payout account used to receive money from marketplaces like BeatPass. It is different from a regular Stripe dashboard account." cta="Learn how to sign in" href="/help/earnings/stripe-connect/accessing-stripe-dashboard">Stripe Express</Tooltip> for producer payouts. This guide follows the current BeatPass setup flow.

## Before You Start

- Make sure you have a producer profile
- Be ready to confirm the country where you reside and have a bank account
- Have your legal identity and payout details ready for Stripe

</Card>

## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Producer Finances">
    Click your **profile avatar**, open **Backstage**, then go to **Finances**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start onboarding">
    Click **Connect Stripe Account Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your country">
    In **Confirm Your Country**, choose the country where you reside and have a bank account. BeatPass warns that this choice cannot be changed after setup is finalized.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to Stripe">
    BeatPass redirects you to Stripe's hosted onboarding flow for your Stripe Express account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the requested details">
    Stripe asks for the information it needs for your country and payout setup. This can include identity, address, bank, tax, or business details depending on your situation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to BeatPass">
    After Stripe finishes or pauses the flow, return to **Finances** and review your current status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What BeatPass Shows After Setup

After onboarding, BeatPass can show whether the account is:

- **Connected**
- **Charges Enabled**
- **Payouts Enabled**

If Stripe still needs more information, BeatPass can continue showing setup or warning prompts even after the first onboarding session.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I return to BeatPass and setup is still incomplete?">
    Stripe probably still needs more information. Go back to **Finances** and follow any remaining prompts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I picked the wrong country?">
    Country selection is sensitive because BeatPass warns that it cannot be changed after setup is finalized. If you caught the issue early, stop and review the situation before continuing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this the same as a regular Stripe account?">
    No. BeatPass creates a Stripe Express account for marketplace payouts. It is separate from a regular account at `dashboard.stripe.com`.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where do I sign in later?">
    Use Stripe Express at [connect.stripe.com/express_login](https://connect.stripe.com/express_login).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
