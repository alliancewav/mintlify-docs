You increase earnings on BeatPass by improving the parts of the system you can influence: fresh uploads, play activity, catalog quality, and payout readiness.

## Keep the Catalog Active

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Upload Consistently">
    Newer tracks receive a stronger recency factor, so steady uploads help keep your catalog competitive.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prioritize Quality">
    One strong track with real play activity can outperform many tracks that do not get used.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Drive Real Plays

The popularity portion of the formula responds to play activity, especially early growth.

### Practical Ways to Help

- Share new releases where your listeners already are
- Promote tracks early, while recency is strongest
- Re-surface older tracks when they still fit your audience
- Make sure listeners can actually find your best work

## Keep Metadata Clean

BeatPass works better when your tracks are easy to understand and browse.

- Use accurate titles
- Keep genres and descriptions clear
- Fill in track details consistently when available
- Organize your catalog so your strongest work is easy to discover

## Collaborate Intentionally

Collaborations can help you reach more listeners, but remember that contribution is split between credited producers.

Ask two questions:

1. Will this collaboration increase reach or quality enough to justify the split?
2. Does the partnership improve the chances of real play activity?

## Do Not Neglect Direct Sales

Subscription payouts are only one side of BeatPass earnings. Direct sales can still be your strongest revenue source for premium tracks.

<Info>
  Exclusive-only tracks do not contribute to the subscription pool, but they can still earn through direct purchases.
</Info>

## Stay Payout-Ready

Your earnings flow is stronger when payout setup never becomes the blocker.

**Checklist**

- [ ] Stripe Connect setup completed
- [ ] Required verification submitted
- [ ] Payouts still enabled
- [ ] Warning states in **Finances** reviewed promptly

## Track the Right Signals

Use BeatPass tools for the right job:

- **Producer Finances** for payout status, balances, and transaction history
- Producer analytics and contribution-related views for longer-term performance trends

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
