To receive money from BeatPass, you need more than just earnings. Your producer profile and Stripe Connect setup both need to be in a payout-ready state.

## What You Need

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Producer Profile" icon="user">
    You must have a producer profile that can use producer-facing features like **Finances**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stripe Connect Setup" icon="wallet">
    Your payout account must be connected through BeatPass.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Confirmed Country" icon="globe">
    During setup, you must confirm the country where you reside and have a bank account.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Stripe Readiness Signals

BeatPass surfaces the most important readiness indicators in the payout flow:

| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|----------------|
| **Connected** | BeatPass is linked to your Stripe Connect account |
| **Charges Enabled** | Stripe has enabled incoming payment capability for the account |
| **Payouts Enabled** | Stripe has enabled payouts from the account |
| **Details Submitted** | Stripe has received the required information for the current verification stage |

<Info>
  BeatPass focuses the main UI on connection and payout readiness. Stripe can still ask for more details later if regulations or account reviews require it.
</Info>

## What Happens If You Are Not Eligible

If a subscription payout runs while your account is not ready:

1. The transaction can appear as **Excluded**
2. Your share can be redistributed among eligible producers
3. Future payouts can work normally once your setup is complete

<Warning>
  Excluded subscription payouts are not automatically recreated as back pay later. It is important to finish payout setup before you rely on subscription earnings.
</Warning>

## How to Check Your Status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Producer Finances">
    Go to **Backstage** → **Finances**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the dashboard state">
    If BeatPass still shows the onboarding panel, your setup is not complete yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review warnings and statuses">
    Look for signals such as **Setup required**, **Excluded**, or any prompt asking for more information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Become Eligible

1. Click **Connect Stripe Account Now** in **Finances**
2. Confirm your country carefully
3. Complete the Stripe-hosted onboarding flow
4. Add the payout and identity information Stripe requests
5. Return to BeatPass and finish any remaining prompts if Stripe still needs more information

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need Stripe Connect for both track sales and subscription payouts?">
    Yes. Stripe Connect is the payout path BeatPass uses for producer earnings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I still earn before setup is finished?">
    Activity can still happen, but you are not safely payout-ready until your Stripe Connect setup is complete.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why do I see Excluded in Transactions?">
    That means a subscription split ran while your payout setup was not eligible at that time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I fix eligibility later?">
    Yes for future payouts. Complete your Stripe setup and keep it active so later splits can process normally.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
