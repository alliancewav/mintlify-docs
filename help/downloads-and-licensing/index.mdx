BeatPass provides a comprehensive licensing system that protects both creators and users. When you download a beat, you receive a license certificate that grants specific usage rights based on your license type.

<Info>
  **Subscribers:** Download unlimited beats with non-exclusive licenses through your BeatPass membership.
</Info>

---

## License Types

BeatPass offers two types of licenses:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Non-Exclusive License" icon="users">
    **Included with subscription**

    - Multiple artists can license the same beat
    - Perpetual usage rights
    - Monetize on streaming platforms
    - Live performances (up to 50 attendees)
    - Attribution required
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exclusive License" icon="crown">
    **One-time purchase**

    - Only one artist owns the beat
    - Full ownership transfer
    - Unlimited distribution and streaming
    - Content ID registration allowed
    - Stems included
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse & Select">
    Find a beat you like and click Download or Purchase Exclusive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audio Fingerprinting">
    The beat's unique audio fingerprint (BeatPassID) is captured and stored.
  </Step>
  <Step title="License Generation">
    A license certificate is automatically generated with your user details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Files">
    Receive your files (WAV, MP3) along with your license certificate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create & Distribute">
    Use the beat in your music according to license terms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

### BeatPassID (Audio Fingerprinting)

Every track on BeatPass has a unique acoustic fingerprint that:

- Uniquely identifies the audio content
- Cannot be forged or duplicated
- Links your license to the specific audio
- Enables license verification

<Card title="Learn More About BeatPassID" icon="fingerprint" href="/help/downloads-and-licensing/beatpass-id">
  Understand how audio fingerprinting protects you and the producer.
</Card>

### License Verification

Anyone can verify a license certificate's authenticity:

- Visit the verification URL on your certificate
- BeatPass confirms the license is real and active
- Shows license status, track info, and verification count
