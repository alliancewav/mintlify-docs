This page covers the standard BeatPass download flow. It explains who can download, what happens after you click **Download**, and how licenses are generated.

<Note>
  Standard downloads require a paid subscription. Exclusive checkout is covered separately in [Purchasing Exclusive](/help/downloads-and-licensing/exclusive-licenses/purchasing-exclusive).
</Note>

## Before You Click Download

BeatPass checks several things before a download can start.

### Access Rules

- You must be signed in
- Your account must have the download permission (paid plans only)
- Your subscription cannot be overdue
- The track must still be available for non-exclusive download

### Exceptions

| Scenario | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| You own the track | Download is always allowed — no license is generated |
| You purchased exclusive rights | Download is still allowed even after the track is marked sold |
| The track is sold exclusively | New non-exclusive downloads are blocked for everyone else |
| The track is exclusive-only | Standard download is not available unless you are the owner or purchaser |

## The Download Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Download">
    On an eligible track, select **Download**. BeatPass opens the **Download Track** confirmation modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the download">
    Select **Download** in the modal. BeatPass re-checks your access before starting the file transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The track file downloads">
    The original uploaded audio file downloads to your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="License is auto-generated">
    For non-owners, BeatPass automatically generates a non-exclusive license certificate on download and stores it in **Your library** → **Licenses**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post-download modal may appear">
    After the file downloads, a **Download Complete** modal can appear with a link to view your license. If you already had an active license for that track, it shows **License Already Available** with a **View Licenses** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to Find Your Licenses

Open **Your library** → **Licenses** to view all license records tied to your account.

From that page you can:

- Search by track name, artist name, licensee name, or license ID
- Open the printable certificate view
- Use **Verify License** to open the public verification page
- View licenses granted to other users if you are a track owner

## Track Availability

| Track state | Standard download available? |
| --- | --- |
| **Non-exclusive only** | Yes, if your account has download access |
| **Both (non-exclusive + exclusive)** | Yes, unless the track has been sold exclusively |
| **Exclusive only** | No — purchase required |
| **Sold** | No — blocked for all users except the owner and exclusive buyer |

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Download button is not visible" icon="eye-slash" defaultOpen>
    The button is hidden when you are not signed in, lack the download permission, or the track is not eligible. A paid subscription is required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see an overdue subscription message" icon="credit-card">
    BeatPass blocks downloads when your subscription payment is overdue. Resolve the billing issue first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I own the track but have no license" icon="user">
    Owners can download their own tracks freely. No license certificate is generated for self-downloads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The track downloaded but I need my certificate" icon="file-lines">
    Open **Your library** → **Licenses**. The license is auto-generated on download for non-owners and should appear there.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
