This page explains the current exclusive checkout flow in BeatPass. It covers requirements, payment behavior, and what happens immediately after a successful purchase.

<Info>
  Exclusive purchases currently use Stripe card checkout and supported purchase currencies are `USD`, `EUR`, `GBP`, and `CAD`.
</Info>

## Before you start

You need:

- A signed-in BeatPass account
- A track that is still eligible for exclusive purchase
- A producer who has payment setup completed for that track
- A payment card accepted by Stripe

### Common reasons purchase is blocked

- The track is already sold
- The track is not configured for exclusive purchase
- You already purchased it
- You are trying to buy your own track
- The producer does not have the required payment setup

## Current purchase flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the track page">
    Find a track with a **Buy Exclusive** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the price and terms">
    Open the purchase dialog and use **View License Terms** to read the exclusive terms for that track.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the price breakdown">
    BeatPass shows the producer price, the platform fee, and the total amount in the selected currency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe checkout">
    Enter your card details in the Stripe payment form and confirm the purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for success confirmation">
    On success, BeatPass generates the exclusive license, marks the track as sold, and shows the success flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the license later">
    Your exclusive license is stored in <a href="https://open.beatpass.ca/library/licenses">https://open.beatpass.ca/library/licenses</a>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What success looks like

After a successful payment:

- The exclusive license is created
- The track is marked as sold
- BeatPass attempts an immediate track download
- The purchase is recorded in the selected currency
- The certificate becomes available in **Library** -> **Licenses**

## Pricing and currencies

| Pricing item | Current behavior |
| --- | --- |
| **Track price** | Set by the producer |
| **Platform fee** | Added on top of the producer price |
| **Supported currencies** | `USD`, `EUR`, `GBP`, `CAD` |
| **Payment provider** | Stripe |

<Note>
  BeatPass shows the total before you confirm payment. Do not assume every exclusive track uses the same price or currency.
</Note>

## What to expect if payment fails

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Card was declined" icon="credit-card" defaultOpen>
    No license is created and the track stays in its prior state. You can try again with a different card or after fixing the payment issue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Track became unavailable during checkout" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Exclusive sales are first-come, first-served. If another user completed the purchase first, the track becomes unavailable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Payment succeeded but I do not see the license yet" icon="file-certificate">
    Check **Library** -> **Licenses** first. If it still does not appear, use the support path with the purchase details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Refund and billing issues

If you have a charge problem after purchase, use the current BeatPass billing/support path rather than relying on old timing promises. Do not assume a fixed refund window unless the billing policy for your account says so.
