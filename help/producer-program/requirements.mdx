Before applying to the BeatPass Producer Program, make sure you have the information BeatPass currently asks for in Backstage. The exact fields change slightly depending on whether you are creating a new producer profile, claiming an existing one, or getting verified.

---

## Basic Requirements

### BeatPass Account

You need a registered BeatPass account. If you do not have one yet, create it first at [https://open.beatpass.ca/register](https://open.beatpass.ca/register).

### Link to Your Beats

For **Become a Producer** and **Claim Existing Artist** requests, you must provide a public URL where the team can review your work. Common examples include:

- SoundCloud profile or playlist
- YouTube channel or playlist
- Spotify artist page
- BeatStars, Airbit, or similar platform
- Any public URL where your beats can be heard

<Note>
  BeatPass uses this link for quality review. Make sure it is public, loads correctly, and clearly represents your work.
</Note>

### Real Name

The request form requires your real **First name** and **Last name**. This is used for identity review and later payout onboarding if you connect Stripe.

---

## Rights & Ownership

### Original Content

You must have the right to upload the music you plan to publish on BeatPass. That means one of the following is true:

- You created the beats yourself
- You have proper licensing for any samples you used
- You have written permission to distribute the music

### Non-Exclusive License Agreement

By applying, you confirm that BeatPass can host and distribute your music on the platform under a **non-exclusive license**. You keep ownership of your work.

---

## Upload Commitment

The current request form includes a checkbox where you confirm that you will upload at least **2 beats per month**. Treat this as part of the application agreement, not as general marketing copy.

---

## Optional but Recommended

### Instagram Handle

Providing your Instagram handle can help BeatPass verify your identity and connect your request to your public producer presence.

### Record Label or Company

If you are signed to a label or represent a company, you can include this information. It is optional.

### Profile Picture

The current request form only lets you upload a profile picture in the **Claim Existing Artist** flow. The **Become a Producer** form shows the picture area, but it is currently disabled.

### Supporting Materials

You can attach additional files such as:

- Press photos
- Label paperwork
- Screenshots or documents that support your claim
- Any other verification material relevant to your request

---

## Terms Agreement

You must agree to:

- [Terms of Service](https://open.beatpass.ca/terms)
- [Privacy Policy](https://open.beatpass.ca/privacy)
- Producer program requirements covering ownership, licensing, and the upload commitment

|-------------|:-----------------:|:-------------:|:------------:|

| Discord membership | No | No | Yes |

---

## What Happens If You Do Not Meet Requirements

If your request is missing required information or does not meet review standards, BeatPass can deny it. You can then:

- Update your materials
- Provide stronger supporting information
- Reapply when you are ready

If your producer access is revoked after a previous approval, new submissions are blocked. In that case, use [https://open.beatpass.ca/contact](https://open.beatpass.ca/contact) to submit an appeal for admin review.

<Warning>
  If a profile shows **Already Claimed** during the claim flow, you cannot finish that request through the normal form. Use [https://open.beatpass.ca/contact](https://open.beatpass.ca/contact) if you believe the profile should belong to you.
</Warning>
