Before applying to the BeatPass Producer Program, make sure you have everything needed for your application.

---

## Basic Requirements

### BeatPass Account

You need a registered BeatPass account. If you don't have one, [create an account](https://open.beatpass.ca/register) first.

### Link to Your Beats

You must provide a link to your existing beats for quality review. This can be:

- SoundCloud profile or playlist
- YouTube channel or playlist
- Spotify artist page
- BeatStars, Airbit, or similar platform
- Any public URL where your beats can be heard

<Note>
This link is used to verify the quality of your production. Make sure the link is public and accessible.
</Note>

### Real Name

You must provide your real first and last name. This information is used for identity verification and, if you set up payouts, for payment processing.

---

## Rights & Ownership

### Original Content

You must own all rights to the beats you upload. This means:

- You created the beats yourself, OR
- You have proper licensing for all samples used, OR
- You have written permission to distribute the music

### Non-Exclusive License Agreement

By applying, you agree to grant BeatPass a **non-exclusive license** to distribute your music on the platform. You keep full ownership of your work.

---

## Upload Commitment

When applying, you confirm that you will upload at least **2 beats per month** to maintain an active catalog. This helps ensure the platform stays fresh with new content.

---

## Optional but Recommended

### Instagram Handle

Providing your Instagram handle helps verify your identity and can connect listeners to your social presence.

### Record Label or Company

If you're signed to a label or represent a company, you can include this information. It's optional.

### Profile Picture

A professional profile picture helps build trust with potential listeners and licensees.

### Supporting Materials

You can attach additional documents like:

- Press photos
- Label documentation
- Other verification materials

---

## Terms Agreement

You must agree to:

- [Terms of Service](https://open.beatpass.ca/pages/terms-of-service)
- [Privacy Policy](https://open.beatpass.ca/pages/privacy-policy)
- Producer program requirements (ownership, licensing, upload commitment)

## What Happens If You Don't Meet Requirements

If your application is missing required information or doesn't meet quality standards, the BeatPass team will deny the request. You can:

- Update your materials
- Provide additional information
- Apply again once you're ready
