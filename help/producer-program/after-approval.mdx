Once your request is approved, you gain access to the Producer Dashboard and upload tools. This page covers the practical next steps so your account, catalog, and payouts are set up correctly.

---

## What Changes After Approval

Once your request is approved:

- Your account gains **producer permissions**
- You can upload beats and collections
- You can configure licensing for your tracks
- You become eligible for producer earnings
- You can connect Stripe for payouts

---

## First Steps

### 1. Access Your Producer Dashboard

Click your **profile avatar** and select **Backstage** to open your Producer Dashboard. The sidebar includes Dashboard, Finances, Activity, Progress, Leaderboards, My Beats, and My Collections.

<Tip>
  The **Backstage** menu item only appears after approval when you have producer permissions.
</Tip>

### 2. Complete Your Producer Profile

Make your profile more trustworthy and easier to discover:

- Upload a high-quality profile picture
- Add a bio describing your style and sound
- Link your social accounts
- Add photos to your about section if you use them

### 3. Set Up Payouts

To receive earnings, connect Stripe:

1. Open your producer **Finances** area
2. Start the Stripe Connect onboarding flow
3. Provide the identity, tax, and bank details Stripe requests for your country

<Note>
  You can upload immediately after approval, but BeatPass needs a completed Stripe Connect setup before it can pay you out.
</Note>

## Upload Your First Beat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the upload page">
    Start from your producer workspace and go to the upload tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your beat file">
    Drag and drop or choose your audio file. The current producer upload flow only accepts **WAV** files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the required fields">
    Add cover art, track name, artists, BPM, key, scale, and any helpful metadata such as genres, tags, and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a licensing model">
    Pick **Standard Licensing**, **Standard + Exclusive**, or **Exclusive Only**. If you enable exclusive licensing, add a contact email and review the license terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Wait for the fingerprint check to finish, then click **Save Track**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Build Your Catalog

### Cover Art Is Required

New tracks and new collections both need cover art before they can be saved. BeatPass supports custom uploads and genre-matched cover art presets.

### Complete the Core Metadata

For each beat, make sure you fill in:

- **Track Name**
- **Artists**
- **Key**
- **Scale**
- **BPM** (60–200)

Descriptions, genres, tags, collection assignments, and sample-safe status are not always required, but they make your catalog easier to discover and manage.

### Use Collections Where It Helps

If you release beat packs, tapes, or themed drops, use **Create Collection** on the upload page. Collection tracks inherit the collection's artists, genres, and tags when they are first uploaded.

## Understand Your Earnings

As an approved producer, you can earn from:

- **Subscription contribution** tied to BeatPass listening activity
- **Exclusive sales** when buyers purchase your configured exclusive option

For exclusive sales, the buyer pays the platform fee on top of your listed price. Your producer finances area records the sale, payout readiness, and Stripe Connect status.

---

## XP You Start Earning Automatically

BeatPass currently awards:

| Action | XP |
| --- | --- |
| Upload a track | +100 |
| Get a play | +1 |
| Add Metadata | +50 |
| Credit a collaboration | +25 |

BeatPass tracks this automatically. You do not need to claim XP manually.

## Get Verified

If you want the verified badge and its benefits:

1. Go to the Producer Program page
2. Click **Apply to Join**
3. On the request selector, choose **Get Verified**
4. Follow the Discord community verification process

Verified producer features include the blue verified badge, Audio Recon access, producer dashboard notifications (New Subscribers, Analytics Reports, Payout Updates), and automatic beat request matching notifications.

See [Get Verified](/help/producer-program/application-types/get-verified) for the complete process.

## Promote Your Beats

Once your beats are live:

- Share links to your profile and tracks on social media
- Add your BeatPass profile link to your bios
- Respond to beat requests that match your style
- Keep older tracks updated if you improve metadata or artwork
