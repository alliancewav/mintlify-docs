The BeatPass Producer Program lets you upload your beats, build your catalog, and earn money when members download or license your music. Whether you're starting fresh or already have tracks on the platform, there's a path to get you set up.

---

## Why Join?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Keep 100%" icon="wallet">
    You keep 100% of your set price. Buyers pay a 15% platform fee on top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unlimited Uploads" icon="cloud-upload">
    Upload as many beats as you want with no monthly limits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keep Your Rights" icon="shield-check">
    You retain ownership of your music. BeatPass gets a non-exclusive license.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Submit your application with your producer name, a link to your beats, and basic information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Our team reviews your application to ensure quality and legitimacy. Most applications are processed quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Earning">
    Once approved, you can upload beats, access your Producer Dashboard, and start earning from plays and downloads.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Three Ways to Join

Depending on your situation, you'll choose one of three application types:

| Application Type | For Who | What It Does |
|------------------|---------|--------------|
| **Become a Producer** | New producers without a profile | Creates a new producer profile linked to your account |
| **Claim Artist Profile** | Producers whose music is already on BeatPass | Connects an existing artist profile to your account |
| **Get Verified** | Existing producers who want verification | Adds a verified badge to your profile |

## What You'll Need

Before applying, make sure you have:

- A BeatPass account
- A link to your beats (SoundCloud, YouTube, or similar)
- Your real first and last name
- Rights to the music you'll upload

See [Requirements](/help/producer-program/requirements) for full details.

## Quick Facts

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Your earnings** | You keep 100% of your set price |
| **Platform fee** | 15% added on top (paid by buyer) |
| **Upload limits** | Unlimited |
| **Upfront costs** | None |
| **Commitment** | At least 2 beats per month |
| **Rights** | You keep ownership, grant BeatPass non-exclusive license |
| **Approval time** | Most applications are reviewed quickly |
