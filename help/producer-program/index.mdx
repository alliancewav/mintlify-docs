The BeatPass Producer Program gives approved producers access to upload tools, licensing controls, catalog management, and producer earnings. Whether you're starting from scratch or claiming a profile that already exists, this guide covers the current application flow.

---

## Why Join?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Keep 100%" icon="wallet">
    On exclusive sales, you keep your listed price. The buyer pays the 12% + \$3 platform fee on top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unlimited Uploads" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    The current producer tools do not impose a fixed catalog cap for beats or collections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keep Your Rights" icon="shield-check">
    You keep ownership of your music. BeatPass receives a non-exclusive license to host and distribute it on-platform.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Producer Program page">
    Go to [https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-program](https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-program) and click **Apply to Join** (if logged in) or **Apply Now** (if not).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your request type">
    On the **Join the Producer Program** selector page, choose: **Become a Producer** (new profile), **Claim Existing Artist** (existing profile), or **Get Verified** (if you already have producer access).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Provide your real name, a public link to your beats, and any supporting materials BeatPass needs for review. For verification, you'll be directed to Discord instead of a form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved and start uploading">
    Once approved, access your Producer Dashboard to upload beats, manage your catalog, configure licensing, and connect payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application Paths

The request selector at [https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests](https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests) shows different options based on your account state.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Become a Producer" icon="user-plus" color="#22c55e" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/become-producer">
    <Badge color="green">New Profile</Badge>
    Shows when you don't have a producer profile yet. Create a new producer identity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claim Existing Artist" icon="circle-check" color="#3b82f6" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist">
    <Badge color="blue">Existing Profile</Badge>
    Available to all users. Claim a producer profile that already exists on BeatPass.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Get Verified" icon="circle-check" color="#8b5cf6" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/get-verified">
    <Badge color="purple">Discord Verification</Badge>
    Shows only if you already have producer access. Directs to Discord for manual verification.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The request selector dynamically adjusts: users without producer profiles see **Become a Producer** as the primary option, while existing producers see **Get Verified** instead.
</Note>

## What You'll Need

Before applying, make sure you have:

- A BeatPass account
- A public link to your beats
- Your real first and last name
- Rights to the music you plan to upload

See [Requirements](/help/producer-program/requirements) for the full checklist.
