The BeatPass Producer Program gives approved producers access to upload tools, licensing controls, catalog management, and producer earnings. Whether you are starting from scratch or claiming a profile that already exists, the docs in this section are aligned to the current Backstage flow.

---

## Why Join?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Keep 100%" icon="wallet">
    On exclusive sales, you keep your listed price. The buyer pays the 12% + \$3 platform fee on top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unlimited Uploads" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    The current producer tools do not impose a fixed catalog cap for beats or collections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keep Your Rights" icon="shield-check">
    You keep ownership of your music. BeatPass receives a non-exclusive license to host and distribute it on-platform.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the right request type">
    Open **Backstage** and choose the path that matches your situation: create a new producer profile, claim an existing one, or start the verification flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Provide your real name, a public link to your beats, and any supporting materials BeatPass needs for review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved and start uploading">
    Once approved, your Backstage workspace becomes your producer area for uploads, catalog management, finances, and licensing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Application Paths

BeatPass supports three request types overall, but the options you see in **Backstage** depend on your current account state.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Become a Producer" icon="user-plus" color="#22c55e" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/become-producer">
    <Badge color="green">New Profile</Badge>
    Use this when you do not already have a producer profile on BeatPass.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claim Existing Artist" icon="circle-check" color="#3b82f6" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist">
    <Badge color="blue">Existing Profile</Badge>
    Use this when your catalog or producer page already exists and you need account access to it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Get Verified" icon="circle-check" color="#8b5cf6" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/get-verified">
    <Badge color="purple">Discord Flow</Badge>
    Use this only after you already have a working producer profile. It opens a Discord-based verification path instead of a standard form.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  If your current Backstage screen shows **Get Verified** instead of **Become a Producer**, BeatPass already recognizes your account as having a producer profile.
</Note>

## What You'll Need

Before applying, make sure you have:

- A BeatPass account
- A public link to your beats
- Your real first and last name
- Rights to the music you plan to upload

See [Requirements](/help/producer-program/requirements) for the full checklist.
