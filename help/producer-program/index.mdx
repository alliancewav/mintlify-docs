The BeatPass Producer Program gives approved producers access to upload tools, licensing controls, catalog management, and producer earnings. Whether you're starting from scratch or claiming a profile that already exists, this guide covers the application flow.

---

## Why Join?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Keep 100%" icon="dollar-sign">
    On exclusive sales, you keep your listed price. The buyer pays the 12% + $3 CAD platform fee on top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unlimited Uploads" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    The producer tools do not impose a fixed catalog cap for beats or collections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keep Your Rights" icon="shield-halved">
    You keep ownership of your music. BeatPass receives a non-exclusive license to host and distribute it on-platform.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Producer Program page">
    Click **Apply to Join** (if logged in) or **Apply Now** (if not logged in) on the Producer Program page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your request type">
    On the selector page, choose **Become a Producer** (new profile), **Claim Existing Artist** (existing profile), or **Get Verified** (if you already have producer access).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Provide your real name, a public link to your beats, and any supporting materials. For verification, you are directed to Discord instead of a form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved and start uploading">
    Once approved, access your Producer Dashboard to upload beats, manage your catalog, configure licensing, and connect payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application Paths

The request selector shows different options based on your account state:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Become a Producer" icon="user-plus" color="#22c55e" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/become-producer">
    Create a new producer profile. Shows when you don't have a producer profile yet.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claim Existing Artist" icon="circle-check" color="#3b82f6" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist">
    Claim a producer profile that already exists on BeatPass. Available to all users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Get Verified" icon="circle-check" color="#8b5cf6" href="/help/producer-program/application-types/get-verified">
    Get the verified badge through Discord. Shows only if you already have producer access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
The request selector adjusts automatically: users without producer profiles see **Become a Producer** as the primary option, while existing producers see **Get Verified** instead.
</Note>

## What You'll Need

Before applying, make sure you have:

- A BeatPass account
- A public link to your beats
- Your real first and last name
- Rights to the music you plan to upload

See [Requirements](/help/producer-program/requirements) for the full checklist.

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Your earnings** | Keep your listed exclusive price |
| **Platform fee** | 12% + $3 CAD, paid by the buyer |
| **Upload limits** | Unlimited |
| **Upload commitment** | 2 beats per month (application agreement) |
| **Your rights** | You keep ownership |
| **Upfront costs** | $0 |

---

## Submission Rules

- You can only have one request under review at a time
- If your current request is **Pending**, new submissions are blocked until a decision is made
- If your previous producer access was **Revoked**, you must appeal through contact support
