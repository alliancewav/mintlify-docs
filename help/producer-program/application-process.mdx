This guide walks through the current Backstage application flow for producers. BeatPass uses three stages: **Apply**, **Review**, and **Start Earning**.

---

## Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Choose the right request type and complete the information BeatPass asks for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    The BeatPass team reviews your request for quality, legitimacy, and ownership.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Earning">
    Once approved, use your producer tools to upload beats, manage your catalog, and connect payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Application Type

Before filling out the form, select the right application for your situation:

| If you... | Choose... |
|-----------|-----------|
| Do not have a producer profile yet | **Become a Producer** |
| Have music already on BeatPass but no account access | **Claim Existing Artist** |
| Already have a producer profile and want verification | **Get Verified** |

Open your **profile avatar** menu and select **Backstage**.

<Note>
  The selector screen changes based on your current account state. Accounts without a producer profile see **Become a Producer**. Accounts that already have one see **Get Verified** instead. **Claim Existing Artist** is available as its own option.
</Note>

---

## Step 2: Fill Out the Application

### Profile Setup

Depending on your application type:

- **Become a Producer** - Enter your **Producer Name**
- **Claim Existing Artist** - Search for and select the producer profile you want to claim
- **Get Verified** - No standard form is shown; BeatPass sends you to the Discord verification page

For claim requests, BeatPass also checks whether the selected profile is **Available** or **Already Claimed** before you continue.

### Personal Details

Provide your real information:

- **First name** (required)
- **Last name** (required)
- **Company or label** (optional)

For **Claim Existing Artist** requests, you also select your relationship to the profile, such as **Artist**, **Manager**, **Producer**, **Label**, or another supported role.

### Supporting Materials

Provide links and files to support your request:

- **Link to your beats** (required for become/claim) - A URL where your music can be reviewed
- **Instagram handle** (optional)
- **Attachments** (optional) - Additional documents or proof

### Discord Community

The request form includes a **Join Community** section with a Discord button. This is optional for standard applications, but Discord participation is required for **Get Verified**.

### Agreement

Before submitting, you must confirm:

1. Agreement to [Terms of Service](https://open.beatpass.ca/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://open.beatpass.ca/privacy)
2. Ownership of all beats you will upload
3. Granting BeatPass a non-exclusive license to distribute your music
4. Commitment to uploading at least 2 beats per month

---

## Step 3: Submit Your Application

Once you have filled out the required fields and checked both agreement boxes, click **Submit Application**.

You will be taken to a confirmation page showing that your request was received.

<Note>
  You can only have one active application under review at a time. If your current request is **Pending**, BeatPass blocks new submissions until a decision is made.
</Note>

---

## Step 4: Wait for Review

The BeatPass team reviews applications to ensure:

- The quality of your beats meets platform standards
- The information you provided is accurate
- You are a legitimate producer or authorized representative

The request-submitted page currently shows an **Application Review (24-48 hours)** estimate, but treat that as guidance rather than a guaranteed SLA. You will receive a decision by email.

---

## Step 5: Receive Your Decision

### If Approved

You will receive an email confirming your approval. You can then:

- Start uploading beats and collections
- Connect Stripe if you want payouts
- Edit your profile and catalog
- Configure licensing for your tracks

See [After Approval](/help/producer-program/after-approval) for next steps.

### If Denied

If your request is not approved, you will receive an email explaining why. Common reasons include:

- Beat quality does not meet standards
- Missing or incomplete information
- Ownership or identity could not be verified

You can address the issue and apply again later.

### If Access Is Revoked Later

If your producer access is revoked after approval, you cannot submit a new request directly. Use [https://open.beatpass.ca/contact](https://open.beatpass.ca/contact) so the admin team can review your appeal.

---

## Application Status

You can check your request state from **Backstage**.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Request submitted and waiting for review |
| **Approved** | Producer access is active |
| **Denied** | Request was not approved |
| **Revoked** | Producer access was removed and requires an appeal |

---

## If You See a Submission Block

Before showing request options, Backstage checks whether you are currently allowed to submit.

- **Pending block** - BeatPass tells you your request is already under review and asks you to wait for the decision email before submitting again
- **Revoked block** - BeatPass tells you your producer access was revoked and provides a **Contact Support** path to [https://open.beatpass.ca/contact](https://open.beatpass.ca/contact)

This prevents duplicate requests and keeps appeal handling in one place.

## Tips for a Strong Application

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a quality beats link">
    Share a link that showcases your best work. Make sure the link is public and loads properly.
  </Accordion>

    Accurate personal information helps the review process and makes later payout onboarding easier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Add your Instagram">
    Social verification can help confirm your identity and strengthen your request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Join Discord">

  </Accordion>
