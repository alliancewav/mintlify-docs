This guide walks through the Producer Program application flow. BeatPass uses three stages: **Apply**, **Review**, and **Start Earning**.

---

## Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Choose the right request type and complete the required information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    The BeatPass team reviews your request for quality, legitimacy, and ownership.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Earning">
    Once approved, use your producer tools to upload beats, manage your catalog, and connect payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 1: Start Your Application

1. Go to the Producer Program page
2. Click **Apply to Join** (if logged in) or **Apply Now** (if not logged in)
3. You arrive at the request selector

### Choose Your Application Type

| If you... | Choose... |
| --- | --- |
| Don't have a producer profile yet | **Become a Producer** |
| Have music on BeatPass but no account access | **Claim Existing Artist** |
| Already have a producer profile and want verification | **Get Verified** |

<Warning>
The **Backstage** menu item in your profile dropdown goes to the Producer Dashboard, which requires existing producer access. It does not lead to the application page. Always use the Producer Program page to start applications.
</Warning>

---

## Step 2: Fill Out the Application

### Profile Setup

Depending on your request type:

- **Become a Producer** — Enter your **Producer Name**
- **Claim Existing Artist** — Search for and select the producer profile you want to claim
- **Get Verified** — The screen shows **Verify Through Discord** with a **Join Discord Community** button (no submission form)

For claim requests, BeatPass checks whether the selected profile is **Available** or **Already Claimed** before you continue.

### Personal Details

- **First name** (required)
- **Last name** (required)
- **Company or label** (optional)

For **Claim Existing Artist** requests, you also select your relationship to the profile (e.g. Artist, Manager, Producer, Label).

### Supporting Materials

- **Link to your beats** (required for Become / Claim) — a public URL where your music can be reviewed
- **Instagram handle** (optional)
- **Attachments** (optional) — additional documents or proof

### Discord Community

The form includes a **Join Community** section with a Discord button. This is optional for standard applications but required for **Get Verified**.

### Agreement

Before submitting, you must confirm:

1. Agreement to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
2. Ownership of all beats you will upload
3. Granting BeatPass a non-exclusive license to distribute your music
4. Commitment to uploading at least 2 beats per month

---

## Step 3: Submit Your Application

Click **Submit Application** once you have completed the required fields and checked both agreement boxes. You are taken to a confirmation page showing that your request was received.

<Note>
You can only have one active application under review at a time. If your current request is **Pending**, new submissions are blocked until a decision is made.
</Note>

---

## Step 4: Wait for Review

The BeatPass team reviews applications to check:

- Beat quality meets platform standards
- Information provided is accurate
- You are a legitimate producer or authorized representative

The submitted page shows an estimated review time of **1–3 business days**, but timing can vary.

---

## Step 5: Receive Your Decision

### If Approved

You receive an approval email and can then:

- Start uploading beats and collections
- Connect Stripe for payouts
- Edit your profile and catalog
- Configure licensing for your tracks

See [After Approval](/help/producer-program/after-approval) for next steps.

### If Denied

You receive an email explaining why. You can address the issue and apply again later.

### If Access Is Revoked Later

If your producer access is revoked after approval, you cannot submit a new request directly. Contact support so the team can review your appeal.

---

## Application Status

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Pending** | Request submitted and waiting for review |
| **Approved** | Producer access is active |
| **Denied** | Request was not approved |
| **Revoked** | Producer access was removed — requires an appeal |

## Submission Blocks

Before showing request options, the selector checks whether you are allowed to submit:

- **Pending block** — Your request is already under review. Wait for the decision before submitting again
- **Revoked block** — Your producer access was revoked. Contact support to appeal

---

## Tips for a Strong Application

- **Use a quality beats link** — Share a link that showcases your best work and loads properly
- **Use your real name** — Accurate details help the review process and simplify payout setup
- **Add your Instagram** — Social presence can help confirm your identity
- **Join Discord** — Required for verification, and useful for staying informed about producer community updates
