The **Get Verified** option is for producers who already have a BeatPass producer profile and want the verified badge. This is not a submission form — it directs you to the Discord community where BeatPass staff manually verify producers.

---

## Who Should Use This

Choose this path if:

- You already have a BeatPass producer profile
- You want the blue verified badge
- You are ready to join and participate in the Discord community

If you don't have a producer profile yet, use [Become a Producer](/help/producer-program/application-types/become-producer) or [Claim Existing Artist](/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist) first.

---

## What Verification Gives You

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Verified Badge** | Blue check on your producer profile, listing cards, and linked user profile |
| **Audio Recon** | Audio analysis and similarity-detection tools |
| **Producer Dashboard Notifications** | New Subscribers, Analytics Reports, Payout Updates |
| **Beat Request Alerts** | Automatic notifications when new requests match your style |

---

## How Verification Works

### In BeatPass

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Producer Program page">
    Click **Apply to Join** on the Producer Program page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Get Verified">
    On the request selector, choose **Get Verified**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the verification screen">
    The screen shows **Verify Through Discord** with a **Join Discord Community** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join Discord">
    Click **Join Discord Community** to proceed to the BeatPass Discord server.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
There is no submission form on the verification screen. The **Get Verified** flow is purely a handoff to Discord.
</Note>

### In Discord

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the server">
    Use the invite link provided on the verification screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match your name">
    Use a Discord name that matches your BeatPass producer profile name — this helps staff identify you faster.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participate">
    Be active in the Discord server so staff can identify you as a BeatPass producer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your role and badge">
    Staff grant you a BeatPass producer Discord role, then manually mark your BeatPass profile as verified. The blue badge appears after this process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Timeline

Most verifications complete within **1–3 business days**. Using the same name on Discord as your BeatPass producer profile speeds things up.

| Requirement | Details |

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need Discord to get verified?">
    Yes. Verification happens through manual review by BeatPass staff in Discord.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get verified without a producer profile?">
    No. The **Get Verified** option only appears if you already have a producer profile. Create one first using **Become a Producer**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a submission form?">
    No. The verification screen shows **Verify Through Discord** and a Discord join button. There is no form to fill out.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
