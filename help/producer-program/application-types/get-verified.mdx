The **Get Verified** option is for producers who already have a BeatPass producer profile and want the verified badge. This is not a submission form—it's a handoff to the Discord community where BeatPass staff manually verify producers.

---

## Who Should Use This

Choose this path if:

- You already have a BeatPass producer profile
- You want the blue verified badge
- You're ready to join and participate in the Discord community

If you don't have a producer profile yet, use [Become a Producer](/help/producer-program/application-types/become-producer) or [Claim Existing Artist](/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist) first.

---

## What Verification Gives You

### Confirmed Features

These verified-only features are active in the platform:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Verified Badge** | Blue check appears on your producer profile, listing cards, and linked user profile |
| **Audio Recon Access** | Advanced audio analysis and beat matching tools |
| **Exclusive Notifications** | New Subscribers, Analytics Reports, Payout Updates |
| **Notification Groups** | Join Audio Recon and Producer Dashboard notification channels |
| **Beat Request Alerts** | Automatic notifications when new requests match your style |

### Additional Benefits

The verification screen also mentions these benefits, though their implementation status varies:

- **Priority Support** — Enhanced support response times
- **Exclusive Events** — Access to verified-producer opportunities
- **Producer Network** — Additional community connections

## How Verification Works

Verification happens in two parts: the BeatPass app directs you to Discord, then staff manually verify you there.

### In BeatPass

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Producer Program">
    Visit [https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-program](https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-program) and click **Apply to Join**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Get Verified">
    On the request selector at [https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests](https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests), choose **Get Verified**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View verification requirements">
    The verification page at [https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests/verify-artist](https://open.beatpass.ca/backstage/requests/verify-artist) shows **Discord Community Required** with a **Join Discord Community** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join Discord">
    Click **Join Discord Community** to proceed to Discord.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
There is no submission form on the verification page. The **Get Verified** flow is purely a handoff to Discord.
</Note>

### In Discord

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the server">
    Use the invite link: [https://discord.com/invite/N257QBkSeg](https://discord.com/invite/N257QBkSeg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match your name">
    Use a Discord name that matches your BeatPass producer profile name when possible—this speeds up identification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participate in the community">
    Be active in the Discord server so staff can identify you as a BeatPass producer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your role">
    Staff will grant you the BeatPass producer Discord role once they verify your identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profile gets verified">
    After receiving the Discord role, staff manually mark your BeatPass profile as verified. The blue badge appears shortly after.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Timeline Expectations

| Scenario | Typical Wait Time |
|----------|------------------|
| **Name matches + recognized** | Within hours |
| **Standard verification** | 2-3 days |
| **Name mismatch or unclear identity** | May take longer |

<Tip>
Using the same name on Discord as your BeatPass producer profile significantly speeds up the process.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need Discord to get verified?">
    Yes. Verification currently happens through manual review by BeatPass staff in Discord.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
    Most verifications complete within 2-3 days. If your Discord name matches your producer profile, it can happen within hours.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I'm already in Discord but not verified?">
    Contact BeatPass support at [https://open.beatpass.ca/contact](https://open.beatpass.ca/contact) or ask the community team in Discord. Staff may not have matched your Discord account to your producer profile yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get verified without a producer profile?">
    No. The **Get Verified** option only appears if you already have a producer profile. Create one first using **Become a Producer**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a submission form?">
    No. The verification page shows requirements and a Discord join button, but there's no form to fill out. Verification happens manually in Discord.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if the badge doesn't appear after Discord verification?">
    The badge should appear shortly after staff marks you as verified. If it doesn't show within the expected timeframe, contact support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
