The **Claim Existing Artist** request is for producers whose music or producer page is already on BeatPass, but whose current account does not control that profile yet.

---

## Who Should Use This

Choose this path if:

- You already see your music on BeatPass
- The producer profile is not yet connected to your account
- You need BeatPass to verify ownership or authority before linking it to you

If you are starting from scratch with no existing profile, use [Become a Producer](/help/producer-program/application-types/become-producer) instead.

---

## How It Works

1. You search for and select the existing producer profile
2. BeatPass checks whether it is currently available
3. The team reviews your ownership evidence
4. If approved, the profile is linked to your account

---

## What You'll Need

| Requirement | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **The profile to claim** | You search for and select it during the request |
| **Link to your beats** | Public URL showing your music and identity |
| **First and last name** | Your real name for review |
| **Your role** | How you relate to the profile (e.g. Artist, Manager) |
| **Instagram handle** (optional) | Can help with identity review |
| **Attachments** (optional) | Additional proof of ownership or authority |

---

## Step-by-Step Request

### 1. Start the Request

1. Go to the Producer Program page
2. Click **Apply to Join** (if logged in) or **Apply Now** (if not)
3. Choose **Claim Existing Artist** from the available options

<Note>
The **Claim Existing Artist** option is available to all users, regardless of whether they already have a producer profile.
</Note>

### 2. Select the Profile

Search for the producer profile you want to claim. After selecting a profile, BeatPass checks its current ownership state:

- **Available** — The profile can be claimed
- **Already Claimed** — The profile is already linked to another user

If a profile is already claimed but you believe it should belong to you, contact support.

### 3. Profile Picture

The claim flow lets you upload a profile picture for the profile you are claiming.

### 4. Personal Details

Fill in:

- **First name**
- **Last name**
- **Role** (e.g. Artist, Manager, Producer, Label)
- **Record label or company** (optional)

<Note>
The role selector shows multiple options, but approval-based associations are currently implemented for **Artist** and **Manager** roles.
</Note>

### 5. Supporting Materials

Provide:

- **Link to your beats** (required)
- **Instagram handle** (optional)
- **Attachments** (optional)

### 6. Agreement

Check both boxes to confirm:

1. You agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
2. You own the rights to the beats you will upload, grant BeatPass a non-exclusive license, and commit to uploading at least 2 beats per month

### 7. Submit

Click **Submit Application** to send the claim for review.

---

## What Happens Next

BeatPass verifies that the profile is available, that you are the rightful owner or authorized representative, and that your supporting materials make sense for the claim. You receive an email when the claim is approved or denied.

Once approved, the profile is linked to your account and you can manage it from your producer tools. See [After Approval](/help/producer-program/after-approval) for next steps.

---

## What If the Profile Is Already Claimed?

If you see **Already Claimed** when selecting a profile:

1. The profile is already owned by another user
2. The normal claim flow stops there
3. If you believe the ownership is wrong, contact support at contact@beatpass.ca

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I cannot find my profile?">
    Try alternate spellings or shorter versions of the name. If you still cannot find it, the profile may not exist yet — use **Become a Producer** instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I claim multiple profiles?">
    The claim flow handles one profile at a time. If you have a more complex ownership situation, contact support before submitting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to existing tracks when I claim?">
    The existing profile stays in place. Approval gives your account the ability to manage it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I prove ownership?">
    Provide links showing you control the music and any documents or screenshots that support your claim.
  </Accordion>
