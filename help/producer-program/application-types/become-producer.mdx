The **Become a Producer** request is for people who do not already have a producer profile on BeatPass. It creates a brand-new profile linked to your account after approval.

---

## Who Should Use This

Choose this path if:

- You are new to BeatPass as a producer
- You do not already see a BeatPass producer profile that should belong to you
- You want a fresh producer profile built around your current account

If your music is already on BeatPass under an existing profile, use [Claim Existing Artist](/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist) instead.

---

## What You'll Need

Before starting your request, prepare:

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Producer name** | How you want to appear publicly on BeatPass |
| **Link to your beats** | Public URL to your music for review |
| **First and last name** | Your real name for identity review |
| **Record label or company** (optional) | Add this if you are signed or represent a business |
| **Instagram handle** (optional) | Can help with identity verification |
| **Attachments** (optional) | Extra proof or supporting material |

---

## Step-by-Step Request

### 1. Start the Request

Click your **profile avatar** -> **Backstage**, then choose **Become a Producer**.

### 2. Profile Setup

Enter your **Producer Name**. This is the public name BeatPass will use for your producer profile if you are approved.

<Note>
  The current Become flow does not let you upload a profile picture during the request itself. You can add profile media after approval.
</Note>

### 3. Personal Details

Fill in:

- **First name**
- **Last name**
- **Record label or company (optional)**

### 4. Supporting Materials

Provide:

- **Link to your beats** (required)
- **Instagram handle (optional)**
- **Attachments (optional)**

### 5. Join Community

The form includes a Discord community section. It is optional for this request type, but useful if you want producer community updates and later verification.

### 6. Agreement

Check both boxes to confirm:

1. You agree to the [Terms of Service](https://open.beatpass.ca/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://open.beatpass.ca/privacy)
2. You own all rights to the beats you will upload, grant BeatPass a non-exclusive license, and commit to uploading at least 2 beats per month

### 7. Submit

Click **Submit Application** to send the request for review.

## What Happens Next

### Review

BeatPass reviews:

- The quality of your beats
- The accuracy of your information
- Whether the request appears legitimate

The request-submitted screen currently shows a **24-48 hour** estimate, but timing can vary.

### Decision

You will receive an email when a decision is made:

- **Approved** - Your new producer profile is created and activated
- **Denied** - BeatPass explains why so you can improve and reapply later

### After Approval

Once approved:

- You gain access to BeatPass producer tools
- You can start uploading beats and collections
- You can connect Stripe for payouts

See [After Approval](/help/producer-program/after-approval) for the next steps.

---

## Benefits You Unlock

As an approved producer, you get:

- **Keep 100% of your price** on exclusive listings
- **Unlimited uploads** in the current producer tools
- **Catalog management** for beats and collections
- **Payout access** through Stripe Connect
- **XP and activity tracking** tied to uploads, plays, and collaborations

See [Benefits](/help/producer-program/benefits) for the full overview.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upload a profile picture during this request?">
    Not in the current Become flow. The picture picker is shown in the layout, but it is disabled for new-profile requests. Add your profile media after approval instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my request is denied?">
    You will receive an email explaining why. Fix the issue, strengthen your materials, and apply again when ready.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does review take?">
    BeatPass currently shows a 24-48 hour estimate on the submission confirmation page, but actual review time can vary.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need a paid subscription to be a producer?">
    No. Producer access is separate from listener subscriptions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
