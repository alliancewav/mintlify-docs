The **Become a Producer** request is for people who do not already have a producer profile on BeatPass. It creates a brand-new profile linked to your account after approval.

---

## Who Should Use This

Choose this path if:

- You are new to BeatPass as a producer
- You do not see a BeatPass producer profile that should belong to you
- You want a fresh producer profile built around your current account

If your music is already on BeatPass under an existing profile, use [Claim Existing Artist](/help/producer-program/application-types/claim-existing-artist) instead.

---

## What You'll Need

| Requirement | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Producer name** | How you want to appear publicly on BeatPass |
| **Link to your beats** | Public URL to your music for review |
| **First and last name** | Your real name for identity review |
| **Record label or company** (optional) | Add this if you are signed or represent a business |
| **Instagram handle** (optional) | Can help with identity verification |
| **Attachments** (optional) | Extra proof or supporting material |

---

## Step-by-Step Request

### 1. Start the Request

1. Go to the Producer Program page
2. Click **Apply to Join** (if logged in) or **Apply Now** (if not)
3. Choose **Become a Producer** from the available options

<Note>
If you already have a producer profile, you will see **Get Verified** as the primary option instead of **Become a Producer**.
</Note>

### 2. Profile Setup

Enter your **Producer Name**. This is the public name BeatPass will use for your producer profile if approved.

### 3. Personal Details

Fill in:

- **First name**
- **Last name**
- **Record label or company** (optional)

### 4. Supporting Materials

Provide:

- **Link to your beats** (required)
- **Instagram handle** (optional)
- **Attachments** (optional)

### 5. Join Community

The form includes a Discord community section. It is optional for this request type, but useful if you want producer community updates and later verification.

### 6. Agreement

Check both boxes to confirm:

1. You agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
2. You own all rights to the beats you will upload, grant BeatPass a non-exclusive license, and commit to uploading at least 2 beats per month

### 7. Submit

Click **Submit Application** to send the request for review.

---

## What Happens Next

### Review

BeatPass reviews the quality of your beats, the accuracy of your information, and whether the request appears legitimate. The submitted page shows an estimated review time of **1–3 business days**, but timing can vary.

### Decision

You receive an email when a decision is made:

- **Approved** — Your new producer profile is created and activated
- **Denied** — BeatPass explains why so you can improve and reapply

### After Approval

Once approved, you gain access to producer tools and can start uploading beats, connecting Stripe for payouts, and managing your catalog. See [After Approval](/help/producer-program/after-approval) for next steps.
