Whether you just joined the Producer Program or have been on BeatPass for a while, these strategies help you grow your audience, increase plays, and maximize your earnings.

---

## Upload Consistently

Consistency is one of the most important factors in growing on BeatPass.

- **Newer tracks earn more** — The [contribution system](/help/earnings/contribution-system/formula) gives higher value to recently uploaded tracks due to time decay
- **Regular uploads keep you visible** — Active producers appear more frequently in discovery channels and recommendations
- **Aim for a steady cadence** — Even 2–4 tracks per month keeps your catalog fresh

<Tip>
  Quality matters more than quantity. One track with 1,000 plays earns significantly more than ten tracks with 10 plays each.
</Tip>

## Promote Your Music

BeatPass gives you the tools — but promoting outside the platform drives new listeners in.

### On Social Media

- **Share direct links** to your tracks and artist profile
- **Use the embed player** to showcase beats on your website or blog
- **Post consistently** when you upload new content
- **Engage with your audience** — reply to comments and messages

### On BeatPass

- **Pin your best track** to the top of your artist profile
- **Repost tracks** from producers you collaborate with to build community
- **Respond to beat requests** — fulfilling requests earns you visibility with active subscribers

## Use the Gamification System

The [gamification system](/help/gamification/index) rewards activity and increases your visibility:

- **Earn XP** for uploads, plays, metadata completion, and achievements
- **Level up** your producer title — higher levels signal experience to listeners
- **Unlock achievements** that display on your profile as social proof
- **Climb leaderboards** — top-ranked producers get more exposure in discovery features

<Info>
  Check your **Progress** page in the Producer Dashboard to see your current XP breakdown, upcoming achievements, and next level milestone.
</Info>

## Collaborate with Other Producers

Collaborations expand your reach by putting your name in front of another producer's audience:

- Collaborative tracks appear on **both producers' profiles**
- Your collaboration network is visible on your dashboard
- BeatPass recommends collaboration opportunities based on genre and style

See [Collaborations](/help/uploading/collaborations) for how to credit co-producers on your tracks.

## Optimize Your Profile

Your artist profile is your storefront. Make it count:

- **Write a compelling bio** — Tell visitors who you are and what you create
- **Upload a professional photo** and header image
- **Pin your strongest track** so it's the first thing visitors hear
- **Add professional credits** to showcase your work outside BeatPass
- **Apply for verification** to earn the verified badge and boost credibility

See [Artist Profiles](/help/profiles/artist-profiles/index) for a full guide.

---

## Offer Exclusive Licenses

Exclusive licenses are a direct revenue stream on top of subscription earnings:

- **Set competitive prices** based on your experience and demand
- **Create license presets** to save time when configuring terms
- **Highlight exclusive availability** — tracks available for exclusive purchase attract serious buyers

See [Exclusive Licenses for Producers](/help/licensing-for-producers/exclusive-licenses/index) for setup instructions.

## Growth Checklist

Use this as a recurring review:

- [ ] Uploading at least 2–4 tracks per month
- [ ] All metadata fields completed on every track
- [ ] Cover art added to every track and album
- [ ] Artist bio and profile photo up to date
- [ ] Pinned track is your best current work
- [ ] Checking Backstage Insights weekly
- [ ] Responding to beat requests that match your style
- [ ] Sharing new releases on social media
- [ ] Reviewing leaderboard position monthly
