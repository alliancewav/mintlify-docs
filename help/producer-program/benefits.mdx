BeatPass producer access is more than an upload permission. It unlocks the tools needed to publish your catalog, offer licenses, and earn from activity on the platform.

---

## Revenue & Earnings

### Keep 100% of Your Price

For exclusive sales, you keep the listed price you set for the beat. BeatPass adds the platform fee to the buyer's checkout total instead of subtracting it from your listed amount.

| You Set | Buyer Pays | Your Listed Amount |
|---------|------------|-------------------|
| \$50 | \$59.00 | \$50 |
| \$100 | \$115.00 | \$100 |
| \$200 | \$227.00 | \$200 |

<Note>
  Payouts still depend on an active Stripe Connect setup, and your final bank deposit timing depends on Stripe settlement and payout processing.
</Note>

### Multiple Income Streams

As a producer, you can earn from:

- **Subscription contribution** tied to BeatPass listening activity
- **Exclusive licenses** when buyers purchase your configured exclusive option
- **Ongoing catalog performance** as more of your beats get discovered and used

## Upload & Catalog

### Unlimited Uploads

The current producer tools do not impose a fixed catalog cap. You can keep building your library of beats and collections over time.

### Catalog Management

Your producer workspace lets you:

- Upload individual beats and collections
- Edit metadata, cover art, and licensing
- Reorder collection tracks
- Replace audio when needed
- Review finances and producer activity

---

## Rights & Ownership

### Keep Your Rights

You retain full ownership of your music. By joining the program, you grant BeatPass a **non-exclusive license** to distribute your beats on the platform.

This means:

- You can still sell your beats elsewhere
- You control your track pricing and exclusive terms
- Existing non-exclusive BeatPass licenses stay valid if a beat later sells exclusively

<Note>
  BeatPass may use up to 60 seconds of your beats, together with cover art and credits, on BeatPass-owned channels for promotion. You can opt out by emailing `contact@beatpass.ca` with the subject `Promo Opt-Out`.
</Note>

## No Upfront Costs

There is no fee to join the Producer Program. You pay nothing to get started, and when you make exclusive sales, BeatPass adds the platform fee to the buyer instead of billing you for entry.
