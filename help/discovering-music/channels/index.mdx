Channels are the reusable discovery containers behind much of BeatPass. A channel can power a homepage module, a genre page, or a standalone discovery page.

## What A Channel Can Hold

The current channel system can render channels for:

- Tracks
- Artists
- Albums
- Playlists
- User profiles
- Genres
- Other nested channels

## Why Channels Matter

Channels let BeatPass reuse the same discovery engine across different parts of the product instead of hard-coding each homepage or genre experience.
