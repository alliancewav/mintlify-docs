Some browse pages include filters at the top that let you narrow results by keyword, genre, or artist. These filters are optional — they only appear when the page supports them.

## When Filters Appear

Filters are shown on standalone browse pages where the content type supports filtering. You will not see filters when:

- The page is a nested section inside a larger page.
- The page only shows sub-sections rather than direct content.
- Filtering is not enabled for that page.

## Available Filters

Depending on what a page displays, you may see some or all of these:

| Filter | What it does | Appears on |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Search…** | Text search within the current results | All filterable pages |
| **All Genres** | Narrow by genre | Track and album browse pages |
| **All Artists** | Narrow by artist | Track and album browse pages |

Artist-only browse pages show **Search…** and **All Genres** but not the artist filter. Playlist, user, and genre pages show **Search…** only.

<Note>
  Some page headers integrate the filters directly into the header area rather than showing a separate filter bar below it.
</Note>
