The **For You** channel provides personalized beat recommendations based on your listening history. It's designed to help you discover new beats that match your unique taste and preferences.

## What Is the For You Channel?

The For You channel is a special personalized channel that analyzes your listening behavior and recommends beats you haven't heard yet but are likely to enjoy. Unlike other channels that show the same content to everyone, this channel is unique to you.

</Info>

## How It Works

The recommendation system analyzes several aspects of your listening history:

### What We Analyze

- **Genres you listen to** — The genres you play most often are given priority
- **BPM preferences** — Beats with similar tempo to your favorites
- **Musical keys** — Keys that match your listening patterns
- **Favorite producers** — Tracks from producers similar to ones you enjoy

### How Recommendations Are Generated

1. **Your recent plays are analyzed** — The system looks at beats you've played in the last 90 days
2. **Preferences are weighted** — Beats you play more often have greater influence on recommendations
3. **New beats are matched** — The system finds beats you haven't heard that match your preferences
4. **Fresh content is prioritized** — Recently uploaded beats that match your taste appear first

## Recognizing the For You Channel

The For You channel has a distinctive visual style to help you identify it:

- **Shimmering border** — A subtle animated glow effect outlines the channel container
- **Rotating light accent** — Two shimmer beams rotate around the border
- **Theme-matched colors** — The glow uses your current theme's accent color

This unique styling helps distinguish personalized content from regular curated channels.

## When You'll See This Channel

The For You channel appears when:

- You are **logged in** to your BeatPass account
- You have **played at least a few beats** recently
- The system can find **enough matching recommendations** based on your history

<Note>
  If you're new to BeatPass or haven't listened to much content yet, the channel won't appear. Start exploring and playing beats you like, and personalized recommendations will become available as your listening history grows.
</Note>

## Getting Better Recommendations

To improve your personalized recommendations:

### Play More Beats

The more you listen, the better the system understands your preferences. Don't just skip through — play beats you enjoy all the way through.

### Explore Different Genres

If you typically stick to one genre, try exploring others. The system will learn your full range of preferences and can recommend beats that blend styles you enjoy.

### Use Your Library

Like beats you enjoy. While the recommendation system primarily uses play history, engaging with content helps build a more complete picture of your taste.

## Tips for Using the Channel

- **Scroll through regularly** — New recommendations appear as you continue listening
- **Give beats a chance** — The system is designed to introduce you to new sounds, not just repeat familiar ones
- **Check back often** — Recommendations update based on your recent activity

## Privacy Note

Your listening history is used solely to improve your personal recommendations. This data is not shared publicly and is only used to enhance your discovery experience on BeatPass.
