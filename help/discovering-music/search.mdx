BeatPass search helps you find exactly what you're looking for. Search for beats by name, discover producers, find albums, or locate playlists—all from one search bar.

## Using Search

### Starting a Search

1. Click the **search bar** at the top of any page
2. Type your search term (beat name, producer name, keyword, etc.)
3. Press **Enter** or click the search icon to see results

As you type, you'll see instant suggestions appear below the search bar. Click any suggestion to go directly to that item.

### Search Results

After searching, results are organized into tabs based on content type:

- **Top results** — The most relevant matches across all categories
- **Tracks** — Individual beats matching your search
- **Artists** — Producers whose name or profile matches
- **Albums** — Beat collections and albums
- **Playlists** — User-created and curated playlists
- **Profiles** — User accounts on BeatPass
- **Tags** — Hashtags and keywords used to categorize beats

Click any tab to filter results by that specific type.

## Search Tips

### Finding Specific Beats

- Search by **beat name** to find a specific track
- Search by **producer name** followed by a keyword (e.g., "808 mafia type beat")
- Use **descriptive terms** like "dark trap" or "soulful r&b"

### Discovering Producers

- Search a producer's **name** to find their profile
- Browse their profile to see all their beats, albums, and more

### Using Tags

Tags are keywords attached to beats that describe their style, mood, or characteristics. To search by tag:

1. Search for a term like "type beat" or "drill"
2. Click the **Tags** tab in search results
3. Select a tag to see all beats with that tag

<Tip>
You can also browse tags directly by clicking on any tag shown on a beat or album page.
</Tip>

## From Search Results

Once you find what you're looking for, you can:

- **Play** — Click the play button to listen
- **Like** — Save the beat to your library
- **View details** — Click the item to see more information
- **Visit producer** — Click the producer name to see their profile

## Mobile Search

On mobile devices, a search bar appears at the top of the search results page. Simply tap it and start typing to find what you need.
