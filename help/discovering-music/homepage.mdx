The BeatPass homepage is built from multiple discovery sections. Rather than a fixed set of panels, the content can vary — you may see different sections, layouts, and recommendations depending on what is available.

## What You Might See

The homepage can include:

- **Browse sections** showing tracks, albums, playlists, or producers.
- **Personalised recommendations** tailored to your listening history.
- **Promotional banners** and featured content.
- **Genre or collection highlights** presented in different layouts.

## Personalised Sections

Some homepage sections adapt to your activity. If BeatPass does not have enough data to generate recommendations for you (for example, if you are a new user or not signed in), those sections are hidden entirely — they will not show as empty blocks.

## Content Can Change

The exact sections you see may evolve over time. The homepage is not hard-coded to always show the same panels. The number of sections, their order, and whether filters appear can all vary.

<Note>
  If you want to explore beyond what the homepage shows, use [Search](/help/discovering-music/search), [Genre pages](/help/discovering-music/channels/genre-channels), or [Producer pages](/help/discovering-music/producer-pages) to discover more content.
</Note>
