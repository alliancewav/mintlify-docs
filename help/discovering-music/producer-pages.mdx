Producer pages are served from the current `/artist/...` routes. They are the main place to inspect a producer's catalog and related discovery tabs.

## Current Header Behavior

The producer page header can show:

- Producer image and name
- Verified badge when the artist is verified
- **Unclaimed** badge when the profile has no owner
- Action buttons for **Play**, saving the producer to your library, **Message**, and **More**

## Message Button Rules

The message action is not always available. It is disabled when:

- The producer profile is unclaimed
- You are looking at your own producer profile
- You are not logged in

## Tabs You May See

Producer page tabs are dynamic. The current page can include:

- **Discography**
- **Similar artists**
- **Gallery**
- **Beats**
- **Albums**
- **Followers**
- **Achievements**

<Note>
  **Achievements** is restricted to the producer owner or an admin-level user. **Similar artists** is only shown when BeatPass has similar artists to display.
</Note>

## How To Use Producer Pages For Discovery

- Start with **Discography** for the broadest overview.
- Jump to **Beats** when you want the full track list.
- Use **Albums** to move into collections.
- Open **Similar artists** when one producer should lead you to more.
- Use **More** for additional context actions such as radio when that option is available.
