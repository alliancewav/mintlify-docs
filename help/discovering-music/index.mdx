## Quick Tips for Finding Beats

- **Use the search bar** at the top of the page to quickly find beats by name, producer, or keyword
- **Follow producers** you like to see their new releases in your feed
- **Browse genres** to discover beats organized by style
- **Try Radio** when you want continuous playback of similar beats
- **Check trending sections** on the homepage to see what's popular right now

## Building Your Collection

Once you find beats you love, you can:
- **Like beats** to save them to your library
- **Create playlists** to organize beats for different projects
- **Follow producers** to stay updated on their new releases
- **Download or license** beats when you're ready to use them in your projects
