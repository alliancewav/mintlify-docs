The Progress page shows your XP (experience points), current level, and achievements. As you upload tracks, gain plays, and hit milestones, you earn XP that helps you level up and unlock special achievements.

<Info>
**Audience:** Producers with an artist profile on BeatPass
</Info>

---

## How to Access Progress

1. Go to your **Producer Dashboard**
2. Click **Progress** in the sidebar
3. View your level, XP, and achievements

---

## Your Level & XP

At the top of the Progress page, you'll see:

- **Current Level** — Your producer level (e.g., Level 5)
- **Level Title** — A name associated with your level
- **Total XP** — How much experience you've earned
- **Progress Ring** — Visual showing your progress toward the next level
- **XP to Next Level** — How many more XP you need to level up

Your progress updates automatically as you earn XP. Some updates may take up to 60 seconds to appear.

---

## How to Earn XP

There are several ways to earn XP and level up:

### Upload Tracks

**+100 XP per track**

Every track you publish to BeatPass earns you 100 XP. Building your catalog is one of the fastest ways to level up.

### Get Plays

**+1 XP per play**

When listeners play your tracks, you earn 1 XP for each play. Popular tracks with many plays will significantly boost your XP over time.

### Complete Metadata

**+50 XP per complete track**

Add complete metadata to your tracks—BPM, key, scale, tags, and description—to earn 50 XP per fully completed track.

### Collaborate

**+25 XP per collaboration**

When you work with other producers on collaborative tracks, you earn 25 XP for each collaboration.

### Unlock Achievements

**+100 to +10,000 XP per achievement**

Achievements award varying amounts of XP depending on their rarity and difficulty. Some rare achievements can give you thousands of XP at once.

## Achievements

Achievements are milestones you unlock by reaching specific goals. Each achievement awards XP and appears on your profile.

### Achievement Stats

The Achievements section shows:

- **Unlocked** — How many achievements you've earned
- **Locked** — Achievements you haven't unlocked yet
- **Total XP Earned** — XP gained from achievements
- **Progress Bar** — Percentage of all achievements unlocked

### Rarity Levels

Achievements come in four rarity levels, each with distinct styling:

| Rarity | Description | XP Range |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Common** | Basic milestones | 150–800 XP |
| **Rare** | Notable accomplishments | 1,000–2,000 XP |
| **Epic** | Impressive achievements | 2,500–3,500 XP |
| **Legendary** | Top-tier milestones | 4,000–6,000 XP |

Legendary achievements display with a special shimmer effect.

### Locked Achievements

Achievements you haven't earned yet appear with a red lock icon. The description tells you what you need to do to unlock them.

## Achievement Categories

Achievements are organized into categories based on what actions unlock them:

### Upload Achievements

Earned by publishing tracks to the platform.

- **Track Producer** — Top 50% of producers by track count
- **Prolific Creator** — Top 25% of producers by track count
- **Production Master** — Top 10% of producers by track count
- **Studio Legend** — Top 5% of producers by track count

### Trending Achievements

Earned when your tracks perform well.

- **Rising Star** — Track in top 25% by play count
- **Chart Topper** — Track in top 10% by play count
- **Viral Hit** — Track in top 5% by play count

### Metadata Achievements

Earned by maintaining high-quality track information.

- **Metadata Whiz** — Over 70% metadata accuracy
- **Metadata Maven** — Over 80% metadata accuracy
- **Metadata Architect** — Over 90% metadata accuracy

### Profile Achievements

Earned by completing your producer profile.

- **Photogenic** — First picture uploaded to your profile
- **Model** — More than 5 pictures in your about section
- **Linked** — More than 3 links added to your profile
- **Oracle** — Bio filled out on your artist profile

### Genre Achievements

Earned by performing well within genres.

- **Genre Specialist** — Top 10% track performance within a genre
- **Genre Master** — Top 20% genre diversity with 3+ genres
- **Genre Virtuoso** — Top 10% genre diversity with 5+ genres

### Collaboration Achievements

Earned by working with other producers.

- **Collaboration Enthusiast** — Complete first collaboration
- **Team Player** — Complete 10 collaborations
- **Bridge Builder** — Complete 25 collaborations
- **Network Spider** — Complete 50 collaborations

### Composite Achievements

Earned through overall platform performance.

- **Rising Talent** — Top 25% overall with 5+ tracks
- **Elite Producer** — Top 15% overall with 10+ tracks
- **Legendary Producer** — Top 5% overall with 20+ tracks

### Milestone Achievements

Earned by reaching time-based and special milestones.

- **Early Adopter** — Joined during beta phase
- **Rising** — 3-month-old artist profile
- **Seasoned** — 6-month-old artist profile
- **OG** — 1-year-old artist profile
- **Veteran** — 2-year-old artist profile
- **Legacy** — 3-year-old artist profile
- **Top Biller** — Have a top-earning track

### Seasonal Achievements

Earned by uploading during special dates.

- **Shadow Regent** — Upload during Halloween (Oct 30–Nov 1)
- **Santa's Helper** — Upload during Christmas (Dec 23–26)
- **Opening Act** — Upload during New Year (Dec 31–Jan 2)
- **Lover Boy** — Upload during Valentine's Day (Feb 13–15)

Seasonal achievements award 200 XP and appear only when you upload during those specific windows.

## Important Notes

### Profile Completion

To receive XP from collaborative track plays, all collaborators must have completed their user profile. Make sure your profile is fully set up to earn collaborative XP.

### Zero-XP Achievements

Some special achievements (like seasonal badges) may award 0 XP but still count toward your overall progress and appear on your profile.

### Progress Updates

Your progress updates automatically. New achievements may take up to 60 seconds to appear after you meet the requirements.
