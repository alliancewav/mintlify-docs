The **Activity** page shows how your artist profile is performing over time. It combines long-range performance cards, a current-year upload graph, and a download panel with time filters.

<ProducerOnly />

## How to Get Here

Click your **profile avatar** → **Backstage**, then select **Activity** in the sidebar.

## Main Sections

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Performance Overview" icon="gauge-high">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Activity Overview" icon="calendar-days">

  <Card title="Downloads & Engagement" icon="download">

  </Card>

## Performance Overview

The first section is labelled **All Time** in the app, so it covers long-term trends rather than short campaign windows.

| Card | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Total Tracks** | Current catalog size and recent additions |
| **Total Plays** | Lifetime play total plus the current comparison change |
| **Platform Share** | Your contribution share and contribution points |
| **Achievements** | Achievement totals and recent achievement activity |

## Activity Overview

The middle section is a year view of your upload activity.

### How the Graph Works

- Each square represents one day in the current year
- Darker squares mean more uploads on that day
- Hover a square on desktop to see a tooltip
- Select a day to open a details panel with exact upload count and up to five tracks from that date

### X-Ray Mode

Toggle **X-Ray** to add comparison context:

- Your upload activity stays highlighted
- Broader platform activity appears behind days where you had no uploads
- The info button explains the colour logic used in the graph

## Downloads & Engagement

The bottom section focuses on download activity around your catalog.

### Summary Cards

| Card | What it tracks |
| --- | --- |
| **Downloads** | Total downloads in the selected period |
| **Unique Users** | Distinct users in that period |
| **This Week** | Current weekly download count |
| **Top Track** | Best-performing track for the selected period |

### Period Filters

Switch between **7D**, **30D**, **90D**, and **All** to adjust the time window.

### Recent Download List

Each row can include:

- The downloader profile
- The downloaded track with a direct link
- A relative time showing how long ago the download happened
- A license action when a certificate can be opened for that download

## Empty and Error States

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No catalog or low activity" icon="circle-info" defaultOpen>
    Cards stay near zero until your artist has uploaded music and started collecting plays or downloads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No downloads in the selected period" icon="download">
    The download list stays empty until activity exists for the selected filter.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analytics could not be loaded" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    The page shows a real error state instead of placeholder metrics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
