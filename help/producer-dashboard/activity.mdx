The Activity page shows how your artist profile is moving over time. It combines long-range performance cards, a current-year upload activity graph, and a download activity panel with time filters.

<Info>
  Open it at <a href="https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-dashboard/activity">https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-dashboard/activity</a>.
</Info>

## Main sections

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Performance Overview" icon="gauge-high">
    All-time cards for tracks, plays, platform share, and achievements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Activity Overview" icon="calendar-days">
    A current-year day-by-day upload graph with optional X-Ray mode.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Downloads & Engagement" icon="download">
    Period-based download totals, recent activity, and license shortcuts when available.
  </Card>

## Performance Overview

The first section is labeled **All Time** in the app, so it is built for long-term trend reading rather than short campaign reporting.

| Card | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Total Tracks** | Current catalog size and recent additions |
| **Total Plays** | Lifetime play total plus the current comparison change |
| **Platform Share** | Your contribution share and contribution points |
| **Achievements** | Achievement totals and recent achievement activity |

## Activity Overview

The middle graph is a year view of your upload activity.

### What the graph does

- Each square represents one day in the current year
- Darker activity means more uploads on that day
- Hovering a square on desktop shows a tooltip
- Selecting a day opens a details panel for that date

### X-Ray mode

The page includes an **X-Ray** toggle for comparison context.

When X-Ray is on:

- Your upload activity still stays highlighted
- Broader platform activity can appear behind days where you had no uploads
- The info button explains the color logic used in the graph

### Day details panel

When you select a day with uploads, BeatPass can show:

- The exact date
- Upload count for that day
- Up to five tracks from that date
- Approximate upload times when available

## Downloads & Engagement

The bottom section focuses on download activity around your catalog.

### Summary cards

| Card | What it tracks |
| --- | --- |
| **Downloads** | Total downloads in the selected period |
| **Unique Users** | Distinct users in that period |
| **This Week** | Current weekly download count |
| **Top Track** | Best-performing track for the selected period |

### Period filters

You can switch between:

- `7D`
- `30D`
- `90D`
- `All`

### Recent download list

Rows in the recent activity list can include:

- The downloader profile
- The downloaded track
- A relative time such as how long ago the event happened
- A direct track link
- A license action when BeatPass can open a certificate for that download

<Tip>
  Pair this page with the **Licenses** area in the app at <a href="https://open.beatpass.ca/library/licenses">https://open.beatpass.ca/library/licenses</a> when you want to cross-check download activity against issued certificates.
</Tip>

## Empty and error states

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No catalog or low activity yet" icon="circle-info" defaultOpen>
    Cards can stay near zero until your artist has uploaded music and started collecting plays or downloads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No downloads in the selected period" icon="download">
    The download list stays empty until activity exists for the selected filter.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analytics could not be loaded" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    The page shows a real error state instead of placeholder metrics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
