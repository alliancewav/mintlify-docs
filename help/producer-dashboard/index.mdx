The Producer Dashboard is your central workspace for managing your catalog, monitoring performance, tracking earnings, and viewing your progress on BeatPass.

<ProducerOnly />

## Open the Dashboard

Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation bar, then select **Backstage**. Use the sidebar to navigate between dashboard pages.

<Note>
  If your account does not have a linked artist profile, the dashboard shows a "No Artist Profile" state instead of redirecting. Complete your [Producer Program application](/help/producer-program) first.
</Note>

## How the Dashboard Works

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Artist context" icon="user" defaultOpen>
    Most pages load data for your linked artist profile. The **Finances** page can also show an artist switcher when your account manages more than one artist.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Overview first, editing second" icon="table-columns">
    The dashboard is designed for quick stats and navigation. Uploading, editing tracks, and full insights live in the backstage editing tools.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Empty states" icon="circle-info">
    If you don't have uploads, downloads, albums, achievements, or Stripe setup yet, you'll see empty or setup states instead of placeholder data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Refresh and cache behavior" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Each page refreshes differently. **Progress** has a manual **Refresh** button. **Leaderboards** keep previous results visible while loading new categories. **Finances** can show cache timestamps or stale-cache warnings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
