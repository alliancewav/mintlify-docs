The Producer Dashboard is a summary workspace for BeatPass artists. It brings together your catalog overview, activity snapshots, rankings, finances, and progress tools without replacing the deeper editing flows in Backstage.

<Info>
  **Who this is for:** Accounts with a linked artist profile. Some pages stay in setup or empty states until you have tracks, albums, or Stripe onboarding completed.
</Info>

## Open the dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Log in to your BeatPass account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the dashboard">
    Open <a href="https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-dashboard">https://open.beatpass.ca/producer-dashboard</a>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a page">
    Use the left-side dashboard menu or the shortcut cards on the Home page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If your account does not have a linked artist profile yet, BeatPass will guide you back to **Backstage** to finish setup.
</Note>

## What stays consistent across the dashboard

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Artist context" icon="user" defaultOpen>
    Most pages load data for your current linked artist profile. The **Finances** page can also show an artist switcher when your account manages more than one artist.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Summary first, editing second" icon="table-columns">
    The dashboard is designed for overview and quick actions. Uploading tracks, editing releases, and full insights screens still live in **Backstage**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real empty states" icon="circle-info">
    BeatPass does not invent numbers. If you do not have uploads, downloads, albums, achievements, or Stripe setup yet, you will see empty or setup states instead of placeholder claims.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Refresh and cache behavior" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Each page refreshes differently. **Progress** has its own manual **Refresh** button, **Leaderboards** keep the previous category visible while new results load, and **Finances** can show cache timestamps or stale-cache warnings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
