The **My Beats** page is your dashboard-level catalog view. It combines summary cards, a searchable track table, growth guidance, and collaboration tools for your current artist.

<ProducerOnly />

## How to Get Here

Click your **profile avatar** → **Backstage**, then select **My Beats** in the sidebar.

## Growth Status

The page header summarises how your catalog is trending. Select the help icon to open the growth guide with improvement tips.

| Label | Threshold |
| --- | --- |
| **Exceptional growth!** | Above +15% |
| **Strong growth** | Above +5% |
| **Growing steadily** | Above 0% |
| **Slight decline** | Above −5% |
| **Needs attention** | −5% or below |

## Overview Cards

| Card | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Total Tracks** | Catalog size plus recent upload context |
| **Total Plays** | Lifetime plays and recent change |
| **Growth Rate** | Positive, stable, or declining trend |
| **Collaborators** | Unique collaborators and collaborative-track totals |

## Track Table

The main working area on the page.

### Columns

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Track** | Artwork and track link |
| **Artist** | Primary artist |
| **Duration** | Track length |
| **Plays** | Play total |
| **Growth** | Per-track trend indicator |
| **Points** | Contribution points |
| **Last uploaded** | Upload date |
| **Actions** | Quick links for **Insights** and **Edit** |

### Available Actions

- **Upload new track** — opens the upload flow
- **Insights** — opens the track's backstage insights page
- **Edit** — opens the track editor
- **Delete selected** — appears when you select rows

### Filters

- Artwork
- Album
- Plays count
- Date uploaded
- Licensing
- Points

<Note>
  This page helps you review the catalog, but full editing still happens in the backstage track editor.
</Note>

## Collaboration Insights

Focuses on people already connected to your catalog.

- Total collaborator count
- Collaborative-track count
- A ranked list of top collaborators
- A detail modal when more collaboration data is available

## Collaboration Recommendations

Suggests artists to explore next based on your genre profile and catalog data.

- Your top genres used as recommendation context
- Suggested artists with similarity signals and shared genres
- A **View Profile** action for each suggestion

If BeatPass does not have enough data, the section encourages you to build your catalog before inventing recommendations.

## Empty States

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No linked artist profile" icon="user" defaultOpen>
    The page shows a not-found state when your account does not have a linked artist profile. Complete your [Producer Program application](/help/producer-program) first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No tracks yet" icon="music">
    The table stays empty until you upload your first track.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No recommendation data yet" icon="sparkles">
    Recommendation panels stay in guidance mode until BeatPass has enough track data to suggest matches.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
