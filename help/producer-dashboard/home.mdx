The Home page is the landing view when you open the Producer Dashboard. It shows who you are signed in as, your current artist context, and quick links to every dashboard page.

<ProducerOnly />

## How to Get Here

Click your **profile avatar** in the top bar, then select **Backstage**. The sidebar opens on **Dashboard** by default.

## What You See

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Greeting header" icon="hand">
    Time-based greeting, current date, artist or display name, and level summary.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Navigation" icon="compass">
    Shortcut cards generated from the current sidebar menu configuration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Stats" icon="chart-bar">

  </Card>

## Greeting Header

The top of the page personalises the dashboard around the signed-in account.

It can include:

- A greeting such as **Good morning**, **Good afternoon**, or **Good evening**
- The current day and date
- Your linked artist name (or your BeatPass display name as a fallback)
- Your current producer level title and XP total

<Note>
  The level summary comes from the same progress system used on the [Progress](/help/producer-dashboard/progress) page.
</Note>

## Quick Navigation

The shortcut grid is the fastest way to move between dashboard pages.

- Cards match the sidebar menu — **Dashboard**, **Finances**, **Activity**, **Progress**, **Leaderboards**, **My Beats**, **My Collections**
- Some cards may open pages outside the dashboard in a new tab
- If no menu items are configured, BeatPass shows **No menu items configured** instead of fake shortcuts

## Quick Stats

Eight cards surface the most visible numbers for your current artist.

| Card | What it tracks |
| --- | --- |
| **Tracks** | Total tracks for the current artist |
| **Total Plays** | Lifetime plays across your catalog |
| **Albums** | Total album count |
| **Downloads** | Track download count |
| **Followers** | Follower total |
| **Level** | Current level and XP |
| **Growth** | Current growth rate, or a calculating state |
| **View Profile** | Shortcut to your public artist page |

<Tip>
  Use the Home page to decide where to investigate next. If a number changes here, open the page that owns that metric for more detail.
</Tip>

## When Data Is Missing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="You are new to the dashboard" icon="sparkles" defaultOpen>
    New artists see **0** on several cards until tracks, plays, downloads, or followers begin to accumulate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Growth is still calculating" icon="chart-line">
    The **Growth** card stays neutral while BeatPass calculates the current rate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No linked artist profile" icon="user">
    The dashboard shows a not-found state when your account does not have a linked artist profile. Complete your [Producer Program application](/help/producer-program) first.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
