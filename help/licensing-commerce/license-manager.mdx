## Overview

The **License Manager** is your central hub for managing all track licenses on BeatPass. Whether you're an artist who has licensed beats or a producer tracking licenses granted to others, everything is organized in one place.

<Info>
  **Location:** Library \> Licenses (`/library/licenses`)

  **Audience:** Artists (viewing acquired licenses) and Producers (tracking granted licenses)
</Info>

---

## Accessing the License Manager

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Library">
    Click on **Library** in the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Licenses">
    Choose **Licenses** from the library menu, or navigate directly to `/library/licenses`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Licenses">
    All your licenses appear in a searchable, filterable list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downloading Certificates

For licenses you've acquired, you can download a professional PDF certificate:

## License Verification

Every license can be verified publicly using its unique UUID.

### Verifying a License

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the License UUID">
    Find the UUID on the license card or certificate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit Verification Page">
    Navigate to `https://open.beatpass.ca/verify-license/{UUID}`

    Or click the **Verify License** button on the license card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Verification Results">
    The page displays:

    - License validity status
    - Track information
    - License type and terms
    - Issue date
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Verification outcomes

When you verify a license, the status will show one of three outcomes:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Active" icon="circle-check" color="#22c55e">
    ðŸŸ¢ The license is valid and currently usable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Revoked" icon="ban" color="#ef4444">
    ðŸ”´ The license was invalidated (for example, due to chargebacks, fraud investigations, or a dispute ruling).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Expired" icon="clock" color="#eab308">
    ðŸŸ¡ The license term has ended. For non-exclusive licenses, this only appears if the license was issued with a fixed term and the `expires_at` date has passed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Understanding `expires_at` for non-exclusive licenses

Most non-exclusive licenses on BeatPass are perpetual. When a non-exclusive license includes a term limit, the verification page will show an `expires_at` timestamp indicating when the allowed usage window ends. If that date is blank, the license does not have a fixed expiry.

### What to do if a license shows as invalid or expired

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="For Buyers / Artists" icon="user-exclamation" color="#f97316">
    - Double-check that you entered the correct license UUID from your certificate.
    - If status is **Expired**, consider purchasing a new license or contacting the producer to discuss renewal options.
    - If status is **Revoked**, stop using the track commercially and reach out to support with your purchase receipt and certificate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="For Producers" icon="shield-exclamation" color="#fb7185">
    - Confirm the license UUID belongs to your catalog and matches the intended licensee.
    - If you revoked the license, ensure you have documented the reason (chargeback, dispute, or terms violation) in case of inquiries.
    - For expired non-exclusive licenses with `expires_at`, reach out to the buyer with renewal or upgrade options if appropriate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## License Generation

Licenses are automatically generated in these scenarios:

### Subscription Downloads

When a subscriber downloads a track:

1. Download initiated from track page
2. License automatically generated
3. License appears in both parties' License Managers
4. Certificate available immediately

### Exclusive Purchases

When an exclusive license is purchased:

1. Purchase completed via Stripe
2. Exclusive license generated
3. Track marked as "Exclusively Licensed"
4. Both parties notified
5. Certificate available immediately

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long do licenses last?">
    Most BeatPass licenses are **perpetual** â€” they don't expire as long as they remain in good standing. The license terms specify exact duration and usage rights.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a license be revoked?">
    Licenses can be revoked in cases of:

    - Terms of service violations
    - Fraudulent activity
    - Successful copyright disputes
    - Payment chargebacks

    You'll be notified if a license status changes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't find my license">
    If a recently acquired license isn't showing:

    1. Wait a few minutes for processing
    2. Refresh the page
    3. Check your email for license confirmation
    4. Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I transfer a license to someone else?">
    License transfers depend on your license type. Non-exclusive licenses are generally non-transferable. Exclusive licenses may have transfer provisions â€” check your license terms or contact support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if the producer deletes the track?">
    Your license remains valid even if the original track is removed from the platform. Keep your downloaded files and license certificates as documentation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a license for every track I download?">
    Yes. Each download generates a separate license. This ensures clear documentation of your rights to use each track commercially.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Distributor & Label Requirements

Many distributors and labels require proof of licensing. Here's how to provide it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Your Certificate">
    Get the PDF certificate from your License Manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Note the Verification URL">
    Include the verification URL in your submission materials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Documentation">
    Provide both the certificate and verification URL to your distributor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Distributors Need

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Proof of License" icon="file-pdf">
    Download the **PDF Certificate** from your License Manager. This is your primary proof of licensing rights.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="License ID" icon="fingerprint">
    Find the **UUID** on your license card or certificate. This unique identifier proves authenticity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Verification Link" icon="link">
    Provide the URL: `https://open.beatpass.ca/verify-license/{UUID}` â€” distributors can verify instantly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Track Information" icon="info-circle">
    Track details are on the certificate or the track page. Include artist name, track title, and duration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
