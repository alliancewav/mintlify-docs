**Plus** is the current recommended paid plan at **\$45 CAD/month**.

---

## What Plus Unlocks Today

The current Plus plan configuration lists:

- All features unlocked
- Unlimited Non-Exclusive Downloads
- Custom beat requests (3 tokens)
- License storage & management
- Enhanced Support (Email, Chat)

<Tip>
  **Plus** is currently marked as the recommended paid plan in the live product catalog.
</Tip>

---

## What Plus Changes Compared With Classic

- It raises the request allowance from **1** to **3**.
- It keeps the same enhanced email/chat support level as **Classic**.
- It remains below **Pro**, so it does not add **Priority Support**.

## Who Plus Fits Best

Choose **Plus** if you want:

- More than **1** request token each month
- The current recommended paid tier
- The same download foundation as the other paid plans
- Enhanced support without moving to the highest tier
