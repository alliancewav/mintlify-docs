## Feature Details

### Unlimited Downloads

All paid plans (Classic, Plus, Pro) include truly unlimited beat downloads:

- **No monthly caps** — Download as many beats as you need
- **No fair-use restrictions** — No hidden limits or throttling
- **Instant access** — Downloads are available immediately

### Commercial Licensing Rights

Every beat you download with a paid subscription includes a non-exclusive license allowing:

- Release on streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, etc.)
- Monetization on YouTube, TikTok, and social media
- Live performances at events up to 50 attendees
- Worldwide distribution with no geographic restrictions

**Requires attribution**: Credit the producer (e.g., "Produced by [Producer Name]")

**Not included**: Content ID registration, exclusive ownership, stem files (unless purchasing exclusive)

### BeatPassID Verification

Every download generates a license certificate with:

- Unique fingerprint verification
- Timestamp of download
- Your name and license details
- Verification link for proving ownership

### Custom Beat Requests

Submit requests describing your ideal beat:

- Specify genre, BPM, mood, and style
- Include reference tracks
- Add special requirements
- Producers submit within 24 hours

| Plan | Requests/Month |
|------|----------------|
| Explorer | 1 |
| Classic | 1 |
| Plus | 3 |
| Pro | 5 |

Unused requests do not roll over to the next month.

## Billing & Flexibility

All plans share these policies:

- **Monthly billing only** — No annual contracts
- **Cancel anytime** — No cancellation fees
- **Upgrade immediately** — New benefits activate instantly
- **Downgrade at renewal** — Keep current benefits until next billing cycle
- **Licenses are permanent** — Downloaded beats stay licensed forever
