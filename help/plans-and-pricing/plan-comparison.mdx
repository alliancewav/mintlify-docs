## What Stays The Same Across Paid Tiers

- **Classic**, **Plus**, and **Pro** are all monthly paid plans billed in **CAD**.
- All paid tiers currently include **unlimited non-exclusive downloads**.
- All paid tiers use the same base billing flow through **Billing**.
- BeatPass shows the platform fee separately during checkout.

---

## Where The Tiers Actually Differ

- **Explorer** is the free plan and does not unlock paid-plan downloads.
- **Classic** gives you the lowest paid price and **1** request token.
- **Plus** raises that limit to **3** request tokens and is the current recommended plan.
- **Pro** raises the limit to **5** request tokens and adds **Priority Support**.

<Warning>
  The extra Pro items **Studio services** and **Mixing & Mastering** are still marked **coming soon** in the live product catalog.
</Warning>

## Which Comparison Matters Most

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Requests">
    If request volume is your main decision point, the current split is:

    - **Explorer** - 1
    - **Classic** - 1
    - **Plus** - 3
    - **Pro** - 5
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Downloads">
    If downloads are the main reason you pay:

    - **Explorer** does not unlock the paid-plan download flow.
    - **Classic**, **Plus**, and **Pro** all currently include unlimited non-exclusive downloads.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Support">
    If support matters most:

    - **Explorer** - Standard Support (Email)
    - **Classic** and **Plus** - Enhanced Support (Email, Chat)
    - **Pro** - Priority Support
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
