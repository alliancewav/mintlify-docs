# Explorer Plan (Free)

The Explorer plan is your entry point to BeatPass. It's completely free and gives you full access to stream, discover, and connect with producers—perfect for finding your sound before committing to a paid subscription.

## What's Included

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lossless Streaming & Playlist Tools">
    Stream every beat in the catalog at full quality. Create and organize unlimited playlists to save the beats you love.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Like, Comment & Save Unlimited Beats">
    Interact with any beat—like tracks to show support, leave comments for producers, and save favorites to your library without limits.

## What's Not Included

The Explorer plan does **not** include:

- Beat downloads (streaming only)
- Commercial licensing rights
- Exclusive licensing discounts
- Chat support
- Additional custom beat requests beyond 1 per month

## Who Is Explorer For?

Explorer is ideal if you're:

- **New to beat licensing** and want to understand how BeatPass works before subscribing
- **Discovering your sound** and browsing different styles and genres
- **Building connections** with producers before committing to downloads
- **Not ready to release music yet** but want to save beats for later

## Upgrading from Explorer

When you're ready to download beats and release music, upgrading is simple:

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation
2. Select **Billing** from the menu
3. Choose the plan that fits your needs
4. Complete payment to activate immediately

Your liked beats, playlists, and producer connections all transfer when you upgrade—nothing is lost.
