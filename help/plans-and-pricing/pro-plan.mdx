**Pro** is the highest current BeatPass plan at **\$59 CAD/month**.

---

## What Pro Lists Today

The current Pro plan configuration lists:

- Everything in Classic
- Custom beat requests (5 tokens)
- Studio services (coming soon)
- Mixing & Mastering (coming soon)
- Priority Support

<Warning>
  The **Studio services** and **Mixing & Mastering** items are currently marked **coming soon** in the live plan configuration. They should not be treated as active plan perks today.
</Warning>

---

## What Pro Adds Right Now

Today, the active differences that matter most are:

- The highest current request limit at **5**
- **Priority Support**

The coming-soon service items should be treated as future-facing catalog text, not current access.
