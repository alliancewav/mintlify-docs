The Pro plan is designed for serious artists who create consistently and need maximum flexibility. With the most custom beat requests, priority support, and exclusive studio partner discounts, Pro removes every barrier between you and your music.

## Who Is Pro For?

Pro is ideal if you're:

- **Releasing consistently** and need a steady supply of custom beats
- **Working with a label** or professional team that requires quick turnarounds
- **Recording in studios** and want to save on production costs
- **Serious about your craft** and need priority assistance when issues arise

## Getting the Most from Pro

### Maximize Your Custom Requests

With 5 requests per month, consider:

- **Project planning** — Submit requests early in your creative process for albums or EPs
- **Style exploration** — Try different genres or production styles without commitment
- **Specific needs** — Request beats for specific songs you're writing

### Use Your Discounts

Pro includes the best savings on professional services:

- **Studio time** — Book sessions at partner studios with Pro-exclusive rates
- **Mixing and mastering** — Polish your tracks at reduced prices
- **Exclusive purchases** — When a beat is perfect for a flagship release, buy it exclusively at subscriber discounts

## Downgrading from Pro

If Pro is more than you need, you can switch to Plus or Classic:

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top navigation
2. Select **Billing** from the menu
3. Click **Change plan** and select your preferred tier

Your change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. You keep Pro benefits until then.

<Info>
**Already downloaded beats stay licensed forever** — Even if you downgrade or cancel, any beats you downloaded during your subscription remain fully licensed for commercial use.
</Info>
