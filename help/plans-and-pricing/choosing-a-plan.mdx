Use this guide to pick the current BeatPass plan that matches your request volume, support needs, and budget without relying on old plan descriptions.

---

## Quick Decision Guide

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I only need free access right now">
    Choose **Explorer**. It is the free plan behind non-paid accounts and currently includes playlists, messaging, notifications, lyrics, and **1** beat request each month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want the lowest-cost paid plan">
    Choose **Classic** at **\$29 CAD/month**. It is the entry paid plan and currently includes unlimited non-exclusive downloads, **1** request token, and enhanced email/chat support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I need more than 1 request per month">
    Choose **Plus** if **3** request tokens are enough, or **Pro** if you need **5**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want the current recommended paid plan">
    Choose **Plus**. It is currently marked as the recommended paid plan in the live product catalog.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I care most about support level">
    Choose **Pro** if you need **Priority Support**. Choose **Classic** or **Plus** if enhanced email/chat support is enough.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What Should Drive Your Decision

- Pick **Explorer** if you are still browsing and do not need paid-plan downloads.
- Pick **Classic** if downloads matter but you only need **1** request token.
- Pick **Plus** if your main upgrade reason is moving from **1** request token to **3**.
- Pick **Pro** if you need **5** request tokens and **Priority Support**.

<Warning>
  Do not choose **Pro** only for **Studio services** or **Mixing & Mastering**. Those two items are still marked **coming soon** in the live plan configuration.
</Warning>

---

## Billing Notes

- Paid plans are billed monthly in **CAD**.
- You can upgrade, downgrade, cancel, or renew from **Billing**.
- BeatPass adds a platform fee (**12% + $3.00 flat**) on top of the plan price at checkout. The fee is shown before you confirm payment. [Learn more →](/help/billing/platform-fee)
