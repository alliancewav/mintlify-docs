# BeatPass Subscription Plans

BeatPass offers four membership tiers designed for creators at every stage—from discovering new beats to releasing music professionally. Every paid plan includes unlimited beat downloads with commercial licensing rights.

## What Every Paid Plan Includes

All paid subscriptions (Classic, Plus, and Pro) share these core benefits:

- **Unlimited Non-Exclusive Downloads** — Download as many beats as you need with no limits or restrictions
- **Commercial Licensing** — Full rights to release on streaming platforms, YouTube, and social media
- **BeatPassID Verification** — Every download includes a license certificate with unique fingerprint verification
- **Perpetual Licenses** — Licenses you obtain remain valid forever, even if you cancel your subscription
- **Custom Beat Requests** — Submit requests and receive tailored beats from producers (number varies by plan)

## Billing & Payment

- **Monthly billing only** — All plans are billed monthly with no long-term contracts
- **Cancel anytime** — No cancellation fees or commitments
- **Licenses stay forever** — Even after canceling, any beats you downloaded remain licensed for commercial use
