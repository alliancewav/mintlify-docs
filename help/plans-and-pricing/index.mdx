## What Changes When You Move Up

- **Explorer** is for free account access and discovery tools.
- All paid plans currently unlock **unlimited non-exclusive downloads**.
- Beat request capacity increases as you move from **Classic** to **Plus** to **Pro**.
- **Plus** is the current recommended paid tier.
- **Pro** is the only current plan that adds **Priority Support**.
- The two extra Pro perks are still marked **coming soon** and should not be treated as active today.

## Shared Billing Rules

- All paid plans are billed **monthly**.
- Current paid prices are in **CAD**.
- If you already have a paid plan, open your **profile avatar** -> **Billing** and use **Change plan**.
- If you do not have a paid plan yet, open your **profile avatar** -> **Billing** and use **View Plans**.
- BeatPass shows the base plan price and the platform fee before you confirm checkout.
