**Classic** is the entry paid plan at **\$29 CAD/month**.

---

## What Classic Unlocks Today

The current Classic plan configuration lists:

- All features unlocked
- Unlimited Non-Exclusive Downloads
- Custom beat requests (1 token)
- License Management
- Enhanced Support (Email, Chat)

---

## What Classic Changes Compared With Explorer

- It unlocks the paid-plan download flow.
- It keeps the request allowance at **1**, matching Explorer's current request count.
- It upgrades support from standard email support to enhanced email/chat support.

---

## What Classic Does Not Add

- More than **1** request token per month
- The recommended-plan badge used on **Plus**
- **Priority Support**, which is only on **Pro**

---

---

## Who Classic Fits Best

Classic is the right starting point if you want:

- The lowest-cost paid plan
- Paid-plan download access
- A single request token each month
- Enhanced email and chat support without paying for higher request limits
