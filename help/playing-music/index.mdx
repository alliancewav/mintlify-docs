# Playing Music on BeatPass

BeatPass features a built-in player that lets you stream beats in high quality while browsing the platform. Whether you're on desktop or mobile, you can control playback, build a queue, and discover new sounds without interruption.

## The Player Bar

When you play a beat, a player bar appears at the bottom of your screen. This bar stays visible as you navigate, so your music never stops while you're browsing.

**The player bar shows:**
- **Track artwork** — Click to view the track page
- **Track name and producer** — Links to their pages
- **Playback controls** — Play, pause, skip, and more
- **Time display** — Current position and total duration
- **Additional options** — Volume, queue, lyrics, and download

## Desktop vs. Mobile

### Desktop Experience

On desktop, the player bar sits at the bottom of your screen with full controls visible:

- **Left side** — Track artwork, name, producer, and like button
- **Center** — Playback buttons and seekbar with time display
- **Right side** — Volume, queue toggle, lyrics, download, and expand

### Mobile Experience

On mobile devices, the player appears as a compact bar that you can tap to expand:

- **Mini player** — Shows track info, basic controls, and progress
- **Expanded view** — Tap the bar to see full-size artwork, all controls, and queue
- **Swipe gestures** — Swipe down to minimize, swipe to access queue

## Interacting with Tracks

While listening, you can:

- **Like a track** — Click the heart icon to save it to your library
- **Add to queue** — Right-click (or long-press on mobile) and select **Add to queue**
- **View track page** — Click the track name or artwork
- **View producer** — Click the producer name to see their profile
- **Download** — Click the download icon (paid subscription required)

<Info>
The player continues playing even when you navigate to different pages. Your current track and queue are preserved until you close the browser tab.
</Info>
