## Accessing Leaderboards

### Requirements

To view and participate in leaderboards, you need:

- A BeatPass account with an artist profile
- At least one published track (required to appear in rankings)

### Navigation

1. Log in to BeatPass
2. Navigate to `/producer-dashboard/leaderboards`
3. Use the **Category Filter** to switch between ranking types

<Tip>
  Click **"How it works"** in the header to open the Rankings Guide for detailed methodology information.
</Tip>

## Leaderboard Categories

BeatPass features **7 distinct leaderboard categories**, each measuring different aspects of your producer journey.

### Overall Rankings

The **Overall** leaderboard provides a composite score combining performance across all categories.

| Component         | Weight | Description                                                   |
| ----------------- | ------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Points (XP)**   | 25%    | Your total experience points from achievements and activities |
| **Plays**         | 20%    | Total play count across all your tracks                       |
| **Uploads**       | 15%    | Number of published tracks                                    |
| **Quality**       | 15%    | Metadata completeness percentage                              |
| **Collaboration** | 15%    | Collaborative tracks and partnerships                         |
| **Earnings**      | 10%    | Platform contribution share                                   |

<Note>
  The Overall leaderboard is the best indicator of a well-rounded, successful producer on BeatPass.
</Note>

### Points (XP) Rankings

Ranks producers by total **Experience Points** earned through platform engagement.

**How to earn XP:**

- Upload tracks: +100 XP per track
- Complete achievements: +100 to +10,000 XP depending on rarity
- Collaborate with others: +25 XP per collaboration
- Unlock milestones: Variable XP rewards

**Tiebreaker:** When XP totals are equal, achievement count determines ranking.

### Plays Rankings

Ranks producers by **total play count** across all published tracks.

**What counts:**

- Authentic listening sessions from unique users
- Minimum listen duration threshold must be met
- Bot/artificial plays are filtered out

**Metadata shown:**

- Total plays across catalog
- Average plays per track
- Track count contributing to total

<Warning>
  Artificial play manipulation is detected and filtered. Focus on creating quality content that attracts genuine listeners.
</Warning>

---

### Uploads Rankings

Ranks producers by **number of published tracks** on the platform.

**What counts:**

- Only approved, public tracks are counted
- Draft or private tracks do not contribute
- Removed tracks are deducted from count

**Benefits of consistent uploading:**

- Higher visibility in discovery algorithms
- More opportunities for plays and engagement
- Demonstrates active producer presence

---

### Quality Rankings

Ranks producers by **metadata completeness** across their catalog.

**Quality score components (25% each):**

| Field     | Requirement                       |
| --------- | --------------------------------- |
| **BPM**   | Track tempo must be set           |
| **Key**   | Musical key must be specified     |
| **Scale** | Major/Minor scale indicated       |
| **Tags**  | At least one genre/mood tag added |

**Additional factors:**

- Track descriptions boost completion
- Complete tracks (all fields filled) count toward ranking
- Average is calculated across all tracks

<Tip>
  Navigate to **Producer Dashboard â†’ Tracks** to quickly identify and fix incomplete metadata.
</Tip>

**Metadata shown:**

### Collaboration Rankings

Ranks producers by their **collaborative activity** with other artists.

**What contributes to collaboration score:**

- Number of collaborative tracks (multiple artists)
- Unique collaborator count
- Combined plays on collaborative works
- Collaboration-related achievements

<Info>

## Real-Time Updates

### Data Freshness

BeatPass leaderboards use a multi-layer caching system for performance:

| Layer                  | Refresh Rate  | Purpose                         |
| ---------------------- | ------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Server cache**       | 5 minutes     | Database query optimization     |
| **Client cache**       | 2 minutes     | Fast category switching         |
| **Background refresh** | 3 minutes     | Automatic updates while viewing |
| **Focus refresh**      | On tab return | Fresh data when you return      |

<Note>
  Rankings typically reflect changes within 5-10 minutes of the underlying activity.
</Note>

## Tips to Improve Your Rankings

### Overall Ranking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Balance All Categories">
    Focus on improving your weakest category rather than maxing out one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Maintain Consistency">
    Regular activity across all metrics beats sporadic bursts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Your Progress">
    Check rankings weekly to identify trends and adjust strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Points (XP) Ranking

- Complete all available achievements
- Upload tracks regularly for consistent XP
- Collaborate with other producers
- Fill out your artist profile completely

### Plays Ranking

- Promote your tracks on social media
- Create engaging, high-quality content
- Optimize track titles and descriptions for discovery
- Engage with your listener community

### Uploads Ranking

- Maintain a consistent release schedule
- Focus on quantity while maintaining quality
- Consider releasing singles alongside albums
- Keep content fresh and relevant

### Quality Ranking

- Always fill in BPM, Key, and Scale for every track
- Add descriptive tags that match your music
- Write compelling track descriptions
- Review and update older tracks with missing data

### Collaboration Ranking

- Reach out to producers in complementary genres
- Feature other artists on your tracks
- Join collaboration challenges or events
- Credit all collaborators properly on uploads

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I appear on any leaderboard?">
    You need at least one published, public track to appear in rankings. Draft, private, or removed tracks don't count. Upload and publish a track to become eligible.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often do rankings update?">
    Rankings update approximately every 5 minutes. When you switch categories, you'll see the latest cached data, with background refreshes every 3 minutes while viewing the page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Overall and individual category rankings?">
    Individual category rankings measure one specific metric (e.g., total plays). The Overall ranking combines all categories with weighted percentages to provide a holistic view of producer performance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see my exact rank in each category?">
    Yes! The Overall leaderboard metadata shows your rank in each component category (e.g., "#3 Points â€¢ #7 Plays â€¢ #12 Uploads").
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why did my rank drop even though I uploaded a new track?">
    Rankings are relative to other producers. If others uploaded more content or their tracks performed better, your relative position may decrease even with your own growth.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are plays from bots or artificial sources counted?">
    No. BeatPass uses fraud detection to filter artificial plays. Only authentic listening sessions meeting minimum duration requirements count toward rankings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is the Quality score calculated?">
    Quality measures metadata completeness: BPM (25%) + Key (25%) + Scale (25%) + Tags (25%). A track with all fields filled scores 100%. Your ranking is based on the average across all tracks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I delete a track?">
    Deleting a track removes it from all calculations. Your Upload count decreases, total plays drop, and Quality average recalculates. Rankings update within 5-10 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I hide my profile from leaderboards?">
    Currently, all producers with published tracks automatically appear in leaderboards. This ensures fair, transparent competition. Contact support if you have privacy concerns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do private or unlisted tracks count toward rankings?">
    No. Only public, approved tracks contribute to leaderboard calculations. This ensures all ranked producers are discoverable by the community.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
