The current routed **Producer Dashboard -> Leaderboards** page does **not** expose a genre leaderboard tab or genre selector.

<Info>
  If you are using the normal producer dashboard flow, you should not expect a genre-based ranking view on the leaderboard page today.
</Info>

## What To Use Instead

- Use the standard leaderboard categories in [Leaderboard Categories](/help/gamification/leaderboards/categories).
- Use your artist, album, and track analytics to understand where your releases are performing best.
- Use your upload catalog and profile positioning to organize genre-specific strategy outside the leaderboard page.
