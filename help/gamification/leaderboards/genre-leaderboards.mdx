## Genre Rankings Overview

BeatPass does not currently have dedicated **genre-specific leaderboards**. Instead, genre performance is recognized through the **achievement system**.

<Info>
  The 7 leaderboard categories are: Overall, Points, Plays, Uploads, Quality, Collaboration, and Earnings. Genre-specific rankings may be added in future updates.
</Info>

## Genre Diversity

Genre diversity is tracked by:

1. **Counting unique genres** across your track catalog
2. **Calculating diversity score** based on genre spread
3. **Comparing to other producers** for percentile ranking

<Note>
  To maximize genre achievements, upload tracks across different genres while maintaining quality.
</Note>

Genre-specific leaderboards may be introduced in future platform updates. These could include:
