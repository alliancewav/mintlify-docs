The **Leaderboards** page in your Producer Dashboard lets you filter rankings by category. Here are the available categories:

## Categories

| Category | What it reflects |
| --- | --- |
| **Overall** | A combined ranking across multiple factors |
| **Quality** | Metadata completeness and track preparation |
| **Uploads** | How much music you have published |
| **Plays** | Total listening activity on your tracks |
| **Points** | Your XP total |
| **Earnings** | Earnings-related ranking data |
| **Collab** | Collaboration activity with other producers |

## How Rankings Work

Each category ranks producers based on the activity described above. The exact calculations behind each ranking may adjust over time. Use the **How it works** link on the leaderboard page for the most current explanation of how rankings are determined.

<Note>
The leaderboard page does not currently include genre-specific or time-period filters. All rankings reflect cumulative activity.
</Note>
