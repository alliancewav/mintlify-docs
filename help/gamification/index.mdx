BeatPass gamification is built around the **Progress** and **Leaderboards** pages in your Producer Dashboard. These tools let producers track XP, unlock achievements, and compare their standing across ranking categories.

## What the Current Product Includes

- A **Progress** page with your level title, XP total, and XP-to-next progress
- Achievement tracking with unlocked, locked, and total XP-earned summaries
- A **Leaderboards** page with category switching for **Overall**, **Quality**, **Uploads**, **Plays**, **Points**, **Earnings**, and **Collab**

## Getting There

Open your **Producer Dashboard** from the sidebar, then choose **Progress** or **Leaderboards**.
