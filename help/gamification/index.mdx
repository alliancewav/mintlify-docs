BeatPass gamification is currently centered on the **Producer Progress** and **Producer Leaderboards** pages. These tools let producers track XP, unlock achievements, and compare their standing across a set of ranking categories.

<Info>
  The routed producer experience currently lives in **Producer Dashboard -> Progress** and **Producer Dashboard -> Leaderboards**. Some older docs and screenshots referenced additional public leaderboard surfaces that are not part of the standard routed producer flow.
</Info>

## What The Current Product Highlights

- A progress page with level title, XP total, and XP-to-next progress.
- Achievement tracking with unlocked, locked, and total XP-earned summaries.
- A leaderboard page with category switching for **Overall**, **Quality**, **Uploads**, **Plays**, **Points**, **Earnings**, and **Collab**.

## Extra Context

Some artist-profile views also link to a standalone **Producer Achievements Guide** page at `/producer/achievements-guide`. Use that as a general orientation page, but trust your live **Progress** page for your current XP, level, and unlocked achievements.
