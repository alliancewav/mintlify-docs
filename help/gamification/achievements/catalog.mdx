The live achievement catalog is shown on your **Progress** page in the Producer Dashboard. This is the most current and reliable source for which achievements exist, their rarity, and their XP rewards.

## Where to View the Catalog

Open your **Producer Dashboard**, then go to **Progress**. Scroll to the achievements section to see all unlocked and locked achievements for your account.

## Achievement Themes

Achievements in BeatPass are built around producer activity themes such as:

- **Upload** — Publishing tracks
- **Popularity** — Gaining plays and listeners
- **Contribution** — Earning through the platform
- **Quality** — Completing metadata and track preparation
- **Collaboration** — Working with other producers

## What to Trust

Your **Progress** page is the source of truth for:

- Which achievements exist for your account right now
- Whether an achievement is locked or unlocked
- The XP reward attached to each achievement
- The rarity label currently applied to it

<Note>
The achievement list can grow over time. Older screenshots or external references may show a different set than what you see today.
</Note>
