BeatPass does not currently expose a fixed help-page checklist that should be treated as the permanent achievement catalog. The live catalog is the one shown in-product on your **Progress** page and related producer-achievement surfaces.

<Info>
  Older documentation that promised an exact number of categories or achievements is not reliable enough for the current product.
</Info>

## Themes You Will Commonly See

Current achievement data in BeatPass is built around producer activity themes such as:

- upload
- popularity
- contribution
- quality
- collaboration

## Best Practice

Treat the live UI as the source of truth for:

- which achievements exist for your account right now
- whether an achievement is locked or unlocked
- the XP reward attached to that achievement
- the rarity label currently applied to it
