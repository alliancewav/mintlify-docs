## Rarity System

Each achievement has a rarity level (1-4) that indicates difficulty and XP reward:

| Rarity | Level | XP Range | Difficulty |
|--------|-------|----------|------------|
| **Common** | 1 | 100-1,000 XP | Entry-level, most producers can earn |
| **Rare** | 2 | 1,500-2,000 XP | Requires consistent effort |
| **Epic** | 3 | 2,500-3,500 XP | Significant accomplishment |
| **Legendary** | 4 | 4,000-6,000 XP | Elite producer milestone |

## Visual Indicators

### Achievement Cards

Unlocked achievements display with rarity-based colors:

| Rarity | Background | Icon |
|--------|------------|------|
| Common | Gray tint | Gray star |
| Rare | Blue tint | Blue star |
| Epic | Purple tint | Purple star |
| Legendary | Amber tint + shimmer | Amber star + glow |

**Locked achievements** show with red-tinted backgrounds and lock icons.

### Special Visual Effects

Legendary achievements feature special visual effects:
- **Legendary**: Golden shimmer animation, amber tint background, and ring glow around the icon

**Locked achievements** always display with red-tinted backgrounds and lock icons, regardless of their actual rarity.
