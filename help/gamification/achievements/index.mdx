## Achievement Rarity

Achievements are categorized by rarity, which determines their XP reward:

| Rarity        | XP Range       | Description                                      |
| ------------- | -------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Common**    | 100-1,000 XP   | Entry-level achievements most producers can earn |
| **Rare**      | 1,500-2,000 XP | Requires consistent effort                       |
| **Epic**      | 2,500-3,500 XP | Significant accomplishments                      |
| **Legendary** | 4,000-6,000 XP | Elite achievements for exceptional producers     |

## How Achievements Work

### Checking Criteria

Achievements are checked automatically when relevant actions occur:

- **Upload achievements** â€” Checked when you upload a track
- **Play achievements** â€” Checked when your tracks receive plays
- **Profile achievements** â€” Checked when you update your profile
- **Milestone achievements** â€” Checked periodically based on account age
- **Seasonal achievements** â€” Checked when uploading during holiday periods

### Percentile-Based Achievements

Many achievements use percentile rankings (e.g., "top 10%"). These are calculated relative to all producers on the platform:

- Rankings update as the platform grows
- Your percentile can change even without new activity
- Focus on consistent improvement rather than specific rankings

### Permanent Unlocks

Once earned, achievements are **permanent**:

- Achievements cannot be lost
- XP from achievements is added to your total
- Even if criteria change, earned achievements remain
