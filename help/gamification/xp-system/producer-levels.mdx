BeatPass shows your level as a combination of:

- a **level number**
- a **producer title**
- your **current XP**
- your **progress percentage**
- the **XP remaining to the next level**

## Where You See Your Level

## How Progress Works In Practice

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="XP increases your level progress">
    Every eligible XP event moves your progress forward.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The page shows XP to next">
    You do not need to calculate the next step manually; BeatPass shows the remaining XP needed for the next level.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Trust the live page">
    If you see old screenshots or older documentation that use different level ladders or title names, trust what your current **Producer Progress** page shows.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why This Doc Does Not Publish A Full Threshold Table

BeatPass currently presents the level result directly in the product UI. The safest way to understand your current standing is to use the live page rather than relying on an older static threshold chart.
