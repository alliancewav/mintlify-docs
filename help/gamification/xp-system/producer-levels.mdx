BeatPass shows your level as a combination of:

- A **level number**
- A **producer title** (e.g. Novice, Apprentice, Rising, Beat Master, and so on up to Legendary Producer)
- Your **current XP**
- A **progress ring** showing percentage toward the next level
- The **XP remaining** to the next level

## Where You See Your Level

Your **Progress** page in the Producer Dashboard is the primary place to see your current level, title, and progress.

## How Progress Works

Every eligible XP event moves your progress forward. As you earn XP from uploads, plays, metadata, collaborations, and achievements, your progress ring fills and you advance through producer levels.

The page shows exactly how much XP you still need for the next level, so you do not need to calculate it yourself.

## Level Titles

Producer levels follow a progression from beginner to expert. The level titles shown on your Progress page include names like **Novice**, **Apprentice**, **Rising**, **Beat Master**, and so on through higher tiers. Your current title and the next title are always visible on the Progress page.

<Note>
If you see older screenshots or references with different level names, trust what your current **Progress** page shows.
</Note>
