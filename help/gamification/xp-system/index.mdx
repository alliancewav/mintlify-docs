BeatPass awards **XP** to producer accounts for specific activity. That XP feeds the level and progress display on your **Progress** page and also powers the **Points** leaderboard category.

## Where XP Shows Up

- On the **Progress** page header as your current XP total
- In the progress ring and XP-to-next summary
- In the **Points** category on the **Leaderboards** page
- In achievement rewards when BeatPass grants XP for an unlock

## Important Notes

- Achievement XP rewards are not all the same size
- Some special achievements count toward progress without awarding XP
- Collaboration XP depends on the collaboration rules shown in the XP guide
