BeatPass awards **XP** to producer accounts for specific activity. That XP feeds the level and progress UI on the **Producer Progress** page and also powers the **Points** leaderboard category.

<Info>
  The live source of truth for your current XP total and level progress is `/producer-dashboard/progress`.
</Info>

## Where XP Shows Up

- On the **Producer Progress** header as your current XP total.
- In your progress ring and XP-to-next summary.
- In the **Points** category on **Producer Leaderboards**.
- In achievement rewards when BeatPass grants XP for an unlock.

## Important Notes

- Achievement rewards are not all the same size.
- Some special achievements can count toward progress without awarding XP.
- Collaboration-related XP depends on the current collaboration rules shown in the product.
