## Use the Right Path

| Need | Best path |
|------|-----------|
| General question about BeatPass | [Getting Help](/help/contact-support/getting-help) |
| Bug, broken page, playback problem, or account issue | [Report an Issue](/help/contact-support/reporting-issues) |
| Feature request or product suggestion | [Feedback](/help/contact-support/feedback) |
| Download, billing, or login fix you can try yourself first | [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) |
| Copyright or takedown concern | [Copyright & DMCA](/help/legal/copyright-and-dmca) |

<Note>
  BeatPass currently has a verified in-app **Report comment** action for comments. Other issues still rely on the contact form or direct email.
</Note>

## What BeatPass Can Help With

- Account access and security questions
- Billing, renewals, coupons, and invoice questions
- Download, licensing, and certificate issues
- Producer application, upload, or dashboard problems
- Bug reports, playback problems, and broken page behavior
- Copyright, ownership, and content policy concerns

For simple “how do I…” questions, the Help Center is usually faster than waiting for a reply. For account-specific or payment-specific issues, contact BeatPass directly so the team can review your situation.

---

## Before Contacting Support

To help BeatPass resolve your issue faster, include:

- The email address connected to your BeatPass account
- The page, track, playlist, producer profile, or request involved
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Any exact error message you saw
- Screenshots when they add useful context

## Response Expectations

The current public contact page says BeatPass typically responds within **24-48 hours** during business days. Treat that as a target rather than a guaranteed service-level promise, especially for complex payment, licensing, or ownership issues.
