Found a bug or need to report problematic content? This guide explains how to report issues so the BeatPass team can investigate and address them quickly.

---

## Types of Issues You Can Report

### Technical Issues (Bugs)

Report when something isn't working correctly:

- App crashes or freezes
- Features not working as expected
- Display or layout problems
- Playback issues
- Upload failures
- Error messages

### Content Issues

Report problematic content:

- Copyright infringement
- Inappropriate content
- Misleading information
- Stolen or unauthorized uploads
- Spam or abuse

### Account Issues

Report when there's a problem with your account:

- Suspicious activity
- Unauthorized access
- Account security concerns

## How to Report a Bug

### Using the Contact Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Contact Page">
    Navigate to the **Contact** page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Your Information">
    Enter your **name** and **email address**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the Bug">
    Include all relevant details (see below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Send Message** to submit your report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What to Include in a Bug Report

A good bug report helps the team find and fix the issue faster:

| Information | Why It Helps |
|-------------|--------------|
| **What you were doing** | Helps us reproduce the issue |
| **What you expected** | Clarifies what should happen |
| **What actually happened** | Describes the bug behavior |
| **Device and browser** | Helps identify platform-specific issues |
| **Screenshots/recordings** | Shows exactly what you're seeing |
| **Error messages** | Provides technical details |

### Example Bug Report

> "When I try to upload a track on Chrome (Version 118) on Windows 11:
>
> 1. I select a WAV file (about 25MB)
> 2. The upload progress reaches 100%
> 3. Then I see 'Upload failed' with error code 413
>
> Expected: The track should upload successfully
>
> This happens every time with files over 20MB."

## How to Report Content Issues

### Copyright Concerns

If you believe content infringes on your copyright:

1. Contact us at **contact@beatpass.ca** or through the **Contact** page
2. Include:
   - The specific track or content URL
   - Evidence of your ownership
   - Your contact information
3. The team will review and respond within 24-48 hours

<Note>
  Per the BeatPass Terms of Service, Producers retain all ownership of their Beats. If you believe your content has been uploaded without authorization, please include proof of your original ownership.
</Note>

### Inappropriate Content

To report content that violates the community guidelines:

1. Use the **Contact** page
2. Provide:
   - Link to the content
   - Reason for the report
   - Any additional context

<Note>
  Reports are reviewed by the BeatPass team and handled confidentially. The person who uploaded the content is not notified of who reported it.
</Note>

## What Happens After You Report

### For Bug Reports

1. **Acknowledgment** — Receipt of your report is confirmed
2. **Investigation** — The BeatPass team attempts to reproduce the issue
3. **Prioritization** — Severity and impact are assessed
4. **Resolution** — A fix is developed
5. **Update** — You may be contacted when resolved

### For Content Reports

1. **Review** — The BeatPass team reviews the reported content
2. **Assessment** — The team determines if it violates platform policies
3. **Action** — Appropriate action is taken (removal, warning, etc.)
4. **Notification** — You may receive an update on the outcome

### Response Time

| Issue Type | Expected Response |
|------------|-------------------|
| **Critical bugs** (app unusable) | Within 24 hours |
| **Standard bugs** | 24-48 hours |
| **Content reports** | 24-48 hours |
| **Security issues** | Prioritized immediately |

## Before Reporting

### For Technical Issues

Try these basic troubleshooting steps first:

1. **Refresh the page** — Sometimes issues are temporary
2. **Clear browser cache** — Cached data can cause problems
3. **Try a different browser** — Isolates browser-specific issues
4. **Check your internet connection** — Connectivity issues can mimic bugs
5. **Log out and log back in** — Refreshes your session

### Check Known Issues

Some issues may already be known and being addressed. Check back in this Help Center for updates.

## Urgent Issues

### Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it immediately through the Contact page. Include "SECURITY" in your message for priority handling.

### Account Compromise

If you believe your account has been compromised:

1. Change your password immediately
2. Contact the support team through the Contact page
3. Include "URGENT: Account Security" in your message

Security issues are taken very seriously and will receive a response as quickly as possible.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Playback not working">
    - Check if your browser allows audio playback
    - Try disabling browser extensions
    - Ensure you're not in a private/incognito window with restricted permissions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Upload keeps failing">
    - Check that your file meets format requirements
    - Ensure you have a stable internet connection
    - Try a smaller file to test
    - Clear browser cache and try again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't log in">
    - Verify you're using the correct email
    - Try resetting your password
    - Clear cookies and try again
    - Check if your account email is verified
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
