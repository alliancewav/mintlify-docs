If downloads are not working, start by checking your account state and the track's licensing state.

## I Can't See the Download Button

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="You need a paid plan">
    Standard BeatPass downloads require a paid subscription. Free accounts can stream and save music, but they do not unlock licensed downloads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="You are not logged in">
    BeatPass requires an authenticated account for download actions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="This track is not available through the standard download flow">
    Some tracks are exclusive-only, sold exclusively, or otherwise not available for subscription downloads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## The Download Action Fails

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My session expired">
    Sign out and back in again, then retry the download.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My subscription has a billing problem">
    If your payment is overdue, BeatPass can disable downloads and licensing until you resolve billing or switch back to the free state.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The browser download never starts">
    Check popup or download blocking, clear browser cache, and try another modern browser.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Exclusive Tracks Won't Download

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The track is exclusive-only">
    Exclusive-only tracks are not part of the standard subscription download flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The track was already sold exclusively">
    Once a track is sold exclusively, other users cannot download it through the standard non-exclusive route.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## I Downloaded a Track but Can't Find My License

For qualifying downloads, BeatPass stores your certificates in:

1. **Library**
2. **Licenses**

If you do not see the certificate entry:

- Refresh the page
- Confirm the download actually completed
- Make sure the track qualified for certificate generation under the standard licensed flow

## File Format Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What file format should I expect?">
    The exact file bundle depends on the track and license flow. BeatPass's standard subscription download flow should not be treated as a guaranteed WAV-plus-MP3 bundle for every track.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I choose between file formats during a normal subscription download?">
    BeatPass does not currently document a manual format picker in the standard subscription download flow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## I Downloaded the Wrong Track

If you accidentally downloaded the wrong track, simply download the correct eligible track instead. If you believe a platform error caused the issue, contact **contact@beatpass.ca** with the track URL and account email.
