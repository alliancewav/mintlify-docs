## Quick Fixes

These general steps can sometimes help with minor or temporary issues:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Refresh the page">
    Press **F5** or **Ctrl+R** (Windows) / **Cmd+R** (Mac) to reload and clear temporary glitches.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Clear your browser cache">
    Outdated cached data can occasionally cause problems. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Try a different browser">
    If the issue continues, test with a different modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check your internet connection">
    Make sure you have a stable connection. Try loading other websites to confirm.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Disable browser extensions">
    Some extensions (especially ad blockers) can interfere with BeatPass. Try disabling them temporarily.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
