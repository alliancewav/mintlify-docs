## Quick Fixes

Before diving into specific issues, try these general solutions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Refresh the page">
    Many temporary issues can be resolved by refreshing your browser. Press **F5** or **Ctrl+R** (Windows) or **Cmd+R** (Mac).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Clear your browser cache">
    Outdated cached data can cause problems. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Try a different browser">
    If issues persist, try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try loading other websites to confirm.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Disable browser extensions">
    Some browser extensions (especially ad blockers) can interfere with BeatPass. Try disabling them temporarily.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
