Having trouble accessing your BeatPass account? Start with the issue that matches your current screen.

## I Forgot My Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Go to the BeatPass login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Forgot password?">
    Use the **Forgot password?** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Submit the email address attached to your BeatPass account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the email link">
    BeatPass sends a link that lets you reset or create your password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Password reset links expire. If the link stops working, request a new one.
</Note>

## I Can't Log In

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wrong email or password">
    Double-check the email address, turn off Caps Lock, and try again. If you are unsure, request a password reset.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Too many attempts or temporary lockout">
    Wait a few minutes before trying again, then use the correct credentials or a password reset link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The login page is not loading correctly">
    Refresh the page, clear browser cache, disable extensions that interfere with auth pages, and test another modern browser.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I signed up with Google">
    Use **Continue with Google** and sign in with the same Google account linked to BeatPass.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Two-Factor Authentication Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't have my authenticator app">
    Use one of your saved recovery codes from the 2FA setup flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My 2FA code is not working">
    Wait for a fresh code, make sure your device time is correct, and enter the code before it expires.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I lost my recovery codes">
    Contact **contact@beatpass.ca** and be ready to verify account ownership before BeatPass can help with 2FA recovery.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Google Sign-In Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Google popup is blocked">
    Allow popups for BeatPass and try **Continue with Google** again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I connected the wrong Google account">
    Go to **Account Settings** and review the social-login connection there. Disconnect the wrong Google account before reconnecting the right one.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I already have a BeatPass account with this email">
    Use the account-linking prompts if BeatPass offers them, or sign in with your email and password first and then review account settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Email Verification Problems

BeatPass currently requires email confirmation.

If you did not receive the verification email:

1. Check spam or junk folders
2. Make sure you are looking at the correct inbox
3. Use **Resend email** on the verification page
4. Remember that verification links expire after **24 hours**

## I Need to Change My Account Email

BeatPass's current account settings do not expose a self-service email-change form. If you need help with the email attached to your account, contact support from the address you can still access if possible.

## My Account Is Suspended or Locked

- Temporary lockouts can happen after repeated failed login attempts
- For a suspension or an access block you do not understand, contact **contact@beatpass.ca**
