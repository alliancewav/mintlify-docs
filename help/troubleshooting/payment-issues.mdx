## My Payment Was Declined

If your payment wasn't processed:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Card was declined">
    **Common reasons:**
    - Insufficient funds
    - Card expired
    - Bank blocked the transaction
    - Incorrect card details
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Check that your card details are entered correctly
    2. Ensure your card hasn't expired
    3. Contact your bank to authorize the transaction
    4. Try a different payment method
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment processing error">
    If you see a generic error message:
    1. Wait a few minutes and try again
    2. Refresh the page and re-enter your payment details
    3. Try a different browser
    4. If the issue persists, contact support at **contact@beatpass.ca**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Subscription Management

### Viewing Your Subscription

To view your current subscription:

1. Click your profile picture in the top right
2. Select **Billing** from the menu
3. You'll see your current plan and billing details

### Changing Your Plan

To upgrade or downgrade your subscription:

1. Go to **Profile Menu** → **Billing**
2. Click **Change plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the change

<Note>
  When you change plans, the new rate takes effect at your next billing cycle. You'll keep your current plan benefits until then.
</Note>

### Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Profile Menu** → **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel plan**
3. Confirm your cancellation

After canceling:
- You'll keep your plan benefits until the end of your current billing period
- Your account will revert to the free Explorer plan
- You won't be charged again unless you resubscribe

## I Was Charged Incorrectly

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Charged the wrong amount">
    Check your billing page to see your subscription details. If the charge doesn't match your plan price, contact support at **contact@beatpass.ca** with:
    - Your account email
    - The date and amount of the charge
    - A screenshot of the charge from your bank statement
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Charged after canceling">
    If you were charged after canceling:
    1. Confirm your cancellation went through — check **Profile Menu** → **Billing**
    2. If you canceled recently, the charge may be for the period before cancellation
    3. If the charge seems incorrect, contact support at **contact@beatpass.ca**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Double charged">
    Duplicate charges can happen due to:
    - Multiple subscription attempts
    - Technical errors
    
    Contact support at **contact@beatpass.ca** — the team will investigate and refund any duplicate charges.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updating Payment Information

To update your payment method:

1. Go to **Profile Menu** → **Billing**
2. Click **Update** next to your payment method
3. Enter your new card details
4. Save the changes

<Tip>
  Update your payment information before your card expires to avoid service interruption.
</Tip>

## Subscription Plans

BeatPass offers the following subscription plans:

| Plan | Price | Benefits |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Explorer** | Free | Stream unlimited tracks, create playlists, like and follow |
| **Classic** | $29 CAD/month | All Explorer features + unlimited downloads, license certificates |
| **Plus** | $45 CAD/month | All Classic features + priority support, advanced features |
| **Pro** | $59 CAD/month | All Plus features + premium benefits, early access to new features |

## Refund Policy

All BeatPass sales are generally **final** due to the nature of digital goods. Refunds are only considered in limited circumstances.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund on my subscription?">
    Subscription payments are **non-refundable**. When you cancel, you keep access until your billing period ends. No prorated refunds are issued for unused time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund on a beat purchase?">
    Exclusive beat purchases are final once files are downloaded or a license is generated. A refund may be considered **only within 24 hours** of purchase if the purchase was accidental and no files were downloaded. After 24 hours, the purchase is considered deliberate.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Accidental purchase">
    If you made an accidental purchase and have **not** downloaded any files, email [contact@beatpass.ca](mailto:contact@beatpass.ca?subject=Refund%20Request) immediately (within 24 hours) with your account email and transaction details.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I want to dispute a charge">
    **Always contact BeatPass support first** before filing a chargeback with your bank. Filing a chargeback without contacting us may result in account suspension. See the full [Refund Policy](/help/billing/refund-policy) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## My Subscription Benefits Aren't Working

If you've subscribed but can't access premium features:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your subscription status">
    Go to **Profile Menu** → **Billing** to confirm your subscription is active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and back in">
    Sometimes a fresh login is needed to sync your account permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear your browser cache">
    Old cached data can cause display issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If the issue persists, email **contact@beatpass.ca** with your account email.
  </Step>
</Steps>
