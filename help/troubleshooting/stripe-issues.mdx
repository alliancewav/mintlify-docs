This guide is for producers using Stripe Connect through BeatPass.

<Note>
  Stripe Connect issues are separate from subscriber billing issues. If you are trying to pay for a subscription, use [Payment Issues](/help/troubleshooting/payment-issues).
</Note>

## Start in the Right Place

Producer payout setup and status live in **Producer Dashboard** → **Finances**.

If you manage more than one producer profile, make sure you are looking at the correct selected artist before troubleshooting balances or onboarding state.

## I Haven't Connected Stripe Yet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Finances">
    Go to **Producer Dashboard** → **Finances**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your country">
    BeatPass currently starts onboarding with a country-confirmation step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to Stripe">
    Finish the Stripe Connect onboarding flow hosted by Stripe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## I Opened the Wrong Stripe Dashboard

BeatPass uses **Stripe Express**, not a standard Stripe dashboard account.

Use:

- [connect.stripe.com/express_login](https://connect.stripe.com/express_login) for BeatPass payouts
- `dashboard.stripe.com` only for separate, regular Stripe accounts you manage outside BeatPass

## The Connect Button or Redirect Isn't Working

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Nothing happens when I start onboarding">
    Check popup blocking, refresh the page, and retry in another browser if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stripe opens but I can't finish">
    Complete the missing identity or bank details Stripe requests. If Stripe rejects a document, use Stripe's guidance or support from the Express flow itself.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My country is not available in the BeatPass country step">
    If BeatPass does not offer your country in the onboarding dialog, the current producer payout flow cannot continue for that country. Contact support if you need clarification on eligibility.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## My Account Shows Connected but Not Fully Ready

Finances can show a connected account that still lacks full payout readiness.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Charges or payouts are not enabled">
    Your Stripe account still has missing requirements. Re-open the setup flow from **Producer Dashboard** → **Finances** or log in to Stripe Express to complete the pending steps.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The page says pending or verification required">
    Stripe is still reviewing information or waiting on more details. Check the Express dashboard for requirements before contacting BeatPass.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## I'm Not Receiving Money

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Subscription payouts are queued">
    A queued payout means BeatPass calculated the split but is waiting for platform funds to become available in Stripe. This does not automatically mean your Stripe account is broken.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My bank details are wrong">
    Sign in to Stripe Express and review the payout destination there.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I have no visible payout yet">
    Check the selected artist in **Producer Dashboard** → **Finances**, review the transaction list, and remember that BeatPass financial data can include cached metadata rather than instant live settlement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see mixed-currency warnings">
    BeatPass can surface mixed-currency balance states in Finances. Review the balance details carefully before assuming money is missing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What Can Flow Through Stripe Connect?

BeatPass currently uses Stripe Connect for:

- Monthly payout activity
- Exclusive sales
- Track purchases

<Tip>
For exclusive sales and track purchases, BeatPass is designed so the producer keeps 100% of the listed price and the platform fee is added on top for the buyer.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my bank account?">
    Manage payout destinations from Stripe Express after logging in with the onboarding email or phone verification flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I view my Stripe Express dashboard directly?">
    Yes. Use [connect.stripe.com/express_login](https://connect.stripe.com/express_login).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does BeatPass see from Stripe?">
    BeatPass can see connection and readiness status, but Stripe keeps the sensitive banking and identity details inside its own onboarding flow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
