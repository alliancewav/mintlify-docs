Setting the right exclusive price matters because it controls both how your beat is positioned and how easy it is for a buyer to move from interest to checkout.

## Current Price Fields

For exclusive-capable beats, BeatPass currently lets you set:

- **Exclusive price**
- **Currency**
- **Exclusive contact email**

The price appears anywhere BeatPass shows the beat as instantly purchasable.

## Current Supported Currencies

BeatPass currently supports these exclusive sale currencies:

- **USD**
- **EUR**
- **GBP**
- **CAD**

If you need a different currency, do not document it or promise it until the product actually supports it.

## Platform Fee Behavior

BeatPass adds the platform fee to the buyer's total instead of subtracting it from your listed exclusive amount.

| Your Listed Price | Buyer Pays |
|-------------------|------------|
| \$50 | \$59.00 |
| \$100 | \$115.00 |
| \$200 | \$227.00 |

## Pricing Factors to Consider

### Beat Quality and Demand

Higher prices make more sense when the beat has:

- Strong production quality
- Unique sound or obvious market fit
- Proven play activity or buyer interest
- Premium deliverables such as stems, MIDI, or full trackouts

### Your Catalog Strategy

Think about whether the beat is:

- A broad discovery beat that benefits from **Standard + Exclusive**
- A premium record you want to protect as **Exclusive Only**
- A flexible catalog beat where a moderate exclusive price makes sense

### Your Reputation

Pricing should also reflect:

- Your current credits
- Your catalog depth
- The quality of your profile presentation
- How often buyers already engage with your beats

## Contact Email Matters

BeatPass requires a contact email on exclusive-capable tracks. Buyers may use it when:

- They have licensing questions
- They want to discuss custom terms
- Instant purchase is not the right fit for the situation

## Payment & Payout Notes

When someone completes an exclusive purchase:

1. The buyer pays your listed price plus the BeatPass platform fee
2. BeatPass records the sale in your producer finances
3. Stripe handles settlement and payout to your connected account

<Info>
  You need Stripe Connect set up before BeatPass can pay you out for exclusive sales.
</Info>

## After the Beat Sells

Once a beat is sold exclusively:

- The beat is marked **Sold**
- The exclusive terms are locked
- BeatPass stops new standard licenses for that beat

That means price changes only matter **before** the sale happens.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the price later?">
    Yes, as long as the beat has not already sold exclusively.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Should every beat have the same price?">
    Usually no. It is more practical to price around demand, deliverables, and how premium the beat really is.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I want to talk to the buyer first?">
    Use the exclusive contact email so buyers can reach out before or instead of immediate purchase.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to document Stripe's exact fees here?">
    No. BeatPass docs should explain the platform fee and payout dependency without overpromising processor-specific fees that can vary.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
