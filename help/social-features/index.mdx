## Quick Start

### Building Your Network

1. **Follow producers** you enjoy by clicking the **Follow** button on their profile or producer page
2. **Browse your feed** to see new uploads from producers you follow
3. **Repost tracks** you love to share them with your own followers
4. **Comment on tracks** to engage with the community and show appreciation

## Who Can Use Social Features?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="All Visitors">
    - View producer and user profiles
    - View follower/following lists
    - Share content via links
    - View comments on tracks
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Logged-in Users">
    All the above, plus:

    - Follow/unfollow users and producers
    - Leave comments on tracks and albums

    - Receive notifications for social activity

  <Accordion title="Producers">

    - Receive notifications when users comment or repost your content
    - Delete inappropriate comments on your own tracks

## Notification Preferences

Control how you receive social notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notification Preferences">
    Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation, then click the **settings gear** to open your notification preferences. Or go directly to `/notifications/settings`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Preferences">
    Toggle notifications on or off for each type:

    - **New Follower** — When someone follows your profile
    - **Comments & Replies** — When someone comments on your beats or replies to your comments
    - **Reposts** — When someone reposts your beats (producers only)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Delivery Channels">
    For each notification type, choose how you want to receive it:

    - **Email** — Sent to your email address
    - **Browser** — Real-time push notifications (requires permission)
    - **In-App** — Shows in your notification center
  </Step>
</Steps>
