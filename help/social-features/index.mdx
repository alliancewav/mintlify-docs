BeatPass includes a small set of public social features that work across profiles, producer pages, tracks, albums, and playlists. This section covers the features that are currently exposed in the web player: following, comments, reposts, and sharing.

<Note>
  Private conversations are documented separately in the [Messaging](/help/messaging) section.
</Note>

---

## Included Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Following System" icon="user-plus" href="/help/social-features/following-system">
    Follow user profiles and producers to keep your library, profile tabs, and notifications up to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Comments" icon="comment-lines" href="/help/social-features/comments">
    Leave public comments on tracks and albums, including time-stamped comments on track waveforms.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reposts" icon="repeat" href="/help/social-features/reposts">
    Repost tracks and albums to your public profile when reposts are enabled on your BeatPass instance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sharing & Embeds" icon="share-nodes" href="/help/social-features/sharing-and-embeds">
    Copy share links for tracks, albums, producers, and playlists, or embed tracks and albums on external sites.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Where These Features Appear

| Surface | Social actions currently exposed |
| --- | --- |
| **User profiles** | Follow or unfollow the user, open public follower/following tabs, start a message if you're logged in |
| **Producer pages** | Follow the producer, share the page, message the claimed owner when available |
| **Tracks** | Leave waveform-linked comments, reply to comments, repost, share, embed |
| **Albums** | Leave standard comments, repost, share, embed |
| **Playlists** | Share the playlist link |

---

## What Social Features Do Not Include

- BeatPass does not currently expose a separate follower activity feed page.
- Embeds are available for **tracks** and **albums** only, not producer pages or playlists.
- Reposts depend on the repost feature being enabled for the current BeatPass installation.
