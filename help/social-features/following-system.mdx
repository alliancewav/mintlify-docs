BeatPass supports two public follow relationships:

- **User follows** for regular profile-to-profile connections
- **Producer follows** for artist pages and producer discovery surfaces

Both can affect your notifications and the public lists shown on profiles.

---

## Following Users

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a user profile">
    Go to the person's public profile from a comment, message shortcut, follower list, or profile link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Follow button">
    Click **Follow** in the profile header. It changes to **Unfollow** after the follow is saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage from the same button">
    Click **Unfollow** at any time to remove the connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

User follow relationships are exposed most clearly on your public profile tabs:

- **Followers**
- **Following**

---

## Following Producers

Producer follows are currently exposed through the producer page and some producer discovery cards.

| Location | How to Follow |
| --- | --- |
| **Producer page header** | Use the **Follow** or **Following** button |
| **Similar Producers panels** | Follow directly from the producer card |
| **Other producer cards** | Use the visible follow action when the card exposes one |

<Info>
  On producer pages, the follow action is tied to the producer profile itself. Messaging the producer is a separate action and only appears when the page is claimed by a user account.
</Info>

---

## Managing Your Follows

| Where to review follows | What you'll see |
| --- | --- |
| **Your profile -> Followers** | Users following your public user profile |
| **Your profile -> Following** | User accounts you follow |
| **Library -> Your producers** | Producers you have followed or saved in your library |
| **Producer page -> Followers tab** | Followers for that producer profile |

---

## Notifications Connected to Following

Following is connected to two notification types:

| Notification | Who can receive it | Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **New Follower** | Everyone | Someone follows your user profile |
| **New Uploads** | Everyone | A producer you follow uploads new beats |

Configure both from [Notification Preferences](/help/notifications/notification-settings).

---

## What Following Does Not Do

<Note>
  BeatPass does not currently provide a dedicated follower activity feed. Following affects public lists, saved producers, messaging suggestions, and notifications instead.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't follow someone">
    Common causes:

    - You're not logged in
    - You're viewing your own user profile
    - The page has not finished loading the current follow state yet
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My follower count seems wrong">
    Refresh the page first. Follower and following counts can take a moment to refresh across every surface.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm not getting new upload notifications">
    Check that:

    - You are following the producer, not just listening to their beats
    - **New Uploads** is enabled in your notification settings
    - Your chosen delivery channel is enabled and working
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
