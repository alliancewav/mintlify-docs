**Your tracks** is the saved-tracks view inside your library. It lives at `/library/songs` and shows the tracks you have added to your personal library.

<Info>
  Current page title: **Your tracks**. Current subtitle: **Your favorite tracks in one place**.
</Info>

## What You Can Do Here

- Search within your saved tracks.
- Play a saved track again from the table.
- Open track and artist pages from the table.
- Use the table controls to sort or filter your saved tracks.

## How Tracks Get Added

Tracks appear here after you save them to your library from the BeatPass player or a track page/card.

## What To Expect On The Page

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Search">
    Use the search field labeled **Search within tracks** to narrow the table without leaving your library.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Playback">
    The table uses the standard BeatPass track controls, so you can cue or play tracks directly from this page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Empty state">
    If nothing has been saved yet, BeatPass shows: `You have not added any songs to your library yet.`
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Uses

- Keep a shortlist of beats you want to revisit later.
- Compare saved tracks before downloading or licensing one.
- Jump back into producers you liked from a saved track row.
