This section lives at `/library/artists` and keeps the producer profiles you have saved in your library.

<Info>
  Current page title: **Your artists**. Current subtitle: **Your favorite producers**.
</Info>

## What You Can Do Here

- Search saved producers with **Search within artists**.
- Sort producers by **Recently added** or **A-Z**.
- Open a producer page from your library.

## How Producers Get Added

A producer appears here after you save that artist profile to your library.

## Important Naming Note

Older documentation referred to this area as **Following**. The current library surface is broader and is labeled around saved or favorite producers rather than a dedicated notification-style follow inbox.

## What To Expect On The Page

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Search and sorting">
    This page supports search plus two sort options: recently added and alphabetical order.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Empty state">
    If you have not saved any producers yet, BeatPass shows: `You have not added any artists to your library yet.`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Notifications">
    Saving a producer to your library is not the same thing as enabling a dedicated release-notification feed from this page. Manage notifications through the product areas that expose notification settings directly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
