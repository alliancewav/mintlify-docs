Your Liked Beats section stores all the beats you've saved by clicking the heart icon. It's a quick way to build a collection of favorites you can return to anytime.

## How to Like a Beat

There are several ways to like a beat:

- **On beat cards** — Click the heart icon when hovering over any beat card
- **On the beat page** — Click the heart button on the beat's detail page
- **In the player** — Click the heart in the player bar while a beat is playing
- **From context menus** — Right-click a beat and select the like option

When you like a beat, it's instantly saved to your library.

## Accessing Your Liked Beats

1. Click **Liked Beats** in the sidebar under Your Library
2. Or navigate to `/library/songs` directly

## Managing Your Liked Beats

### Search

Use the search bar at the top to filter your liked beats by:

- Beat title
- Producer name
- Genre

### Sort Options

Click the sort dropdown to organize your beats by:

| Sort Option | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Recently added** | Most recently liked beats first (default) |
| **A-Z** | Alphabetically by title |
| **Release date** | Newest releases first |

### Play All

Click the **Play** button to start playing all your liked beats as a queue.

## Unlike a Beat

To remove a beat from your liked collection:

1. Click the filled heart icon on any beat you've liked
2. The beat will be removed from your Liked Beats

<Note>
  Unliking a beat doesn't affect any downloads or licenses you have for that beat.
</Note>

## Tips

- **Like as you browse** — Build your collection over time by liking beats that catch your ear
- **Use likes for shortlisting** — Like beats you're considering for a project, then decide later
- **Combine with playlists** — For more organization, add liked beats to themed playlists
