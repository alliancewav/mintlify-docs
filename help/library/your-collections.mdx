**Your collections** is the collections view inside your library. It lives at `/library/albums` and stores the collections you have saved for later.

<Info>
  Current page title: **Your albums**. Current subtitle: **Your favorite collections**.
</Info>

## What You Can Do Here

- Search saved collections with **Search within albums**.
- Open a collection page from your library.
- Re-sort the page by **Recently added**, **A-Z**, or **Release date**.

## How Collections Get Added

Collections appear here after you save them to your library from a collection card or collection page.

## What To Expect On The Page

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Naming">
    The route and page title use **albums**, but the library UI also refers to them as **collections**. Both labels point to the same saved collection area.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sorting">
    This page has built-in sort controls for recently added order, alphabetical order, and release date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Empty state">
    If you have not saved any collections yet, BeatPass shows: `You have not added any albums to your library yet.`
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
