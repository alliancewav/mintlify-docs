The **Licenses** page lives at `/library/licenses` and is the central place to review BeatPass license records tied to your account.

<Info>
  Current page title: **Your licenses**. Current subtitle: **View and manage all your track licenses**.
</Info>

## What You Can Do Here

- Search by track, artist, licensee, or license ID.
- Open the public verification page for a license.
- Download a certificate when that action is available on the license card.
- Review licenses you received and, if you sell licenses on BeatPass, licenses granted to other users.

## What Appears On This Page

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Your own licenses">
    If you downloaded or purchased a licensed track through BeatPass, the related license record can appear here once BeatPass has generated it for your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Granted licenses">
    If you are the owner of tracks sold or granted through BeatPass, this page can also include licenses issued to other users.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Verification">
    Use **Verify License** to open the public verification page for that license record.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Certificate downloads">
    The certificate download action is shown only when the license card has a downloadable certificate ready.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Important Notes

- Older documentation called this page **My Licenses**. The current library label is simply **Licenses**.
- Do not rely on this page as proof that every past download instantly generated a printable file. Availability depends on the current license record and certificate state stored by BeatPass.
- Public verification pages are designed for confirmation, not for exposing your full private account information.
