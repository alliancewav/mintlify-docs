The upload page is where producers add new beats and build collections. This guide covers the upload flow, form fields, validation behavior, and tips for a smooth upload experience.

<ProducerOnly />

## Accessing the Upload Page

Click **Upload beats** in the top navigation bar. The page heading reads **Upload Your Beats** with the sub-text: "Drag and drop your beats, videos & collections here, or click below to browse."

## Upload Modes

The upload page supports two modes:

| Mode | Best for |
|------|----------|
| **Single-beat upload** | Standalone track releases |
| **Create Collection** | Uploading multiple beats as a collection |

Enable the **Create Collection** toggle before adding files to build a collection. The toggle description reads: "Automatically create a collection when multiple files are selected."

## Supported Audio Formats

BeatPass only accepts **WAV** audio files.

| Accepted | Not accepted |
|----------|-------------|
| **WAV** | MP3, FLAC, AAC, OGG, and other formats |

If you upload an unsupported format, a dialog titled **Unsupported File Format** appears with an **Upload a Different File** button.

## Adding Files

You have two options:

- **Drag and drop** your files directly into the upload area
- Click **Choose Files to Upload** and select files from your computer

After a file is accepted, BeatPass opens the track form and begins audio fingerprinting in the background.

## Track Form Sections

The form is organized into five sections:

### Cover Art & Style

Upload cover art for your beat. You can use a custom image or select from **AI Cover Presets** matched to your genres. See [Cover Art](/help/uploading/cover-art) for full artwork guidelines.

### Track Details

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Track Name** | Yes | Your beat's title |
| **Producers** | Auto-filled | Automatically set to your producer profile |
| **Description** | No | Describe the beat's style, mood, or intended use |
| **Collection/Album** | No | Assign the beat to an existing or new collection |
| **Tags** | No | Add searchable tags (custom values allowed) |

### Music Properties

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Key** | Yes | Select from C through B, including sharps and flats |
| **Scale** | Yes | Major or Minor |
| **BPM** | Yes | Between 50 and 200 |

### Protection & Verification

Choose your beat's sample clearance status:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cleared" icon="shield">
    The beat is 100% original or all samples are fully cleared.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Needs Clearance" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    The beat contains samples that still require outside clearance.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

If you choose **Needs Clearance**, an additional notes field appears and buyers are warned accordingly.

This section also shows the audio fingerprinting status as the duplicate check runs.

### Licensing Options

This section is **collapsed by default** and automatically set to **Standard Licensing** (non-exclusive). You don't need to open it to save your beat.

If you want to offer exclusive licensing, expand the section and choose a model:

| Option | Description |
|--------|------------|
| **Standard Licensing** | Available to all BeatPass users (default) |
| **Standard + Exclusive** | Offer both standard licensing and exclusive purchase |
| **Exclusive Only** | Offer the beat only as an exclusive opportunity |

When exclusive licensing is enabled, additional fields appear:

- **Exclusive price** and **currency** (USD, EUR, GBP, or CAD)
- **Exclusive status** — Available, Sold, or Private
- **Exclusive contact email** (auto-filled with your email)

## Audio Fingerprint Status

During upload, BeatPass shows these fingerprint states:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Upload present but duplicate check hasn't started |
| **Checking** | Duplicate detection is starting |
| **Analyzing** | Audio fingerprint is being analyzed |
| **Verified Unique** | No duplicate found |
| **Check Failed** | Duplicate check failed, but you can still continue |

<Note>
  A failed duplicate check does not block your upload. BeatPass fails open in this situation — you can still save the track.
</Note>

If a matching beat is detected, a modal shows the original track details and similarity score.

## Saving Your Upload

When the required fields are complete, click to save your beat. For collection uploads:

- The collection itself needs its own cover art
- Each track still needs its own audio file

## Upload Minutes

Your plan determines how many minutes of audio you can upload. A notice at the top of the page shows your remaining minutes. If you run out, consider upgrading your plan for more upload capacity.

## Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Export as WAV before uploading" icon="file-audio">
    Convert your final masters to WAV before uploading. All other formats are blocked.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pick genres early" icon="music">
    Genres affect discovery and drive the AI Cover Preset artwork suggestions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Watch the fingerprint status" icon="fingerprint">
    A beat cannot be confirmed unique until the duplicate check finishes. If a duplicate is found, resolve it before saving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
