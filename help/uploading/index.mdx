This section covers the producer upload flow in BeatPass, including single-beat uploads, collections, metadata, cover art, sample-safe status, and audio fingerprinting.

<ProducerOnly />

## What You Can Do

As a BeatPass producer, you can:

- Upload individual beats
- Use the **Create Collection** toggle to build a collection while uploading multiple beats
- Add metadata, cover art, and tags before saving
- Replace audio files later if needed

<Info>
  BeatPass only accepts **WAV** audio files. MP3, FLAC, and other formats are not allowed.
</Info>

## Upload Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the upload page">
    Click **Upload beats** in the top navigation bar. The upload page heading reads **Upload Your Beats**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your audio file">
    Drag and drop your file or click **Choose Files to Upload**. BeatPass runs a duplicate check using audio fingerprinting after each file is selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the track form">
    Fill in the required track information across these sections:

    - **Cover Art & Style** — upload cover art for your beat
    - **Track Details** — track name, producers, description, collection, and tags
    - **Music Properties** — key, scale, and BPM (50–200)
    - **Protection & Verification** — sample-safe status (**Cleared** or **Needs Clearance**) and fingerprinting
    - **Licensing Options** — defaults to **Standard Licensing** (non-exclusive); expand to configure exclusive terms if needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your beat">
    Once the required fields are complete, save the track to publish it. Licensing does not need to be manually configured — it defaults to Standard (non-exclusive).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Required Fields

Before a new track can be saved, these fields must be filled in:

| Field | Section |
|-------|---------|
| **Track Name** | Track Details |
| **Key** | Music Properties |
| **Scale** | Music Properties |
| **BPM** (50–200) | Music Properties |

The producer is automatically assigned as the artist. Cover art, tags, description, and licensing configuration are optional but recommended.

<Tip>
  If you enable exclusive licensing, you'll also need to set an exclusive price, currency, and contact email.
</Tip>

## After Uploading

Once a beat is saved:

- It appears on your producer profile and in BeatPass search results
- It participates in licensing based on the model you selected (Standard by default)
- You can return later to edit metadata, cover art, licensing, or replace the audio file
- Fingerprint data and financial activity update over time
