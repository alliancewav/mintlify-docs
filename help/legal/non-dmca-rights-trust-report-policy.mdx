Use this process for rights and trust reports that are not DMCA copyright takedown notices.

<Note>
  Copyright takedown notices should use the Copyright & DMCA process. If your report includes both copyright and non-DMCA concerns, BeatPass may process each part under the applicable policy.
</Note>

## Covered Reports

This policy covers reports about:

- fully AI-generated beats or undisclosed AI use
- unverifiable AI samples, restricted AI output, or missing Proof of Rights
- trademark, publicity, likeness, voice, persona, impersonation, or false-association concerns
- sample-clearance, licensing, contract, metadata, or ownership disputes
- fraud, fake profiles, fake collaborations, misleading claims, payment abuse, or rights laundering
- harassment, spam, abusive messages, or platform-trust issues

## What to Include

Send the report through support and include:

- the beat title, producer name, profile, message, request, license, or URL involved
- the type of concern
- a clear explanation
- relevant screenshots, license records, proof, receipts, or correspondence
- your contact information for follow-up

Do not submit passwords, private credentials, unnecessary personal data, confidential records, or unredacted third-party data unless BeatPass specifically requests them.

## Possible Outcomes

BeatPass may request clarification, contact affected users, request Proof of Rights, pause downloads, delist content, restrict features, notify buyers, support refund or credit review, or take account action.
