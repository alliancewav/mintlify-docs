BeatPass uses cookies, local storage, pixels, analytics, and similar technologies to run the platform, secure accounts, remember preferences, support checkout, improve performance, and measure communications where permitted.

## Categories

| Category | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Strictly necessary** | Login, authentication, security, checkout, fraud prevention, account settings, and core platform operation |
| **Preferences** | Language, display, playback, accessibility, and saved settings |
| **Analytics and performance** | Usage, errors, traffic, feature adoption, and reliability |
| **Marketing and communications** | Campaign measurement, email preferences, and promotional communications where permitted |

## Controls

Where required, BeatPass will request consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies. Users can control cookies through browser settings, device settings, email unsubscribe tools, and any consent controls BeatPass provides.

Blocking cookies may affect login, checkout, playback, account settings, security, or other platform features.
