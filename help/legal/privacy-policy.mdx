This page provides a simplified overview of the BeatPass Privacy Policy. For the complete policy, visit the BeatPass Privacy Policy page.

<Warning>
  This summary is for convenience only. The official Privacy Policy governs how your data is handled.
</Warning>

## About BeatPass

BeatPass is operated by **Alliance Productions Records Inc.** This policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the BeatPass platform.

Privacy questions and complaints can be sent to the **BeatPass Privacy Officer** through [contact@beatpass.ca](mailto:contact@beatpass.ca).

## Information Collected

### Information You Provide

When you create an account or use BeatPass, the following information is collected:

- **Account details** — Name, email address, username, password
- **Transaction information** — Details when you subscribe or purchase licenses
- **Communications** — Messages you send to other users or to support
- **Beat request details** — Specifications for custom beat requests
- **AI disclosure and rights-verification information** — AI tool names, source notes, license records, receipts, screenshots, plan confirmations, generation records, stems, project files, written attestations, and other Proof of Rights when needed to enforce BeatPass policies

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use BeatPass, the following is automatically collected:

- **Device information** — IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage data** — Pages viewed, beats played, downloads, time spent
- **Cookies** — Small files that help remember your preferences

### Payment Information

When you make a purchase, the payment processor (Stripe) handles your payment details. **BeatPass does not store credit card numbers on its servers.**

## How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used to:

| Purpose | Examples |
|---------|----------|
| **Run the service** | Manage accounts, process transactions, track licensing |
| **Personalize your experience** | Recommend beats, remember preferences |
| **Improve BeatPass** | Analyze usage patterns, develop new features |
| **Process payments** | Handle subscriptions and purchases |
| **Communicate with you** | Send service updates, respond to support requests |
| **Prevent fraud** | Detect unauthorized access, protect accounts |
| **Verify rights and AI policy compliance** | Review AI disclosures, Proof of Rights, source records, and policy reports |

<Warning>
  When submitting Proof of Rights, do not provide passwords, private credentials, unnecessary third-party personal data, confidential records, or full unredacted AI-platform records unless BeatPass specifically asks for them.
</Warning>

BeatPass may use recommendations, personalization, fraud-prevention tools, and moderation signals to operate the platform. Final legal, payout, or account-termination decisions are not handled solely by automation where human review is reasonably required by law or platform policy.

## How Your Information Is Shared

**BeatPass does not sell or rent your personal information.**

Information may be shared with:

- **Service providers** — Companies that help run BeatPass (hosting, payments, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
- **Producers and consumers** — Transaction data shared as needed for licensing (e.g., producers see aggregate download data for royalty calculations)
- **Legal requirements** — When required by law or to respond to legal processes
- **Business transfers** — If BeatPass is acquired, your information may transfer to the new owner

Some service providers may process information outside Quebec or outside your country, including in Canada, the United States, or other jurisdictions where BeatPass or its providers operate.

## Your Messages and Requests

When you send messages through BeatPass or submit beat requests:

- Message content is shared with the recipient(s)
- Beat request details are shared with producers who may fulfill them
- Messages may be reviewed to enforce terms and prevent abuse

## Data Protection

BeatPass uses industry-standard security measures:

- Encryption of sensitive data
- Secure server environments
- Access controls limiting who can view your information

<Note>
  While BeatPass works hard to protect your data, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.
</Note>

BeatPass also maintains privacy governance practices for access, correction, deletion, retention, service-provider controls, incident handling, and privacy complaints.

## Age Requirements

BeatPass is not intended for users under 18. BeatPass does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact **contact@beatpass.ca**.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access the personal information BeatPass holds about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, contact the support team at **contact@beatpass.ca**.

Deletion requests may be limited where records are needed for active licenses, legal obligations, accounting, tax records, fraud prevention, rights verification, AI policy enforcement, disputes, or security.

## Cookies

BeatPass uses cookies to:

- Keep you logged in
- Remember your preferences (like dark mode)
- Understand how you use BeatPass
- Improve the service

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly without them.

See [Cookie and Tracking Policy](/help/legal/cookie-tracking-policy) for more detail about cookie categories and controls.

See [Marketing Communications and Consent](/help/legal/marketing-communications-consent) for commercial-message and unsubscribe rules.

See [Privacy Governance and Privacy Officer](/help/legal/privacy-governance) for the privacy officer contact, governance practices, cross-border processing, and incident handling summary.

## Changes to This Policy

The Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform or by email.

## Contact

If you have questions about BeatPass privacy practices:

- **Privacy Officer**: BeatPass Privacy Officer, Alliance Productions Records Inc.
- **Email**: contact@beatpass.ca
