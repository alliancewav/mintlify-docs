## Key Legal Points

### For Everyone

- BeatPass is operated by **Alliance Productions Records Inc.**, doing business as BeatPass
- By using BeatPass, you agree to the Terms of Service
- Your privacy matters — BeatPass does not sell your personal information
- You must be at least 18 years old to use BeatPass

### For Subscribers

- When you download a beat, you receive a **non-exclusive license** to use it
- Your license is **perpetual** — it doesn't expire, even if you cancel your subscription
- You must **credit the producer** in your releases
- You can use beats in one project per download
- You may not use BeatPass beats, stems, previews, or platform content for AI training, datasets, model development, sample packs, or competing libraries

### For Producers

- You **keep ownership** of all beats you upload
- You authorize BeatPass to operate the platform, issue licenses, generate certificates, verify rights, and distribute your beats under BeatPass terms
- Your beats remain yours even after consumers download them
- Your beats must be human-made, not fully AI-generated
- Any AI samples or AI-generated elements must be commercially licensable through BeatPass and backed by proof if requested

## AI Audio and Human-Made Content

BeatPass is for real producers and human-made work. AI may support your process, but it cannot replace your role as the producer.

- Fully AI-generated beats are not permitted.
- AI samples are allowed only when the final beat remains primarily human-made and the AI output is commercially licensable through BeatPass.
- Producers must be ready to explain how AI was used, to what extent, and what rights they hold.
- If the source or rights cannot be verified, the beat should not be uploaded or kept in a catalog.

See [AI Audio and Human-Made Content](/help/legal/ai-audio-human-made-content) for the full help guide.

## Copyright & DMCA

BeatPass respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe your copyrighted work has been used without permission:

- **File a claim**: See our [Copyright & DMCA guide](/help/legal/copyright-and-dmca) for step-by-step instructions and templates
- **Email**: [contact@beatpass.ca](mailto:contact@beatpass.ca?subject=DMCA%20Takedown%20Notice)
- **Phone**: 514-397-9912
- **Full legal policy**: [Copyright & DMCA](/help/legal/copyright-and-dmca)
