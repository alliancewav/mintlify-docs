## Key Legal Points

### For Everyone

- BeatPass is operated by **Alliance Productions Records Inc.**, doing business as BeatPass
- By using BeatPass, you agree to the Terms of Service
- Your privacy matters — BeatPass does not sell your personal information
- You must be at least 18 years old to use BeatPass

### For Subscribers

- When you download a beat, you receive a **non-exclusive license** to use it
- Your license is **perpetual** — it doesn't expire, even if you cancel your subscription
- You must **credit the producer** in your releases
- You can use beats in one project per download

### For Producers

- You **keep ownership** of all beats you upload
- BeatPass only receives a license to operate the platform and distribute your beats
- Your beats remain yours even after consumers download them

## Copyright & DMCA

BeatPass respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe your copyrighted work has been used without permission:

- **File a claim**: See our [Copyright & DMCA guide](/help/legal/copyright-and-dmca) for step-by-step instructions and templates
- **Email**: [contact@beatpass.ca](mailto:contact@beatpass.ca?subject=DMCA%20Takedown%20Notice)
- **Phone**: 514-397-9912
- **Full legal policy**: [beatpass.ca/pages/dmca](https://open.beatpass.ca/pages/dmca)

## Questions?

If you have questions about the legal terms or policies, contact the support team at **contact@beatpass.ca**.
