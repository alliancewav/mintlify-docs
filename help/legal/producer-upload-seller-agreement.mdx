This page summarizes the Producer Upload and Seller Agreement. It applies to approved producers who upload, list, license, sell, or monetize beats through BeatPass.

<Warning>
  This summary is for convenience only. The official agreement and Terms of Service control.
</Warning>

## Core Rule

Producers keep ownership of their beats, but authorize BeatPass to host, distribute, license, issue certificates for, verify, moderate, and support those beats under BeatPass terms.

## Producer Responsibilities

When uploading or listing beats, producers must confirm that:

- they own or control the rights needed to upload, sell, license, and authorize BeatPass to issue licenses
- the beat is human-made and not fully AI-generated
- samples, loops, vocals, artwork, names, likenesses, and source material are original, cleared, licensed, or otherwise usable
- AI-assisted elements and AI samples comply with the AI Audio Policy
- metadata, credits, sample-safe status, AI disclosures, and license settings are accurate
- Proof of Rights can be provided if BeatPass asks

## Catalog Reviews

BeatPass may request corrections, require disclosure updates, request Proof of Rights, pause downloads, delist beats, restrict uploads, notify affected buyers, or hold unpaid payouts when rights, AI, fraud, metadata, or policy concerns arise.

For non-urgent classifications or Proof of Rights decisions, producers may request review within 7 days after BeatPass sends notice.

## Off-Platform Conflicts

If a producer sells or grants exclusive rights outside BeatPass, they must remove or update the BeatPass listing before any conflicting BeatPass license is issued.
