## Ownership

### Producers Keep Their Rights

When you upload a beat to BeatPass:

- You **retain full ownership** of your beat
- Nothing transfers ownership to BeatPass or to consumers
- You grant BeatPass a license to operate the platform (host, display, distribute your beat)

### What Consumers Get

When you download a beat with a subscription:

- You receive a **non-exclusive, perpetual, worldwide license**
- You can use the beat in **one project**
- The license remains valid even after you cancel your subscription
- You **do not** gain ownership of the beat itself

## License Restrictions

Some uses require additional permission or an exclusive license:

- Television, film, or streaming service productions
- Commercials and advertising campaigns
- Video games
- Major brand campaigns
- Using one beat in multiple projects

## Producer Promotion

BeatPass may promote producers and their beats on its own channels (website and social media). This includes:

- Short clips or full audio from beats
- Cover art, titles, and producer names
- Links to producer profiles

Producers can opt out of promotional use at any time by emailing contact@beatpass.ca with the subject "Promo Opt-Out."

<Note>
  BeatPass will not use your beats in third-party paid advertisements without your written consent.
</Note>

## Content Rules

All content uploaded to BeatPass must:

- Be original work you have the right to upload
- Not infringe on anyone else's copyright
- Not contain illegal, harmful, or offensive material

## DMCA & Copyright

BeatPass respects intellectual property rights and follows the DMCA notice-and-takedown process.

### If Your Work Is Used Without Permission

Send a DMCA notice to **contact@beatpass.ca** including:

- Identification of the copyrighted work
- Location of the infringing material on BeatPass
- Your contact information
- A statement of good faith
- Your signature

### If Your Content Was Removed

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you can submit a counter-notice. See the full Terms of Service for details.

### Repeat Infringers

Accounts with repeated copyright violations may be suspended or terminated.

## Account Termination
