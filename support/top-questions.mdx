## Streaming & Playback

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a streaming limit?">
    **Free accounts**: Limited preview playback per day.  
    **Subscribers**: Unlimited streaming of the full catalog.
    
    See [Usage Limits](/support/usage-limits) for specifics.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why is audio quality different on some tracks?">
    Tracks stream at up to 256kbps AAC. Some older uploads may have lower source quality. Purchased downloads include full WAV quality.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I listen offline?">
    Not currently. Streaming requires an internet connection. Purchased beats can be downloaded for offline use.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Purchases & Receipts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find my receipt?">
    Go to **Account > Order History**. Click any order to view or download your receipt as PDF.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I download my stems?">
    After purchasing a license that includes stems:
    1. Go to **Library > Purchases**
    2. Find your beat
    3. Click **Download > Stems**
    
    Stems are only included with Premium and Exclusive licenses.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Digital licenses are generally non-refundable once files are accessed. Contact [support](/support/contact) if you received defective files or were charged incorrectly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Licensing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when an exclusive is sold?">
    The beat is removed from the marketplace. **Your existing license remains valid** for its full terms. You can continue using the beat as licensed—no new licenses will be sold.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need to credit the producer?">
    Most licenses require producer credit. Check your specific license terms for the exact format (e.g., "Prod. by [Name]").
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use beats on YouTube/Spotify?">
    Yes, most licenses allow streaming platforms. Check your license for:
    - Stream limits
    - Monetization rights
    - Content ID policies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reporting & Safety

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I report content?">
    Click the **three-dot menu** on any track or profile and select **Report**. Choose a reason:
    - Copyright infringement
    - Stolen content
    - Inappropriate material
    - Spam/fake account
    
    Reports are reviewed within 48 hours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Someone stole my beat">
    If your original content was uploaded by someone else:
    1. Report the track (see above)
    2. Email [support@beatpass.ca](mailto:support@beatpass.ca) with:
       - Link to the infringing track
       - Proof of ownership (original files, timestamps)
       - Your producer profile link
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I appeal a content strike?">
    If your content was removed or your account received a strike:
    1. Check your email for the notice with details
    2. Reply to the notice with your appeal
    3. Include any evidence (licenses, original files)
    
    Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Payments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why haven't I received my payout?">
    Common reasons:
    - **Stripe not connected** — Complete setup in Backstage > Finances
    - **Below minimum** — Earnings under $10 carry over
    - **Verification pending** — Check Stripe for required documents
    - **Monthly timing** — Contribution payouts process first week of each month
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I change my email or password?">
    Go to **Account > Settings > Security** to update credentials. Email changes require verification.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    Go to **Account > Settings > Delete Account**. This action is permanent and removes all your data, uploads, and purchase history.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
